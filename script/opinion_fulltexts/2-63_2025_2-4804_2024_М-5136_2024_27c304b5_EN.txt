According to the Charter of the municipal formation of the Donetsk city district of the Donetsk People's Republic, adopted by the decision of the Donetsk city council of the Donetsk People's Republic dated November 09, 2023 No. I/5-1, issues of local importance of the urban district include, among other things, the ownership, use and disposal of property in municipal ownership, the implementation of municipal housing control.
Clause 1.2 of the Decision of the Donetsk City Council of the Donetsk People's Republic dated November 30, 2023 No. I/8-3 “On issues of legal succession in the municipal formation of the Donetsk City District of the Donetsk People's Republic” determined that the successor to the property rights and obligations of the bodies that exercised on the territory of the Donetsk People's Republic on the day of the admission of the Donetsk People's Republic to the Russian Federation and the formation of a new one within the Russian Federation subject, the powers assigned by the legislation of the Russian Federation to the powers of local government bodies are the administration of the Donetsk urban district of the Donetsk People's Republic - in terms of the property necessary to ensure its activities, as well as other property that is in the municipal ownership of the municipal formation of the Donetsk urban district of the Donetsk People's Republic.
The procedure for identifying, recording and accepting into the municipal ownership of the municipality the Donetsk City District of the Donetsk People's Republic ownerless residential real estate is determined by the Procedure for identifying, accounting and accepting into the municipal ownership of the municipality the Donetsk City District of the Donetsk People's Republic ownerless residential real estate, approved by the decision of the Donetsk City Council of the Donetsk People's Republic dated 07/04/2024 No. I/32-5.
According to clause 2.1 of Section II of Procedure No. I/32-5, the city district administration, represented by the municipal property department, independently carries out actions to identify, record and accept into municipal ownership ownerless real estate located on the territory of the Donetsk urban district. Information about residential premises that have signs of ownerlessness may be received by the municipal property department from individuals and legal entities, including public authorities, who have become aware of such property.
Due to the fact that the administration of the urban district of Donetsk of the Donetsk People's Republic, in accordance with the regulations on the department of municipal property of the administration of the urban district of Donetsk, it is determined that the department of municipal property carries out work on the transfer of ownerless (escheated) real estate into the municipal property of the urban district of Donetsk, in connection with which, the court comes to the conclusion that the court with an application to recognize the residential premises as ownerless and to recognize the right of municipal property of the municipality to this residential premises, the authorized body applied.
According to Part 1 of Art. 130 of the Civil Code of the Russian Federation, immovable things (real estate, real estate) include land plots, subsoil plots and everything that is firmly connected to the land, that is, objects whose movement without disproportionate damage to their purpose is impossible, including buildings, structures, unfinished construction projects.
Real estate includes residential and non-residential premises, as well as parts of buildings or structures intended to accommodate vehicles (car spaces), if the boundaries of such premises, parts of buildings or structures are described in the manner established by the legislation on state cadastral registration.
Part 3 of Article 218 of the Civil Code of the Russian Federation provides that in cases and in the manner provided for by this Code, a person may acquire ownership of property that does not have an owner, of property whose owner is unknown, or of property that the owner has abandoned or to which he has lost ownership on other grounds provided by law.
According to Part 1 of Art. 225 of the Civil Code of the Russian Federation, an ownerless thing is a thing that does not have an owner or the owner of which is unknown or, unless otherwise provided by law, the right of ownership to which the owner has refused.
The procedure for considering applications for recognizing a movable thing as ownerless and recognizing the right of ownership of an ownerless immovable thing is regulated by the rules of Chapter 33 of the Civil Procedure Code of the Russian Federation.
According to clause 6, part 1, art. 262 of the Code of Civil Procedure of the Russian Federation, in a special proceeding, the court considers cases of recognizing a movable thing as ownerless and recognizing the right of municipal ownership of an ownerless immovable thing.
According to Part 2 of Art. 293 Chapter 33 of the Code of Civil Procedure of the Russian Federation, the court, having recognized that the immovable thing does not have an owner or the owner of the immovable thing is unknown and it has been registered in the prescribed manner, makes a decision to recognize the right of municipal ownership or the property of a federal city of Moscow, St. Petersburg or Sevastopol for this thing.
Article 2 of the Law of the Donetsk People's Republic of March 21, 2024 No. 66-RZ, establishes that the signs of ownerless property in relation to residential premises are: failure to pay for residential premises and utilities within one year before the day of identification of residential premises that have signs of ownerlessness; lack of information about the registered ownership of residential premises in the Unified State Register of Real Estate; non-use of residential premises, which, among other things, creates a threat to its safety (or an apartment building if the premises is an apartment, a room in an apartment building), safety for the life and health of other persons (including the rights holders of other premises in an apartment building if the premises is an apartment, a room in an apartment building).
Thus, the law provides for the recognition of the right of municipal ownership of ownerless property and there are necessary and sufficient conditions for recognizing an ownerless immovable property as an object of municipal property if it is confirmed that payment for residential premises and utilities has not been paid within one year before the date of identification of the residential premises; there is no information about the registered ownership of residential premises in the Unified State Register of Real Estate; non-use of residential premises, which, among other things, poses a threat to its safety (or an apartment building if the premises is an apartment, a room in an apartment building), safety for the life and health of other persons (including copyright holders of other premises in an apartment building if the premises are an apartment, a room in an apartment building) and the specified real estate object is duly registered as an ownerless real estate object by the Office of the Federal State Registration Service, cadastre and cartography for the Donetsk People's Republic.
Applying to the court with these demands, the administration of the city district of Donetsk of the Donetsk People's Republic presented evidence that the real estate was registered with the body that carries out state registration of the right to real estate as an ownerless property, and has been registered since July 29, 2024, as well as evidence of non-payment of payments for residential premises and utilities, lack of information about the registered ownership of the disputed residential premises in Unified State Register of Real Estate, non-use of residential premises, which meets the requirements of Article 10 of the Law of the Donetsk People's Republic of March 21, 2024 No. 66-RZ.
Since no one has declared their rights to them since the day the disputed object was registered, the administration of the Donetsk urban district of the Donetsk People's Republic has the right to apply to the court for recognition of the right of municipal ownership to it.
In addition, an application to recognize an ownerless immovable property as an object of municipal property was submitted by the body authorized to manage municipal property.
Taking into account the circumstances of the case, the court comes to the conclusion that the administration of the Donetsk urban district of the Donetsk People's Republic complied with the requirements of Article 10 of the Law of the Donetsk People's Republic of March 21, 2024 No. 66-RZ, and considers it possible to recognize the right of municipal ownership in the person of the administration of the Donetsk urban district of the Donetsk People's Republic to ownerless real estate - an apartment located at the address:
