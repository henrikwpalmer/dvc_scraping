The representative of the interested party - the Office of the Federal Service for State Registration, Cadastre and Cartography for the Donetsk People's Republic, did not appear at the court hearing; according to the petition, he asked to consider the case in his absence.
After hearing the stated petition, examining the case materials and the evidence provided in accordance with the requirements of Art. 67 of the Code of Civil Procedure of the Russian Federation, the court believes that the application of the administration of the Mariupol urban district should be left without consideration.
Thus, Chapter 33 of the Code of Civil Procedure of the Russian Federation regulates the procedure for considering cases of recognizing a movable thing as ownerless or recognizing ownership of an ownerless immovable thing.
According to paragraph 3 of Art. 218 of the Civil Code of the Russian Federation, in cases and in the manner provided for by this Code, a person may acquire the right of ownership to property that does not have an owner, to property whose owner is unknown, or to property that the owner has abandoned or to which he has lost the right of ownership on other grounds provided by law.
In accordance with paragraph 1 of Art. 225 of the Civil Code of the Russian Federation, ownerless is a thing that does not have an owner, the owner of which is unknown, or the right of ownership to which the owner has refused.
Based on the provisions of paragraph 3 of Art. 225 of the Civil Code of the Russian Federation, ownerless real estate is accepted for registration by the body that carries out state registration of rights to real estate, upon application from the local government body in whose territory they are located. After a year has passed from the date of registration of an ownerless immovable property, the body authorized to manage municipal property may apply to the court with a request to recognize the right of municipal ownership of this thing. An ownerless immovable property, not recognized by a court decision as having come into municipal ownership, may be again taken into possession, use and disposal by the owner who abandoned it, or acquired into ownership by virtue of acquisitive prescription.
According to Art. 130 of the Civil Code of the Russian Federation, immovable things (real estate, real estate) include land plots, subsoil plots and everything that is firmly connected to the land, that is, objects whose movement without disproportionate damage to their purpose is impossible, including buildings, structures, unfinished construction projects. Real estate includes residential and non-residential premises, as well as parts of buildings or structures intended to accommodate vehicles (car spaces), if the boundaries of such premises, parts of buildings or structures are described in the manner established by the legislation on state cadastral registration. Items not related to real estate, including money and securities, are recognized as movable property.
Recognition of ownership of an ownerless immovable property is carried out in a special proceeding upon the application of the body authorized to manage municipal property, in accordance with Art. Art. 290-293 Code of Civil Procedure of the Russian Federation.
According to clause 1.1 of Decree of the Acting Head of the Donetsk People's Republic No. 140 dated May 10, 2023, it is established that the body and officials of the Donetsk People's Republic exercising the powers classified by the legislation of the Russian Federation as the powers of local government bodies, until the completion of the formation of local government bodies, the local government body of the municipal formation in the Donetsk People's Republic, authorized to manage the municipal property, has the right to apply to the court with a demand for recognition of the right of municipal ownership of ownerless buildings, structures (except for linear objects), premises, objects of unfinished construction that do not have an owner or the owner of which is unknown or the ownership of which the owner has refused, located on the territory of such a municipality, after three months from the date of their registration as ownerless real estate by the body carrying out state registration of rights to real estate property, unless otherwise provided by this Decree. |
Thus, assessing the collected evidence according to the rules of Art. 67 of the Code of Civil Procedure of the Russian Federation in the aggregate, guided by the provisions of the Civil Code of the Russian Federation, Law No. 5-FKZ, and also guided by the Law of the Donetsk People's Republic of March 21, 2024 No. 66-RZ, Decree of the Head of the Donetsk People's Republic of April 28, 2021 No. 116, the court comes to the conclusion that the interest in the immovable property, the purpose of the non-residential premises not lost, the owner did not abandon the property, as stated at the court hearing, and therefore in this case the court sees a dispute about the right.
In accordance with the requirements of Part 3 of Art. 263 of the Code of Civil Procedure of the Russian Federation, if, when filing an application or considering a case in a special proceeding, it is established that there is a dispute about the right, the court issues a ruling to leave the application without consideration, in which it explains to the applicant and other interested parties their right to resolve the dispute in the manner of claim proceedings.
Thus, the court believes that there are all grounds for leaving the application of the administration of the city district of Mariupol, the interested parties - the Department of the Federal Service for State Registration, Cadastre and Cartography for the Donetsk People's Republic, for recognition of the right of municipal ownership of ownerless property without consideration, since there is a dispute about the right.
