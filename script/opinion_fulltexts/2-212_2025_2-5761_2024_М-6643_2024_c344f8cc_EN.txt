The Yenakievo Interdistrict Court, composed of: the presiding judge - Polyushkina N.D., with the secretary - Kharybina L.A., having considered in open court a civil case at the request of the administration of the urban district of Yenakievo of the Donetsk People's Republic, interested parties - the Office of the Federal Service for State Registration, Cadastre and Cartography for the Donetsk People's Republic on the recognition of the right of municipal ownership of an ownerless immovable thing,
INSTALLED:
The administration of the Yenakievo urban district of the Donetsk People's Republic applied to the court for recognition of the right of municipal ownership of an ownerless real estate property, asking to recognize for the municipal formation of the Yenakievo urban district of the Donetsk People's Republic the right of municipal ownership of an ownerless real estate object (apartment), located at the address:
<address>
, non-residential premises with an area of ​​233.29 sq.m. The requirements are motivated by the fact that on 06/04/2024 the Commission for the identification, registration and acceptance into municipal ownership of ownerless real estate and escheatable property in the administrative territory of the city of Yenakievo decided to recognize the above-mentioned property as ownerless, to organize a commission inspection of the thing that has signs of being ownerless with the drawing up of the corresponding act. On August 12, 2024, the property was registered as ownerless. In relation to the specified object in the Unified State Register there is no information about the rights to it.
The applicant's representative for the administration of the Yenakievo urban district of the Donetsk People's Republic did not appear at the court hearing, was notified in advance and properly about the time and place of the consideration of the case, submitted a petition to consider the case in his absence, and fully supports the stated requirements.
The representative of the interested party - the Office of the Federal Service for State Registration, Cadastre and Cartography for the Donetsk People's Republic did not appear at the court hearing, was duly notified of the time and place of the court hearing, did not inform about valid reasons for the absence, and did not ask to consider the case in his absence.
Having studied the arguments of the application and examined the written materials of the case in their entirety, the court comes to the following conclusions.
According to Part. 1 and 3 tbsp. 35 of the Constitution of the Russian Federation, the right to private property is protected by law; no one can be deprived of their property except by a court decision.
In accordance with Part 1 of Article 4 of the Federal Constitutional Law No. 5-FKZ of October 4, 2022 “On the admission of the Donetsk People’s Republic to the Russian Federation and the formation of a new subject within the Russian Federation - the Donetsk People’s Republic” (hereinafter referred to as Law No. 5-FKZ of October 4, 2022), legislative and other regulatory legal acts of the Russian Federation are valid on the territory of the Donetsk People’s Republic Republic from the date of admission of the Donetsk People's Republic to the Russian Federation and the formation of a new subject within the Russian Federation, unless otherwise provided by this Federal Constitutional Law.
According to Part 2 of Art. 4 of Law No. 5-FKZ, the regulatory legal acts of the Donetsk People's Republic are valid on the territory of the Donetsk People's Republic until the end of the transition period or until the adoption of the relevant regulatory legal act of the Russian Federation and (or) the regulatory legal act of the Donetsk People's Republic.
At the same time, Art. 21 of Law No. 5-FKZ establishes the specifics of regulating relations in the sphere of property turnover on the territory of the Donetsk People's Republic.
In accordance with clause 2, part 1 of Art. 21 of Law No. 5-FKZ, until January 1, 2028, on the territory of the Donetsk People's Republic, regulatory legal acts of the Donetsk People's Republic may establish features of the regulation of property relations, including the circulation of land, in agreement with the federal executive body performing the functions of managing state property.
By virtue of Part 7 of Article 12 of Law No. 5-FKZ, the procedure for recognizing property that is not classified as federal property, the property of a constituent entity of the Russian Federation or municipal property as ownerless, established by the regulatory legal acts of the Donetsk People's Republic, is applied until January 1, 2028 in agreement with the federal executive body performing the functions of managing state property.
In pursuance of the above federal constitutional law, on May 10, 2023, the Acting Head of the Donetsk People's Republic signed Decree No. 140 “On the specifics of acquiring the right of state and municipal ownership of ownerless real estate on the territory of the Donetsk People’s Republic”, according to which the executive body of the Donetsk People’s Republic authorized to manage state property has the right to go to court with a demand for recognition of the right of state ownership of ownerless buildings according to within three months from the date of their registration as ownerless real estate by the body carrying out state registration of rights to real estate. The DPR State Property Fund is designated as the executive body of the Donetsk People's Republic authorized to manage state property (clause 1.2 of the Decree).
By Decree of the Acting Head of the Donetsk People's Republic signed on February 27, 2023 No. 64 On some issues related to the management of property that has signs of being ownerless, located on the territory of the Donetsk People's Republic, and the recognition of such property as ownerless (hereinafter referred to as Decree No. 64) The Government of the Donetsk People's Republic is vested with the authority to establish (adopt) a procedure for recognizing property that has signs of being ownerless ownerless and not classified as federal property, property of the Russian Federation or municipal property located on the territory of the Donetsk People's Republic, with the exception of real estate in the form of residential premises, residential buildings, and outbuildings.
As part of the implementation of Decree No. 140, as well as Decree No. 64, by Decree of the Government of the Donetsk People's Republic of June 22, 2023 No. 45-5, the executive body of the Donetsk People's Republic authorized to manage state real estate established the State Property Fund of the Donetsk People's Republic and approved the Procedure for recognizing real estate located on the territory of the Donetsk People's Republic as ownerless, with the exception of residential buildings, residential premises, garden houses and outbuildings intended to satisfy citizens' household and other needs (hereinafter referred to as the Procedure), which regulates legal relations and establishes the procedure for recognizing property on the territory of the Donetsk People's Republic as ownerless, as well as recognizing the right of state ownership of the Donetsk People's Republic to ownerless real estate.
Clause 7 of the Procedure establishes that no later than the next working day after receiving an application for the identification of an ownerless immovable property from the body that identified the object, the DPR SPFU sends to the executive body of the Donetsk People's Republic implementing state policy in the field of mass communications information about the identified ownerless immovable property, with the aim of posting this information on the official website of the Donetsk People's Republic in the information and telecommunications network "Internet" to identify and inform property owners.

According to paragraphs. 1 and 3 tbsp. 225 of the Civil Code of the Russian Federation, ownerless is a thing that does not have an owner or the owner of which is unknown or, unless otherwise provided by law, the owner of which has renounced the right of ownership. If this is not excluded by the rules of this Code on the acquisition of ownership of things that the owner has abandoned (Article 226), on finds (Articles 227 and 228), on stray animals (Articles 230 and 231) and treasure (Article 233), the right of ownership of ownerless movable things can be acquired by virtue of acquisitive prescription.
By virtue of the provisions of Art. 236 of the Civil Code of the Russian Federation, a citizen or legal entity may renounce the right of ownership of property belonging to him by declaring this or taking other actions that definitely indicate his removal from ownership, use and disposal of property without the intention of retaining any rights to this property.
From the content of these norms it follows that the current legislation provides for the possibility of terminating the right of ownership of a particular property by the owner performing actions indicating his renunciation of his property right.
At the same time, actions indicating the owner’s renunciation of the right of ownership may include, among other things, the removal of the owner from the possession and use of property belonging to him, and failure to take measures to maintain this property.
