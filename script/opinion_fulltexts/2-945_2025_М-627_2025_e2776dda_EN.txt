In support of the stated requirements, it is stated that the object has signs of an ownerless immovable property, namely, there is no information about the owner, during the search the owner was not identified, the specified object does not belong to federal property, the necessary procedure for recognizing the object as ownerless in accordance with Decrees of the Acting Head of the Donetsk People's Republic dated 05.10.2023 No. 140, dated 02.27.2023 No. 64, Decree of the Government of the Donetsk People's Republic of June 22, 2023 No. 45-5, articles 225, 218 of the Civil Code of the Russian Federation.
Taking into account the above, the applicant applied to the court to declare the object ownerless and to recognize the ownership of the object.
The applicant’s representative did not appear at the court hearing; the case file contains a request to consider the civil case without his participation and satisfy the stated requirements in full.
The interested parties did not appear at the court hearing, the Ministry of Industry and Trade of the DPR asked to consider the case in the absence of a representative, the stated demands were supported.
Taking into account the provisions of Article 167 of the Code of Civil Procedure of the Russian Federation, the court considers it possible to consider a civil case in the absence of the parties who failed to appear.
Having examined the case materials, the court believes that the stated requirements must be satisfied on the following grounds.
According to paragraph 3 of Art. 218 of the Civil Code of the Russian Federation, in cases and in the manner provided for by this Code, a person may acquire ownership of property that does not have an owner, property whose owner is unknown, or property that the owner has abandoned or to which he has lost ownership on other grounds provided by law.
In accordance with paragraph 1 of Art. 225 of the Civil Code of the Russian Federation, ownerless is a thing that does not have an owner, the owner of which is unknown, or the right of ownership to which the owner has refused.
In accordance with Part 1 of Art. 4 of the Federal Constitutional Law of October 4, 2022 No. 5-FKZ “On the admission of the Donetsk People's Republic into the Russian Federation and the formation of a new subject within the Russian Federation - the Donetsk People's Republic” (hereinafter referred to as Law No. 5-FKZ) legislative and other regulatory legal acts of the Russian Federation are valid on the territory of the Donetsk People's Republic from the date of admission to the Russian Federation of the Donetsk People's Republic and the formation of of the Russian Federation of a new subject, unless otherwise provided by Law No. 5-FKZ.
According to Part 2 of Art. 4 of Law No. 5-FKZ, the regulatory legal acts of the Donetsk People's Republic are valid on the territory of the Donetsk People's Republic until the end of the transition period or until the adoption of the relevant regulatory legal act of the Russian Federation and (or) the regulatory legal act of the Donetsk People's Republic.
At the same time, Art. 21 of Law No. 5-FKZ establishes the specifics of regulating relations in the sphere of real estate turnover on the territory of the Donetsk People's Republic.
In accordance with clause 2, part 1, art. 21 of Law No. 5-FKZ, until January 01, 2028, on the territory of the Donetsk People's Republic, regulatory legal acts of the Donetsk People's Republic may establish features of the regulation of property relations, including the circulation of land, in agreement with the federal executive body performing the functions of managing state property.
By virtue of Part 7 of Art. 12 of Law No. 5-FKZ, the procedure for recognizing property that is not classified as federal property, the property of a constituent entity of the Russian Federation or municipal property as ownerless, established by the regulatory legal acts of the Donetsk People's Republic, is applied until January 1, 2028 in agreement with the federal executive body performing the functions of managing state property.
Decree of the Head of the Donetsk People's Republic dated May 10, 2023 No. 140 “On the specifics of acquiring the right of state and municipal ownership of ownerless real estate on the territory of the Donetsk People’s Republic” established the specifics of acquiring ownership in the territory of the Donetsk People’s Republic
By virtue of clause 1.2 of Decree No. 140, the executive body of the Donetsk People's Republic, authorized to manage state property, has the right to apply to the court with a demand for recognition of the right of state ownership of the objects defined in subclause 1.1 of this clause (with the exception of residential premises, outbuildings intended to satisfy citizens' household and other needs), after three months from the date of their registration as ownerless real estate the body carrying out state registration of rights to real estate.
By virtue of clause 1 of the Decree of the Head of the Donetsk People's Republic dated 02.27.2023 No. 64 “On some issues related to the management of property that has signs of ownerlessness, located on the territory of the Donetsk People’s Republic, and the recognition of such property as ownerless,” the Government of the Donetsk People’s Republic is vested with the authority to manage property that has signs of ownerlessness and is not classified as federal property, property Donetsk People's Republic or municipal property located on the territory of the Donetsk People's Republic, in accordance with the Rules for the management of bodies of the Donetsk People's Republic, Lugansk People's Republic, Zaporozhye region and Kherson region, having signs of ownerlessness and not classified as federal property, property of a constituent entity of the Russian Federation or municipal property, property located on the territories of these constituent entities of the Russian Federation, approved by the Decree of the Government of the Russian Federation dated 28 December 2022 N 2474 (hereinafter referred to as the Rules), with the exception of real estate in the form of residential premises, garden houses, outbuildings intended to satisfy citizens' household and other needs.
Decree of the Government of the Donetsk People's Republic dated June 22, 2023 No. 45-5 “On recognizing real estate located on the territory of the Donetsk People's Republic as ownerless, with the exception of residential buildings, residential premises, garden houses, outbuildings intended to satisfy citizens' household and other needs” approved the procedure for recognizing real estate located on the territory of the Donetsk People's Republic as ownerless, with the exception of residential buildings, residential premises, garden houses and outbuildings intended to satisfy citizens' household and other needs, the procedure for including ownerless linear facilities (including their integral technological parts) intended for electricity, heat and gas supply.
