INSTALLED:
The Novoazovsky District Court of the Donetsk People's Republic is processing a civil case at the request of the Administration of the Novoazovsky Municipal District of the Donetsk People's Republic, the interested party: the Office of the Federal Service for State Registration, Cadastre and Cartography for the Donetsk People's Republic, on recognition of the right of municipal ownership of ownerless property.
Before the start of the court hearing, a statement was received from the applicant's representative to refuse the application. Did not appear at the court hearing, duly notified.
The interested parties did not appear at the court hearing, were duly notified of the day, time and place of the hearing of the case, the court was not informed of valid reasons for failure to appear, and they did not submit any objections to the termination of the proceedings due to the refusal of the application.
In accordance with Part 1 of Art. 39 of the Civil Procedure Code of the Russian Federation, the plaintiff has the right to abandon the claim. The court accepts the plaintiff’s refusal of the claim if it is contrary to the law or violates the rights and legitimate interests of other persons.
Having examined the case materials and examined the application, the court believes that there are no grounds for refusing to satisfy the application to terminate the proceedings. The consequences of refusing the claim to the applicant are explained and understandable.
In accordance with Art. 220 of the Code of Civil Procedure of the Russian Federation, the court terminates the proceedings if the plaintiff renounces the claim and the refusal is accepted by the court.
Based on the above, guided by Art. Art. 39, 220, 221 Code of Civil Procedure of the Russian Federation court
DEFINED:
Accept the applicant's refusal at the request of the Administration of the Novoazovsky Municipal District of the Donetsk People's Republic, interested party: Department of the Federal Service for State Registration, Cadastre and Cartography for the Donetsk People's Republic, on recognition of the right of municipal ownership of ownerless property.
Terminate the proceedings in the civil case at the request of the Administration of the Novoazovsky Municipal District of the Donetsk People's Republic, the interested party: the Office of the Federal Service for State Registration, Cadastre and Cartography for the Donetsk People's Republic, on recognition of the right of municipal ownership of ownerless property.
Repeated appeal to the court in a dispute between the same parties, about the same subject and on the same grounds is not allowed in accordance with Art. 221 of the Civil Procedure Code of the Russian Federation.
A private complaint against the ruling may be filed with the Supreme Court of the Donetsk People's Republic through the Novoazovsky District Court of the Donetsk People's Republic within fifteen days from the date of the ruling.
Judge A.V. Popeka
