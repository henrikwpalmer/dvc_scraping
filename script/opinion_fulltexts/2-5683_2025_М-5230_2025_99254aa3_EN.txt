The court, having examined the written evidence in the case, analyzing them both individually and in aggregate, evaluating the presented evidence according to its internal conviction, based on a comprehensive study of the evidence, comes to the following conclusions.
Paragraph one of 225 of the Civil Code of the Russian Federation defines that an ownerless thing is a thing that does not have an owner or the owner of which is unknown or, unless otherwise provided by law, the right of ownership to which the owner has refused.
Paragraph 3 of Article 218 of the Civil Code of the Russian Federation states that in cases and in the manner provided for by the Civil Code of the Russian Federation, a person can acquire ownership of property that does not have an owner, property whose owner is unknown, or property that the owner has refused or to which he has lost ownership on other grounds provided by law.
Part 2 of Article 290 of the Code of Civil Procedure of the Russian Federation states that an application for recognition of ownership of an ownerless immovable property is submitted to the court at its location by the body authorized to manage municipal property or property owned by the federal city of Moscow, St. Petersburg or Sevastopol, or by a person obliged in accordance with the law to operate linear facilities.
According to the first and third parts of Article 1 of the Federal Constitutional Law of the Russian Federation dated October 4, 2022 No. 5-FKZ “On the admission of the Donetsk People's Republic into the Russian Federation and the formation of a new subject within the Russian Federation - the Donetsk People's Republic (hereinafter referred to as the Federal Constitutional Law of October 4, 2022 No. 5-FKZ), the Donetsk People's Republic is admitted to the Russian Federation in accordance with the Constitution of the Russian Federation and Article 4 of the Federal Constitutional Law of December 17, 2001 No. 6-FKZ “On the procedure for admission to the Russian Federation and the formation of a new subject of the Russian Federation within it.” The Donetsk People's Republic is considered accepted into the Russian Federation from the date of signing the Agreement between the Russian Federation and the Donetsk People's Republic on the admission of the Donetsk People's Republic to the Russian Federation and the formation of a new entity within the Russian Federation.
Article one of the Federal Law of the Russian Federation “On the ratification of the Treaty between the Russian Federation and the Donetsk People’s Republic and the admission of the Donetsk People’s Republic into the Russian Federation and the formation of a new subject within the Russian Federation” dated October 4, 2022 No. 372-FZ provides that the Treaty between the Russian Federation and the Donetsk People’s Republic and the admission of the Donetsk People’s Republic into the Russian Federation has been ratified Republic and the formation of a new entity within the Russian Federation, signed on September 30, 2022, October 4, 2022. And article two of the named law shows that Federal Law No. 372-FZ of October 4, 2022 comes into force from the moment of official publication. Federal Law No. 372-FZ of October 4, 2022 was published on October 5, 2022.
Part seven of Article 12 of the Federal Law No. 5-FKZ dated October 4, 2022, establishes that the management of property located on the territory of the Donetsk People's Republic, which has signs of ownerlessness and is not classified as federal property, the property of a constituent entity of the Russian Federation or municipal property, is carried out by the authorities of the Donetsk People's Republic in the manner established by the Government of the Russian Federation, unless otherwise provided by this Federal Constitutional Law. The procedure for recognizing such property as ownerless, established by the regulatory legal acts of the Donetsk People's Republic, is applied until January 1, 2028, in agreement with the federal executive body exercising the functions of managing state property.
Clause 1.1. Acting Decree of the Head of the Donetsk People's Republic No. 64 of February 27, 2023 "On some issues related to the management of property that has signs of ownerlessness, located on the territory of the Donetsk People's Republic, and the recognition of such property as ownerless" determined that the Government of the Donetsk People's Republic is vested with the authority to manage property that has signs of ownerlessness and is not classified as federal property, property Donetsk People's Republic or municipal property located on the territory of the Donetsk People's Republic, in accordance with the Rules for the management of bodies of the Donetsk People's Republic, Lugansk People's Republic, Zaporozhye region and Kherson region, having signs of ownerlessness and not classified as federal property, property of a constituent entity of the Russian Federation or municipal property, property located on the territories of these constituent entities of the Russian Federation, approved by the Decree of the Government of the Russian Federation dated 28 December 2022 No. 2474, with the exception of real estate in the form of residential premises, garden houses, outbuildings intended to satisfy citizens' household and other needs.
Clause 1.1. Decree of the Government of the Donetsk People's Republic No. 45-5 of June 22, 2023 "On the recognition of real estate located on the territory of the Donetsk People's Republic as ownerless, with the exception of residential buildings, residential premises, garden houses, outbuildings intended to satisfy citizens' household and other needs" determined that the executive body of the Donetsk People's Republic authorized to manage state real estate is the Ministry of Property and Land Relations of the Donetsk People's Republic.
In view of the fact that, in accordance with the requirements stated by the Ministry of Property and Land Relations of the Donetsk People's Republic, this application requests recognition of the right of state ownership of the Donetsk People's Republic to an ownerless immovable property that is not residential real estate, in connection with which the court comes to the conclusion that this application for recognition of the right of ownership was submitted by a body authorized by current legislation.
Clause 2.2. Resolution of the Government of the Donetsk People's Republic No. 45-5 of June 22, 2023 “On the recognition of real estate located on the territory of the Donetsk People's Republic as ownerless, with the exception of residential buildings, residential premises, garden houses, outbuildings intended to satisfy citizens' household and other needs” approved the procedure for recognizing real estate located on the territory of the Donetsk People's Republic as ownerless, with the exception of residential buildings, residential premises, garden houses and outbuildings intended to satisfy citizens' household and other needs.
Thus, paragraphs five and six of the Procedure for recognizing ownerless real estate located on the territory of the Donetsk People’s Republic, with the exception of residential buildings, residential premises, garden houses and outbuildings intended to satisfy citizens’ household and other needs (hereinafter referred to as the Procedure), approved by Decree of the Government of the Donetsk People’s Republic dated June 22, 2023 No. 45-5, it is determined that upon identification ownerless immovable property, the body that identified the object sends an application to the Ministry of Property and Land Relations of the Donetsk People's Republic to identify the ownerless immovable property. The application for the identification of an ownerless immovable property shall indicate the cadastral number (if any), the address of the object (if there is no address, its location), its name (purpose), as well as other data characterizing the object and its condition, including the area of ​​the object, or full-scale measurements. The application shall be accompanied by photographic materials, inspection reports, and other documents confirming the circumstances stated in the application.
