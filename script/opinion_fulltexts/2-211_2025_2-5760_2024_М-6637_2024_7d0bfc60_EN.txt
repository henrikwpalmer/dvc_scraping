<address>
, n.p. with an area of ​​117.4 sq.m., does not show signs of an ownerless thing, the consequences of refusing the claim are known.
In accordance with Art. 220 of the Code of Civil Procedure of the Russian Federation, the court terminates the proceedings if the plaintiff has abandoned the claim and the refusal is accepted by the court.
In such circumstances, the court accepts the applicant’s refusal to submit the application, and therefore the proceedings are subject to termination.
According to Art. 221 of the Code of Civil Procedure of the Russian Federation, the proceedings in the case are terminated by a court ruling, which states that a repeated appeal to the court in a dispute between the same parties, about the same subject and on the same grounds is not allowed, with the exception of the termination of proceedings in a case on the protection of the rights and legitimate interests of a group of persons in the manner established by part seven of Article 244.24 of this Code.
