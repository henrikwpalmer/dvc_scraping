According to paragraph 6 of Art. 262 of the Code of Civil Procedure of the Russian Federation, by way of special proceedings, the court considers cases of recognizing a movable thing as ownerless and recognizing the right of municipal ownership of an ownerless immovable thing. This procedure is regulated by Chapter 33 of the Code of Civil Procedure of the Russian Federation.
In accordance with Art. 225 of the Civil Code of the Russian Federation, an ownerless thing is a thing that does not have an owner, or the owner is unknown, or a thing for which the owner has renounced the right of ownership.
In accordance with paragraph 3 of Art. 225 of the Civil Code of the Russian Federation, ownerless real estate is accepted for registration by the body that carries out state registration of rights to real estate, upon application from the local government body in whose territory they are located.

Part 2 of Article 290 of the Code of Civil Procedure of the Russian Federation provides that an application for recognition of ownership of an ownerless immovable property is submitted to the court at its location by the body authorized to manage municipal property or property owned by the federal city of Moscow, St. Petersburg or Sevastopol.
According to Article 4 of the Federal Constitutional Law of the Russian Federation of October 4, 2022 No. 5-FKZ, “On the admission into the Russian Federation of the Donetsk People's Republic and the formation within the Russian Federation of a new subject - the Donetsk People's Republic” (hereinafter referred to as Law No. 5-FKZ), legislative and other regulatory legal acts of the Russian Federation are valid on the territory of the Donetsk People's Republic from the date of admission of the Donetsk People's Republic to the Russian Federation of the People's Republic and the formation of a new subject within the Russian Federation, unless otherwise provided by this Federal Constitutional Law.
Regulatory legal acts of the Donetsk People's Republic are valid on the territory of the Donetsk People's Republic until the end of the transition period or until the adoption of the corresponding regulatory legal act of the Russian Federation and (or) the regulatory legal act of the Donetsk People's Republic.
According to Part 7 of Article 12 of Law No. 5-FKZ, management of property located on the territory of the Donetsk People's Republic, which has signs of ownerlessness and is not classified as federal property, the property of a constituent entity of the Russian Federation or municipal property, is carried out by the authorities of the Donetsk People's Republic in the manner established by the Government of the Russian Federation, unless otherwise provided by this Federal Constitutional Law. The procedure for recognizing such property as ownerless, established by the regulatory legal acts of the Donetsk People's Republic, is applied until January 1, 2028, in agreement with the federal executive body exercising the functions of managing state property.
According to Art. 2 of the Law of the DPR “On the specifics of identifying, using and recognizing the right of municipal property of municipalities of the DPR to residential premises that have signs of ownerless property located on the territory of the DPR” (hereinafter referred to as the Law), adopted by the Resolution of the People's Council on March 21, 2024, the signs of ownerless property in relation to residential premises are: 1. Failure to pay for residential premises and utilities within one year before the day the residential premises are identified, having signs of being ownerless; 2. lack of information about the registered ownership of residential premises in the Unified State Register of Real Estate; 3. non-use of residential premises, which, among other things, creates a threat to its safety (or an apartment building if the premises is an apartment, a room in an apartment building), safety for the life and health of other persons (including the rights holders of other premises in an apartment building if the premises is an apartment, a room in an apartment building).
According to Art. 3 of the Law, local government bodies of the Donetsk People's Republic, represented by the bodies authorized by them (hereinafter referred to as the authorized bodies), independently carry out actions to identify, record and accept residential premises that have signs of ownerless property, and the municipal property of the municipalities of the Donetsk People's Republic, on the territory of which they are located, on the grounds provided by law.
