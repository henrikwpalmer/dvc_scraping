In support of the application, it is stated that, according to paragraph 1 of Article 225 of the Civil Code of the Russian Federation, an ownerless thing is a thing that does not have an owner or the owner of which is unknown or, unless otherwise provided by law, the right of ownership to which the owner has refused.
Part 7 of Article 12 of the Federal Constitutional Law of October 4, 2022 No. 5-FKZ “On the admission of the Donetsk People's Republic into the Russian Federation and the formation of a new subject within the Russian Federation - the Donetsk People's Republic” provides that the management of property located on the territory of the Donetsk People's Republic, which has signs of ownerlessness and is not classified as federal property, the property of a subject of the Russian Federation or municipal property, is carried out bodies of the Donetsk People's Republic in the manner established by the Government of the Russian Federation, unless otherwise provided by this Federal Constitutional Law. The procedure for recognizing such property as ownerless, established by the regulatory legal acts of the Donetsk People's Republic, is applied until January 1, 2028, in agreement with the federal executive body exercising the functions of managing state property.
Paragraph 3 of Article 218 of the Civil Code of the Russian Federation establishes that in cases and in the manner provided for by this Code, a person may acquire the right of ownership to property that does not have an owner, to property whose owner is unknown, or to property that the owner has abandoned or to which he has lost the right of ownership on other grounds provided by law.
According to Article 4 of the Federal Constitutional Law of the Russian Federation of October 4, 2022 No. 5-FKZ, “On the admission into the Russian Federation of the Donetsk People's Republic and the formation within the Russian Federation of a new subject - the Donetsk People's Republic” (hereinafter referred to as Law No. 5-FKZ), legislative and other regulatory legal acts of the Russian Federation are valid on the territory of the Donetsk People's Republic from the date of admission of the Donetsk People's Republic to the Russian Federation of the People's Republic and the formation of a new subject within the Russian Federation, unless otherwise provided by this Federal Constitutional Law.
Regulatory legal acts of the Donetsk People's Republic are valid on the territory of the Donetsk People's Republic until the end of the transition period or until the adoption of the corresponding regulatory legal act of the Russian Federation and (or) the regulatory legal act of the Donetsk People's Republic.
According to Part 7 of Article 12 of Law No. 5-FKZ, management of property located on the territory of the Donetsk People's Republic, which has signs of ownerlessness and is not classified as federal property, the property of a constituent entity of the Russian Federation or municipal property, is carried out by the authorities of the Donetsk People's Republic in the manner established by the Government of the Russian Federation, unless otherwise provided by this Federal Constitutional Law. The procedure for recognizing such property as ownerless, established by the regulatory legal acts of the Donetsk People's Republic, is applied until January 1, 2028, in agreement with the federal executive body exercising the functions of managing state property.
Decree of the Acting Head of the Donetsk People's Republic dated 05/10/2023 No. 140 (hereinafter referred to as Decree No. 140 dated 05/10/2023) established the specifics of acquiring state and municipal property rights to ownerless real estate on the territory of the Donetsk People's Republic.
According to clause 1.2 of Decree No. 140 dated 10.05.2023, the executive body of the Donetsk People's Republic authorized to manage state property has the right to apply to the court with a demand for recognition of the right of state ownership of the objects defined in subclause 1.1 of this clause (with the exception of residential premises, outbuildings intended to satisfy citizens' household and other needs), after three months from the date of their registration as ownerless real estate by the body carrying out state registration of rights to real estate.
By Decree of the Acting Head of the Donetsk People's Republic dated 02.27.2023 No. 64 “On some issues related to the management of property that has signs of ownerlessness, located on the territory of the Donetsk People's Republic, and the recognition of such property as ownerless” (hereinafter referred to as Decree dated 02.27.2023 No. 64), the Government of the Donetsk People's Republic is vested with the authority to establish (adoption) of the procedure for recognizing as ownerless property that has signs of ownerlessness and is not classified as federal property, the property of a constituent entity of the Russian Federation or municipal property located on the territory of the Donetsk People's Republic, with the exception of real estate objects in the form of residential premises, residential buildings, and outbuildings.
In accordance with Decrees dated 10.05.2023 No. 140, dated 27.02.2023 No. 64, Decree of the Government of the Donetsk People's Republic dated 22.06.2023 No. 45-5, the executive body of the Donetsk People's Republic authorized to manage state real estate determined the State Property Fund of the Donetsk People's Republic and approved the Procedure for recognition located on the territory of the Donetsk People's Republic of real estate ownerless, with the exception of residential buildings, residential premises, garden houses and outbuildings intended to satisfy citizens' household and other needs (hereinafter referred to as the Procedure), which regulates legal relations and establishes the procedure for recognizing property as ownerless on the territory of the Donetsk People's Republic, as well as recognizing the right of state ownership of the Donetsk People's Republic to ownerless real estate.
By Decree of the Head of the Donetsk People's Republic of December 13, 2024 No. 695 (hereinafter referred to as Decree of December 13, 2024 No. 695) “On the formation (establishment) of the Ministry of Property and Land Relations of the Donetsk People's Republic,” the Ministry of Property and Land Relations of the Donetsk People's Republic (hereinafter referred to as MIZO DPR) was established and the Regulations on the Ministry of Property and land relations of the Donetsk People's Republic (hereinafter referred to as the Regulations).
In accordance with subclause 3.1.51 of clause 3.1 of the Regulations, the MIZO of the DPR is empowered to apply to the court with demands to recognize an immovable property as ownerless and to recognize the right of state ownership of the Donetsk People's Republic to ownerless immovable things (with the exception of linear objects).
According to subclause 5.1.5. clause 5.1. Regulations, MIZO DPR is the subject of legal relations in terms of powers previously exercised by the State Property Fund of the Donetsk People's Republic within the framework of the implementation of the Decree of the Government of the Donetsk People's Republic of June 22, 2023 No. 45-5 “On the recognition of real estate located on the territory of the Donetsk People's Republic as ownerless, with the exception of residential buildings, residential premises, garden houses and outbuildings, intended to satisfy citizens’ household and other needs.”
Clause 7 of the Procedure establishes that no later than the next working day after receiving an application for the identification of an ownerless immovable property from the body that identified the object, the DPR SPFU sends to the executive body of the Donetsk People's Republic implementing state policy in the field of mass communications information about the identified ownerless immovable property, with the aim of posting this information on the official website of the Donetsk People's Republic in the information and telecommunications network "Internet" to identify and inform property owners.
