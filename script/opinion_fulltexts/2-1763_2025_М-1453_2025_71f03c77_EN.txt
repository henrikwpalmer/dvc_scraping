INSTALLED:
City administration Mariupol DPR filed a petition with the court to the Office of Rosreestr for the DPR to recognize the right of municipal ownership of an ownerless real estate property. In support of the stated requirements, she indicated that as a result of the work carried out in accordance with the procedure established by law to identify ownerless property, an object of real estate was identified that had signs of ownerlessness. She indicated that the specified object was included in the corresponding register of ownerless real estate and it was registered in the cadastral register as ownerless property. She indicated that the three-month period for the above-mentioned object to be registered as ownerless has now expired, and therefore there are grounds provided for by law for its conversion into the ownership of the municipality.
