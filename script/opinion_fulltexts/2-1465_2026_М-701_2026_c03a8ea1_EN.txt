At the court hearing, the applicant, the Ministry of Property and Land Relations of the Donetsk People's Republic, the interested parties, the Office of the Federal Service for State Registration, Cadastre and Cartography for the Donetsk People's Republic and the Ministry of Internal Affairs for the Donetsk People's Republic, having been duly notified of the time and place of the consideration of the case, did not send representatives, submitted applications for consideration of the case without the participation of their representatives.
Having examined the case materials and assessed the evidence presented in its entirety, the court comes to the conclusion that the stated requirements have been satisfied for the following reasons.
According to parts 1, 2 of Article 4 of the Federal Constitutional Law of October 4, 2022 No. 5-FKZ “On the admission of the Donetsk People's Republic into the Russian Federation and the formation within the Russian Federation of a new subject - the Donetsk People's Republic”, legislative and other regulatory legal acts of the Russian Federation are valid on the territory of the Donetsk People's Republic from the date of admission of the Donetsk People's Republic into the Russian Federation and the formation within the Russian Federation Federation of a new subject, unless otherwise provided by this Federal Constitutional Law. Regulatory legal acts of the Donetsk People's Republic are valid on the territory of the Donetsk People's Republic until the end of the transition period or until the adoption of the relevant regulatory legal act of the Russian Federation and (or) the regulatory legal act of the Donetsk People's Republic.
In accordance with Part 7 of Article 12 of the Federal Constitutional Law “On the admission of the Donetsk People's Republic to the Russian Federation and the formation of a new subject within the Russian Federation - the Donetsk People's Republic”, management of property located on the territory of the Donetsk People's Republic, which has signs of ownerlessness and is not classified as federal property, the property of a subject of the Russian Federation or municipal property, is carried out by the authorities of the Donetsk People's Republic in the manner established by the Government of the Russian Federation, if otherwise not provided for by this Federal Constitutional Law. The procedure for recognizing such property as ownerless, established by the regulatory legal acts of the Donetsk People's Republic, is applied until January 1, 2028, in agreement with the federal executive body exercising the functions of managing state property.
According to paragraph 2 of part 1 of Article 21 of Law No. 5-FKZ, until January 1, 2028, on the territory of the Donetsk People's Republic, regulatory legal acts of the Donetsk People's Republic may establish features for regulating property relations, including land turnover, in agreement with the federal executive body performing the functions of managing state property.
In pursuance of the above-mentioned federal constitutional law No. 5-FKZ, the Acting Head of the Donetsk People's Republic signed Decree No. 140 on May 10, 2023, “On the peculiarities of acquiring the right of state and municipal ownership of ownerless real estate on the territory of the Donetsk People’s Republic,” according to which the executive body of the Donetsk People’s Republic authorized to manage state property has the right to go to court with a demand for recognition of the right of state ownership of ownerless buildings after three months from the date of their registration as ownerless real estate by the body carrying out state registration of rights to real estate. The DPR State Property Fund is designated as the executive body of the Donetsk People's Republic authorized to manage state property (clause 1.2 of the Decree).
By Decree of the Acting Head of the Donetsk People's Republic of February 27, 2023 No. 64 “On some issues related to the management of property that has signs of ownerlessness, located on the territory of the Donetsk People's Republic, and the recognition of such property as ownerless,” the Government of the Donetsk People's Republic is vested with the authority to establish (adopt) a procedure for recognizing as ownerless property that has signs of ownerlessness and not classified as federal property, property of a constituent entity of the Russian Federation or municipal property located on the territory of the Donetsk People's Republic, with the exception of real estate in the form of residential premises, residential buildings, and outbuildings.
By Decree of the Head of the Donetsk People's Republic dated December 13, 2024 No. 695 “On the formation (establishment) of the Ministry of Property and Land Relations of the Donetsk People's Republic,” the Ministry of Property and Land Relations of the Donetsk People's Republic was established. Regulations on the Ministry of Property and Land Relations of the Donetsk People's Republic, approved by Decree No. 695 dated December 13, 2024.
In accordance with subclause 3.1.51 of clause 3.1 of the Regulations, the Ministry of Property and Land Relations of the Donetsk People's Republic is empowered to apply to the court with demands to recognize an immovable thing as ownerless and to recognize the right of state ownership of the Donetsk People's Republic to ownerless immovable things.
By virtue of Part 1 of Article 225 of the Civil Code of the Russian Federation, an ownerless thing is a thing that does not have an owner or the owner of which is unknown or, unless otherwise provided by law, the ownership of which the owner has refused.
According to Part 3 of Article 225 of the Civil Code of the Russian Federation, ownerless real estate is accepted for registration by the body that carries out state registration of rights to real estate, upon application from the local government body in whose territory they are located.
In accordance with paragraph 6 of part 1 of Article 262 of the Civil Procedure Code of the Russian Federation, in a special proceeding, the court considers cases of recognizing the right of municipal ownership of an ownerless immovable property.
According to Part 2 of Article 290 of the Civil Procedure Code of the Russian Federation, an application for recognition of ownership of an ownerless immovable property is submitted to the court at its location by the body authorized to manage municipal property or property owned by the federal city of Moscow, St. Petersburg or Sevastopol.
