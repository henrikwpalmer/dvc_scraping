The participants in the trial did not appear at the court hearing; they were duly notified of the place and time of the court hearing; they asked to consider this case in the absence of their representative due to their employment.
Having studied the case materials and examined the evidence presented in the case, the court comes to the following conclusion.
In accordance with clause 6, part 1, art. 262 of the Code of Civil Procedure of the Russian Federation, by way of special proceedings, the court considers cases of recognizing a movable thing as ownerless and recognizing the right of municipal ownership of an ownerless immovable thing.
By virtue of part 3 of Art. 263 of the Code of Civil Procedure of the Russian Federation, which regulates the procedure for consideration and resolution of cases considered by the court in the procedure of special proceedings, if when filing an application or considering the case in the procedure of special proceedings, it is established that there is a dispute about the law, the court issues a ruling on
Full name1
an application without consideration, in which it explains to the applicant and other interested parties their right to resolve the dispute through litigation.
According to the meaning of these norms, the specified application is considered in special proceedings, when there is no need to resolve the dispute about the subjective right itself.
In relation to an application for recognition of the right of municipal ownership of an ownerless immovable thing (clause 1 of Article 290 of the Code of Civil Procedure of the Russian Federation), the absence of a dispute about the right presupposes the absence of claims of other persons to the same immovable thing.
This application was filed as an application considered by the court in a special proceeding, however, from the circumstances established at the court hearing, a dispute about the right is seen, since the issue of recognizing the right of municipal ownership of real estate is being resolved, information about the right holders of which and the fact that the apartment is an object of private ownership is not available, has not been obtained by the court and has not been presented by the applicant.
From the right of everyone to judicial protection of his rights and freedoms, as formulated in Article 46 of the Constitution of the Russian Federation, does not follow the possibility of choosing at his own discretion one or another procedure for judicial protection, the specifics of which in relation to certain types of legal proceedings and categories of cases are determined, based on the Constitution of the Russian Federation, by federal law.
Such federal laws include the Civil Procedural Code of the Russian Federation, which establishes the specifics of consideration and resolution of cases in the order of claim proceedings, the specifics of proceedings in cases arising from public law relations, and the specifics characteristic of special proceedings. In this case, the interested person’s appeal to the court must meet the requirements established for this proceeding.
Under such circumstances, the statement of the administration of the city. Makeevka, the recognition of the right of municipal ownership of ownerless real estate is subject to
