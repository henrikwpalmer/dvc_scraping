Thus, Chapter 33 of the Code of Civil Procedure of the Russian Federation regulates the procedure for considering cases of recognizing a movable thing as ownerless or recognizing ownership of an ownerless immovable thing.
According to paragraph 3 of Art. 218 of the Civil Code of the Russian Federation, in cases and in the manner provided for by this Code, a person may acquire the right of ownership to property that does not have an owner, to property whose owner is unknown, or to property that the owner has abandoned or to which he has lost the right of ownership on other grounds provided by law.
In accordance with paragraph 1 of Art. 225 of the Civil Code of the Russian Federation, ownerless is a thing that does not have an owner, the owner of which is unknown, or the right of ownership to which the owner has refused.
Based on the provisions of paragraph 3 of Art. 225 of the Civil Code of the Russian Federation, ownerless real estate is accepted for registration by the body that carries out state registration of rights to real estate, upon application from the local government body in whose territory they are located. After a year has passed from the date of registration of an ownerless immovable property, the body authorized to manage municipal property may apply to the court with a request to recognize the right of municipal ownership of this thing. An ownerless immovable property, not recognized by a court decision as having come into municipal ownership, may be again taken into possession, use and disposal by the owner who abandoned it, or acquired into ownership by virtue of acquisitive prescription.
According to Art. 130 of the Civil Code of the Russian Federation, immovable things (real estate, real estate) include land plots, subsoil plots and everything that is firmly connected to the land, that is, objects whose movement without disproportionate damage to their purpose is impossible, including buildings, structures, unfinished construction projects. Real estate includes residential and non-residential premises, as well as parts of buildings or structures intended to accommodate vehicles (car spaces), if the boundaries of such premises, parts of buildings or structures are described in the manner established by the legislation on state cadastral registration. Items not related to real estate, including money and securities, are recognized as movable property.
Recognition of ownership of an ownerless immovable property is carried out in a special proceeding upon the application of the body authorized to manage municipal property, in accordance with Art. Art. 290-293 Code of Civil Procedure of the Russian Federation.
By virtue of Part 3 of Art. 263 of the Civil Procedure Code of the Russian Federation, if, when filing an application or considering a case in a special proceeding, it is established that there is a dispute about the right within the jurisdiction of the court, the court issues a ruling to leave the application without consideration, in which it explains to the applicant and other interested parties their right to resolve the dispute in the manner of litigation.
According to the meaning of these norms, the specified application is considered in special proceedings, when there is no need to resolve the dispute about the subjective right itself.

According to Art. 2 of the Law of the Donetsk People's Republic of March 21, 2024 No. 66-RZ “On the specifics of identifying, using and recognizing the right of municipal property of municipalities of the Donetsk People's Republic to residential premises that have signs of ownerless property located on the territory of the Donetsk People's Republic” (hereinafter referred to as Law No. 66-RZ), the signs of ownerless property in relation to residential premises are:
failure to pay for residential premises and utilities within one year from the date of identification of residential premises that have signs of ownerlessness;
lack of information about the registered ownership of residential premises in the Unified State Register of Real Estate;
non-use of residential premises, which, among other things, creates a threat to its safety (or an apartment building if the premises is an apartment, a room in an apartment building), safety for the life and health of other persons (including the rights holders of other premises in an apartment building if the premises is an apartment, a room in an apartment building).
In accordance with Art. 3 of Law No. 66-RZ, local government bodies of the Donetsk People's Republic, represented by bodies authorized by them, independently carry out actions to identify, record and accept residential premises that have signs of ownerless property into the municipal property of the municipalities of the Donetsk People's Republic, on the territories of which they are located, on the grounds provided for by law.
According to Art. 5 of Law No. 66-RZ, the authorized body in connection with information received about residential premises that have signs of ownerlessness:
1) requests information on the official website of the Federal Tax Service of the Russian Federation (https://www.nalog.gov.ru/) - about the availability in the Unified State Register of Legal Entities of information about a legal entity that is a possible owner of residential premises (if it is necessary to establish the status of a legal entity);
2) sends requests to confirm information about identified residential premises that have signs of ownerlessness;
3) within 10 calendar days from the date of discovery of a residential premises that has signs of ownerlessness, places on the official website of the local government body on the Internet, as well as in accessible places at the entrance to the residential premises, an announcement about the identification of a residential premises that has signs of ownerlessness, and about the need for personal appearance before the authorized body of the person (persons) who considers themselves its owner or has (have) rights to it, within 30 calendar days from the date of placement of such an advertisement with the following documents: a) passport of a citizen of the Russian Federation or other identification document; b) title (title) documents for residential premises;
4) after 30 calendar days from the date of publication of the announcement, in the event of the absence of a person (persons) who considers themselves the owner of the residential premises, and after receiving information from the website (if it is necessary to obtain such information) specified in paragraph 1 of this article, responses from the authorities specified in paragraph 2 of this article, indicating that the residential premises, which have signs of ownerless property, do not have an owner: a) within 5 working days, draw up an act of impossibility identifying the owner of this residential premises; b) organizes, if necessary, in the prescribed manner, the carrying out of cadastral work in relation to residential premises that have signs of being ownerless, as well as assigning a postal address to it; c) if necessary, collects other documents confirming the presence of signs of ownerless property in the residential premises, including documents indicating the absence of residents in such residential premises (inspection reports, extracts from house books, personal accounts, etc.); d) organizes a commission inspection of a residential premises that has signs of ownerlessness (with an on-site visit), with the involvement of the necessary representatives of the executive bodies of the Donetsk People's Republic and territorial bodies of the federal executive authorities, organizations (as agreed) and with the drawing up of an appropriate act, which indicates the details of the act, the date of the inspection, information about the persons involved, the location, technical and other condition of the residential premises, an inventory of furniture, household appliances and other movable property located in the residential premises, as well as other information indicating the presence of signs of ownerlessness of the residential premises.
In accordance with part 1 of Art. 10 of Law No. 66-RZ, after three months from the date of registration of a residential premises as ownerless by the territorial body of the federal executive body carrying out state cadastral registration, state registration of rights, maintenance of the Unified State Register of Real Estate, the authorized body applies to the court with an application for recognition of the right of municipal ownership of the municipality to this residential premises in the manner prescribed by the legislation of the Russian Federation.
