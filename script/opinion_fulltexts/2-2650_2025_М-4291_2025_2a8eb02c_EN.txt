The ownerless status of a thing is undesirable for civil circulation; it should be considered as a temporary phenomenon, since it not only makes it difficult to extract its useful properties from such a thing, but creates uncertainty in matters of content, liability due to harm caused during its use, and also entails other negative consequences. Recognition of the right of municipal ownership of ownerless property is closely related to issues of management and disposal of municipal property, which in many cases makes it possible to preserve it from destruction and looting, and makes it possible to use this property in accordance with current legislation. Since within three months from the moment the real estate property was registered as ownerless, no one declared their rights to this property, the administration had the right to apply to the court to recognize the right of municipal ownership to it.

According to Part 3 of Art. 218 of the Civil Code of the Russian Federation, in cases and in the manner provided for by this Code, a person may acquire the right of ownership to property that does not have an owner, to property whose owner is unknown, or to property that the owner has abandoned or to which he has lost the right of ownership on other grounds provided by law.
In accordance with Part 1, Part 3 of Art. 225 of the Civil Code of the Russian Federation, ownerless is a thing that does not have an owner or the owner of which is unknown, or, unless otherwise provided by law, the right of ownership to which the owner has refused. Ownerless real estate is accepted for registration by the body that carries out state registration of rights to real estate, upon application from the local government body in whose territory they are located.
In accordance with Part 1 of Art. 4 of the Federal Constitutional Law of October 4, 2022 No. 5-FKZ “On the admission of the Donetsk People's Republic into the Russian Federation and the formation of a new subject within the Russian Federation - the Donetsk People's Republic”, legislative and other regulatory legal acts of the Russian Federation are valid on the territory of the Donetsk People's Republic from the date of admission of the Donetsk People's Republic into the Russian Federation and the formation of a new entity within the Russian Federation, unless otherwise provided this Federal Constitutional Law.
Regulatory legal acts of the Donetsk People's Republic are valid until the end of the transition period or until the adoption of the corresponding regulatory legal act of the Russian Federation and (or) the regulatory legal act of the Donetsk People's Republic.
According to Part 7 of Art. 12 of the Federal Constitutional Law of October 4, 2022 No. 5-FKZ “On the admission of the Donetsk People's Republic to the Russian Federation and the formation of a new subject within the Russian Federation - the Donetsk People's Republic”, management of property located on the territory of the Donetsk People's Republic, which has signs of ownerlessness and is not classified as federal property, the property of a subject of the Russian Federation or municipal property, is carried out by the authorities of the Donetsk People's Republic in the manner established by the Government of the Russian Federation, unless otherwise provided by this Federal Constitutional Law. The procedure for recognizing such property as ownerless, established by the regulatory legal acts of the Donetsk People's Republic, is applied until January 1, 2028, in agreement with the federal executive body performing the functions of managing state property.
Decree of the Head of the Donetsk People's Republic dated April 28, 2021 No. 116 established a standard procedure for identifying, recording and accepting ownerless real estate and escheatable property into municipal ownership (hereinafter referred to as the Standard Procedure).
According to clause 2.2 of the Standard Procedure, the administrations of districts and cities of the Donetsk People's Republic independently carry out actions to identify, record and accept ownerless real estate into municipal ownership of the administrative-territorial units of the Donetsk People's Republic on the territory of which they are located.
According to clause 2.9 of the Standard Procedure, the administration, within 10 calendar days from the date of discovery of an object of real estate that has signs of ownerlessness, places on the official website of the administration on the Internet an announcement about the identification of an immovable property in the corresponding administrative-territorial unit of the Donetsk People's Republic that has signs of ownerlessness, and about the need for the appearance of a person (persons) who considers themselves its owner or has (have) on She has no right to contact the administration with documents excluding the possibility of considering the specified real estate object ownerless. Photographs of the property may be attached to the inspection report of an item that has signs of being ownerless; sends requests to confirm information about an identified immovable thing that has signs of being ownerless and is subject to this Procedure: to the executive authority that carries out the state registration of real rights to real estate and their restrictions (encumbrances) - about the existence of registered rights to a real estate thing that has signs of being ownerless, and their restrictions (encumbrances); to the executive body implementing state policy and carrying out functions on normative legal regulation, control and supervision in the field of land relations, land management, state registration and maintenance of the State Land Cadastre, land assessment, geodesy and cartography - on the existence of registered rights to a land plot, if the immovable thing is integrally connected with the land plot; to the executive body implementing state policy in the field of taxation and customs affairs - about the availability of information about the legal entity in the Unified State Register of Legal Entities and Individual Entrepreneurs If the summoned person(s) fail to appear, the specified immovable thing will be, at the request of the administration, registered with the body that carries out state registration of real rights to real estate and their encumbrances, as ownerless; within 30 calendar days from the date of publication, organizes a commission inspection of an item that has signs of being ownerless (with an on-site visit), with the involvement of the necessary representatives of government bodies, legal entities (as agreed) and with the preparation of an inspection report of the object, which indicates the location, technical and other condition of the property, as well as other information. or an individual entrepreneur who is a possible owner of the property, as well as regarding information about payment for land at the established address (if the immovable thing is integrally connected with the land plot); to the Migration Service of the Ministry of Internal Affairs of the Donetsk People's Republic, regarding information about the place of residence of an individual who has registered ownership of a real estate item that has signs of being ownerless; to the State Property Fund of the Donetsk People's Republic - about the presence of this object in the Unified Register of State Property Objects and objects in respect of which temporary administration (operational management) has been introduced, as well as objects of other forms of ownership under state control; to the relevant department of the administration - about the presence of an object in municipal ownership; to state and municipal enterprises that provide services to legal entities and individuals in the field of housing and communal services - on fulfilling the burden of maintaining immovable property that has signs of being ownerless.
According to clause 2.11 of the Standard Procedure, after 30 calendar days from the date of publication of the announcement, in the event of the absence of a person (persons) who considers themselves the owner of an immovable thing, and after receiving from the authorities specified in subclause 2.9.3 of clause 2.9 of this Procedure, responses indicating the absence of the owner of an immovable thing that has signs of being ownerless, the administration: within 5 working days draws up an act on impossibility of identifying the owner of this property; organizes, if necessary, in the prescribed manner, work on conducting a technical inventory of immovable property that has signs of being ownerless, preparing technical documentation for this object, as well as assigning a postal address to it; carries out, if necessary, the collection of other documents confirming the presence of signs of ownerless things, including documents indicating the absence of residents in ownerless residential premises (inspection reports, extracts from house books, personal accounts, etc.); to register an immovable thing as ownerless, issues an appropriate order and applies to the body that carries out state registration of real rights to real estate and their restrictions (encumbrances) with an application to register the immovable thing as ownerless.
According to Part 2 of Art. 290 of the Code of Civil Procedure of the Russian Federation, an application for recognition of ownership of an ownerless immovable property is submitted to the court at its location by the body authorized to manage municipal property or property owned by the federal city of Moscow, St. Petersburg or Sevastopol.
According to Part 2 of Art. 291 of the Code of Civil Procedure of the Russian Federation, in the application of the body authorized to manage municipal property or property owned by the federal city of Moscow, St. Petersburg or Sevastopol, on the recognition of ownership of an ownerless immovable thing, it must be indicated by whom, when the immovable thing was registered, and evidence indicating the absence of its owner must also be provided.
According to clause 4.4 of Article 1 of Federal Law No. 218-FZ of July 13, 2015 “On State Registration of Real Estate,” state registration of rights is carried out by entering into the Unified State Register of Real Estate an entry about the right to real estate, information about which is included in the Unified State Register of Real Estate. State registration of the transfer of the right to a real estate object, restriction of the right to a real estate object, encumbrance of a real estate object or a transaction with a real estate object is carried out subject to the existence of state registration of the right to this object in the Unified State Register of Real Estate, unless otherwise established by Federal Law.
The grounds for the implementation of state cadastral registration and (or) state registration of rights are, in particular, acts issued by state authorities or local government bodies within their competence and in the manner established by the legislation in force at the place of publication of such acts at the time of their publication, and establishing the existence, emergence, transfer, termination of a right or restriction of a right and encumbrance of a property (clause 1, part 2, article 14 of Law 218-FZ dated July 13, 2015).
According to paragraphs. 2, 3, 12, 13 of the Procedure for registering ownerless real estate, established by Order of Rosreestr No. P/0086 dated March 15, 2023, registration of ownerless real estate is carried out by the federal executive body, which carries out the functions of developing state policy and legal regulation in the field of state cadastral registration of real estate and state registration of rights to real estate and transactions with it, its territorial bodies, executive authorities of the Republic of Crimea and the federal city of Sevastopol upon transferring to them the powers of the rights registration body by resolutions of the Government of the Russian Federation in accordance with Part 8 of Article 72 of Federal Law No. 218-FZ (hereinafter referred to as the rights registration body). Buildings (structures), structures, premises, parking spaces, objects of unfinished construction (hereinafter referred to as real estate objects), information about which is included in the Unified State Register of Real Estate (hereinafter referred to as the Unified State Register of Real Estate) and which do not have owners or whose owners are unknown, or, unless otherwise provided by law, the owners of which have renounced their ownership rights, are accepted for registration. The decision to register a real estate property as ownerless is made no later than fifteen working days from the date of receipt of the application and necessary documents by the rights registration authority. Registration of a real estate property as ownerless is carried out by entering the relevant information into the Unified State Register of Real Estate in accordance with the procedure for maintaining the Unified State Register of Real Estate, approved in accordance with Part 7 of Article 7 of Federal Law No. 218-FZ.
According to Article 2 of the DPR Law “On the specifics of identifying, using and recognizing the right of municipal property of municipalities of the Donetsk People’s Republic to residential premises that have signs of ownerless property located on the territory of the Donetsk People’s Republic”, adopted by the Resolution of the People’s Council on March 21, 2024 (hereinafter referred to as Law No. 66-RZ of March 21, 2024), signs of ownerless property in relation to residential premises are: failure to pay for residential premises and utilities within one year before the day of identification of residential premises that have signs of ownerlessness; lack of information about the registered ownership of residential premises in the Unified State Register of Real Estate; non-use of residential premises, which, among other things, creates a threat to its safety (or an apartment building if the premises is an apartment, a room in an apartment building), safety for the life and health of other persons (including the rights holders of other premises in an apartment building if the premises is an apartment, a room in an apartment building).
Local government bodies of the Donetsk People's Republic, represented by bodies authorized by them (hereinafter referred to as authorized bodies), independently carry out actions to identify, record and accept residential premises that have signs of ownerless property into the municipal ownership of the municipalities of the Donetsk People's Republic, on the territory of which they are located, on the grounds provided for by law (Article 3 of Law No. 66-RZ).
According to Article 10 of Law No. 66-RZ of March 21, 2024, after three months from the date of registration of a residential premises as ownerless by the territorial body of the federal executive body carrying out state cadastral registration, state registration of rights, maintaining the Unified State Register of Real Estate, the authorized body applies to the court with an application for recognition of the right of municipal ownership of the municipality to this residential premises in the manner prescribed legislation of the Russian Federation.
