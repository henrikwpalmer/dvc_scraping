The applicant - the administration of the city district of Mariupol of the Donetsk People's Republic, the interested party - the Office of Rosreestr for the Donetsk People's Republic, were duly notified of the date, time and place of the consideration of the case, did not appear at the court hearing, submitted to the court applications for consideration of the case in their absence.
In accordance with the requirements of Part 3 of Article 167 of the Civil Procedure Code of the Russian Federation (hereinafter referred to as the Civil Procedure Code of the Russian Federation), the court considers it possible to consider the case in the absence of interested parties.
The court, having examined the case materials and assessed the totality of the evidence presented, considers the stated demands to be justified and subject to satisfaction, for the following reasons.
According to Art. 56 of the Code of Civil Procedure of the Russian Federation, each party must prove the circumstances to which it refers as the basis for its claims and objections.
By virtue of Art. 67 of the Code of Civil Procedure of the Russian Federation, the court evaluates evidence according to its internal conviction, based on a comprehensive, complete, objective and direct examination of the evidence available in the case.
According to Part 3 of Art. 218 of the Civil Code of the Russian Federation, in cases and in the manner provided for by this Code, a person may acquire the right of ownership to property that does not have an owner, to property whose owner is unknown, or to property that the owner has abandoned or to which he has lost the right of ownership on other grounds provided by law.
In accordance with Part 1 of Art. 225 of the Civil Code of the Russian Federation, ownerless is a thing that does not have an owner, the owner of which is unknown, or the right of ownership to which the owner has refused.
Based on the provisions of Part 3 of Art. 225 of the Civil Code of the Russian Federation, ownerless real estate is accepted for registration by the body that carries out state registration of rights to real estate, upon application from the local government body in whose territory they are located.
According to Art. 130 of the Civil Code of the Russian Federation, immovable things (real estate, real estate) include land plots, subsoil plots and everything that is firmly connected to the land, that is, objects whose movement without disproportionate damage to their purpose is impossible, including buildings, structures, unfinished construction objects. Real estate includes residential and non-residential premises, as well as parts of buildings or structures intended to accommodate vehicles (car spaces), if the boundaries of such premises, parts of buildings or structures are described in the manner established by the legislation on state cadastral registration. Items not related to real estate, including money and securities, are recognized as movable property. Registration of rights to movable things is not required, except in cases specified in the law.
Recognition of ownership of an ownerless immovable property is carried out in a special proceeding upon the application of the body authorized to manage municipal property, in accordance with Art. Art. 290-293 Code of Civil Procedure of the Russian Federation.
In accordance with Part 1 of Art. 4 of the Federal Constitutional Law of October 4, 2022 No. 5-FKZ “On the admission of the Donetsk People’s Republic to the Russian Federation and the formation of a new subject within the Russian Federation - the Donetsk People’s Republic” (hereinafter referred to as Law No. 5-FKZ), legislative and other regulatory legal acts of the Russian Federation are valid on the territory
of the Donetsk People's Republic from the date of acceptance of the Donetsk People's Republic into the Russian Federation and the formation of a new subject within the Russian Federation, unless otherwise provided by Law No. 5-FKZ.
According to Part 2 of Art. 4 of Law No. 5-FKZ, the regulatory legal acts of the Donetsk People's Republic are valid on the territory of the Donetsk People's Republic until the end of the transition period or until the adoption of the relevant regulatory legal act of the Russian Federation and (or) the regulatory legal act of the Donetsk People's Republic.
At the same time, Art. 21 of Law No. 5-FKZ establishes the specifics of regulating relations in the sphere of real estate turnover on the territory of the Donetsk People's Republic.
In accordance with clause 2, part 1, art. 21 of Law No. 5-FKZ, until January 1, 2028, on the territory of the Donetsk People's Republic, regulatory legal acts of the Donetsk People's Republic may establish features of the regulation of property relations, including the circulation of land, in agreement with the federal executive body performing the functions of managing state property.
By virtue of Part 7 of Art. 12 of Law No. 5-FKZ, the procedure for recognizing property that is not classified as federal property, the property of a constituent entity of the Russian Federation or municipal property as ownerless, established by the regulatory legal acts of the Donetsk People's Republic, is applied until January 1, 2028 in agreement with the federal executive body performing the functions of managing state property.
In pursuance of the above norm, Resolution of the Government of the Russian Federation dated December 28, 2022 No. 2474 was adopted “On approval of the Rules for the management of bodies of the Donetsk People's Republic, Lugansk People's Republic, Zaporozhye region and Kherson region having signs of ownerlessness and not classified as federal property, property of a constituent entity of the Russian Federation or municipal property, located on the territory of these constituent entities of the Russian Federation”, Law of Donetsk of the People's Republic of March 21, 2024 No. 66-RZ “On the peculiarities of identifying, using and recognizing the right of municipal ownership of municipalities of the Donetsk People's Republic to residential premises with signs of an ownerless creature located on the territory of the Donetsk People's Republic”, amendments were made to the Decree of the Head of the Donetsk People's Republic dated
No. 116 “On the identification, recording and acceptance into municipal ownership of ownerless real estate and escheat property.”
In order to accept, record and register ownerless real estate escheatable property, improve the efficiency of its use, the administration of the city of Mariupol adopted Order No. 766 dated 05.12.2023 “On approval of the Procedure for identifying, recording and accepting ownerless real estate and escheatable property into municipal ownership on the administrative territory of the municipality of the city of Mariupol” (as amended).
In accordance with paragraph 2.12 of the Decree of the Head of the Donetsk People's Republic of
No. 116 “On the identification, registration and acceptance into municipal ownership of ownerless real estate and escheat”, in order to register an immovable object as ownerless, the administration issues a corresponding order and applies to the territorial body of the federal executive body carrying out state cadastral registration, state registration of rights, maintaining the Unified State Register of Real Estate, with an application to register the immovable object as ownerless and other documents provided for by current legislation. A similar norm is enshrined in paragraph 2 of part 1 of Article 7 of the Law of the Donetsk People’s Republic of March 21, 2024 No. 66-RZ “On the specifics of identifying, using and recognizing the right of municipal property of municipalities of the Donetsk People’s Republic to residential premises with signs of ownerless property located on the territory of the Donetsk People’s Republic.”
In accordance with paragraph 2.12 of the Decree of the Head of the Donetsk People's Republic of
No. 116 “On the identification, registration and acceptance into municipal ownership of ownerless real estate and escheat”, in order to register an immovable object as ownerless, the administration issues a corresponding order and applies to the territorial body of the federal executive body carrying out state cadastral registration, state registration of rights, maintaining the Unified State Register of Real Estate, with an application to register the immovable object as ownerless and other documents provided for by current legislation.
A similar norm is enshrined in paragraph 2 of part 1 of Article 7 of the Law of the Donetsk People’s Republic of March 21, 2024 No. 66-RZ “On the specifics of identifying, using and recognizing the right of municipal property of municipalities of the Donetsk People’s Republic to residential premises with signs of ownerless property located on the territory of the Donetsk People’s Republic.”
According to Art. 2 of the Law of the Donetsk People's Republic of March 21, 2024 No. 66-RZ “On the specifics of identifying, using and recognizing the right of municipal property of municipalities of the Donetsk People's Republic to residential premises that have signs of ownerless property located on the territory of the Donetsk People's Republic” (hereinafter referred to as Law No. 66-RZ), the signs of ownerless property in relation to residential premises are:
failure to pay for residential premises and utilities within one year from the date of identification of residential premises that have signs of ownerlessness;
lack of information about the registered ownership of residential premises in the Unified State Register of Real Estate;
non-use of residential premises, which, among other things, creates a threat to its safety (or an apartment building if the premises is an apartment, a room in an apartment building), safety for the life and health of other persons (including the rights holders of other premises in an apartment building if the premises is an apartment, a room in an apartment building).
In accordance with Art. 3 of Law No. 66-RZ, local government bodies of the Donetsk People's Republic, represented by bodies authorized by them, independently carry out actions to identify, record and accept residential premises that have signs of ownerless property into the municipal property of the municipalities of the Donetsk People's Republic, on the territories of which they are located, on the grounds provided for by law.
In accordance with Part 1 of Art. 10 of Law No. 66-RZ, a completely similar norm is enshrined in paragraph 2 of part 1 of Article 7 of the Law of the Donetsk People’s Republic of March 21, 2024 No. 66-RZ “On the peculiarities of identifying, using and recognizing the right of municipal property of municipalities of the Donetsk People’s Republic
Republic for residential premises that have signs of ownerless property located on the territory of the Donetsk People's Republic", after three months from the date of registration of the residential premises as ownerless by the territorial body of the federal executive body carrying out state cadastral registration, state registration of rights, maintaining the Unified State Register of Real Estate, the authorized body applies to the court with an application for recognition of the right of municipal ownership of the municipality to this residential premises in the manner prescribed legislation of the Russian Federation.
The court found that the administration of the city district of Mariupol acts on the basis of Decision No. 1-8/3 dated November 30, 2023, “On issues of legal succession in the municipal formation of the city district of Mariupol DPR”, the main tasks of the administration are in the field of socio-economic development of municipal property management and the powers of a participant in the budget process: management of municipal property, resolving issues of creation, acquisition, use, lease of municipal property, making decisions in advance with the administration of the Head Donetsk People's Republic on the transfer of property from municipal to State or municipal property and on the acceptance of property from state property to municipal property; ensuring the identification, accounting and acceptance into municipal ownership of ownerless real estate and escheat property within the powers and in the manner established by the Head of the Donetsk People's Republic.
