According to Part 3 of Article 218 of the Civil Code of the Russian Federation, in cases and in the manner provided for by this Code, a person may acquire ownership of property that does not have an owner, property whose owner is unknown, or property that the owner has abandoned or to which he has lost ownership on other grounds provided by law.
In accordance with Article 225 of the Civil Code of the Russian Federation, an ownerless thing is a thing that does not have an owner or the owner of which is unknown, or, unless otherwise provided by law, a property right to which the owner has refused.
In accordance with Part 3 of Article 225 of the Civil Code of the Russian Federation, ownerless real estate is accepted for registration by the body that carries out state registration of rights to real estate, upon application from the local government body in whose territory they are located.
In accordance with Part 1 of Article 4 of the Federal Constitutional Law of October 4, 2022 No. 5-FKZ “On the admission of the Donetsk People's Republic into the Russian Federation and the formation of a new subject within the Russian Federation - the Donetsk People's Republic”, legislative and other regulatory legal acts of the Russian Federation are in force on the territory of the Donetsk People's Republic from the date of admission of the Donetsk People's Republic into the Russian Federation and the formation of a new entity within the Russian Federation subject, unless otherwise provided by this Federal Constitutional Law.
Regulatory legal acts of the Donetsk People's Republic are valid until the end of the transition period or until the adoption of the corresponding regulatory legal act of the Russian Federation and (or) a regulatory legal act
Full name10
Republic.
According to Part 7 of Article 12 of the Federal Constitutional Law of October 4, 2022 No. 5-FKZ “On the admission of the Donetsk People’s Republic to the Russian Federation and the formation of a new subject within the Russian Federation - the Donetsk People’s Republic”, management of property located on the territory of the Donetsk People’s Republic, which has signs of ownerlessness and is not classified as federal property, the property of a constituent entity of the Russian Federation or municipal property, is carried out by the authorities of the Donetsk of the People's Republic in the manner established by the Government of the Russian Federation, unless otherwise provided by this Federal Constitutional Law. The procedure for recognizing such property as ownerless, established by the regulatory legal acts of the Donetsk People's Republic, is applied until January 1, 2028, in agreement with the federal executive body performing the functions of managing state property.
Decree of the Head of the Donetsk People's Republic dated April 28, 2021 No. 116 established the Standard Procedure for identifying, recording and accepting ownerless real estate and escheatable property into municipal ownership (hereinafter referred to as the Standard Procedure).
According to clause 2.2. According to the standard procedure, the administrations of districts and cities of the Donetsk People's Republic independently carry out actions to identify, record and accept ownerless real estate into the municipal ownership of the administrative-territorial units of the Donetsk People's Republic on the territory of which they are located.
According to clause 2.9 of the Standard Procedure, within 10 calendar days from the date of discovery of an object of real estate that has signs of ownerlessness, an announcement is placed on the official website of the administration on the Internet about the identification of a real estate item in the relevant administrative-territorial unit of the Donetsk People's Republic that has signs of ownerlessness, and about the need for the appearance of a person (persons) who considers themselves its owner or has (have) ownership of it rights, contact the administration with documents excluding the possibility of considering the specified real estate object ownerless. If the summoned person(s) fail to appear, the specified immovable thing will, at the request of the administration, be registered with the body that carries out state registration of real rights to real estate and their encumbrances, as ownerless. Within 30 calendar days from the date of publication, organizes a commission inspection of an item that has signs of being ownerless (with an on-site visit), with the involvement of the necessary representatives of government bodies, legal entities (as agreed) and with the preparation of an inspection report of the object, which indicates the location, technical and other condition of the property, as well as other information. Photographs of the property may be attached to the inspection report of an item that has signs of being ownerless. Sends requests to confirm information about an identified immovable thing that has signs of ownerlessness and is subject to this Procedure to the executive body that carries out state registration of real rights to real estate and their restrictions (encumbrances), - about the existence of registered rights to immovable things that have signs of ownerlessness, and their restrictions (encumbrances), to the executive body that implements state policy and carries out functions according to the normative legal regulation, control and supervision in the field of land relations, implementation of land management, state registration and maintenance of the State Land Cadastre, land valuation, geodesy and cartography, - on the existence of registered rights to a land plot, if the immovable thing is integrally connected with the land plot; to the executive body implementing state policy in the field of taxation and customs - about the availability in the Unified State Register of Legal Entities and Individual Entrepreneurs of information about a legal entity or individual entrepreneur who is a possible owner of the property, as well as information about payment for land at the established address (if the immovable thing is integrally connected with the land plot); to the Migration Service of the Ministry of Internal Affairs
Full name10
Republic of an individual who has registered ownership of an immovable thing that has signs of being ownerless; to the State Property Fund
Full name10
Republic - on the presence of this object in the Unified Register of State Property Objects and objects in respect of which temporary administration (operational management) has been introduced, as well as objects of other forms of ownership under state control; to the relevant department of the administration - about the presence of an object in municipal ownership; to state and municipal enterprises that provide services to legal entities and individuals in the field of housing and communal services - on fulfilling the burden of maintaining immovable property that has signs of being ownerless.
According to clause 2.11 of the Standard Procedure, after 30 calendar days from the date of publication of the announcement, in the event of the absence of a person (persons) who considers themselves the owner of an immovable thing, and after receiving from the authorities specified in subclause 2.9.3 of clause 2.9 of this Procedure, responses indicating the absence of the owner of an immovable thing that has signs of being ownerless, the administration: within 5 working days draws up an act on impossibility of identifying the owner of this property. Organizes, if necessary, in the prescribed manner, work on conducting a technical inventory of real estate that has signs of being ownerless, preparing technical documentation for this object, as well as assigning a postal address to it. If necessary, collects other documents confirming the presence of signs of ownerless things, including documents indicating the absence of residents in ownerless residential premises (inspection reports, extracts from house books, personal accounts, etc.). To register an immovable thing as ownerless, he issues an appropriate order and applies to the body that carries out state registration of real rights to real estate and their restrictions (encumbrances) with an application to register the immovable thing as ownerless.
According to Article 290 of the Code of Civil Procedure of the Russian Federation, an application to recognize a movable thing as ownerless is submitted to the court by the person who took possession of it at the place of residence or location of the applicant. An application for recognition of ownership of an ownerless immovable property is submitted to the court at its location by the body authorized to manage municipal property or property owned by the federal city of Moscow, St. Petersburg or Sevastopol.
According to Part 1 of Article 291 of the Code of Civil Procedure of the Russian Federation, the application for recognition of a movable thing as ownerless must indicate which movable thing is subject to recognition as ownerless, its main features must be described, as well as evidence indicating the owner’s renunciation of ownership of it, and evidence indicating that the applicant has taken possession of this thing.
According to clause 4.4 of Article 1 of Federal Law No. 218-FZ dated
DD.MM.YYYY
“On State Registration of Real Estate”, state registration of rights is carried out by entering into the Unified State Register of Real Estate an entry on the right to real estate, information about which is included in the Unified State Register of Real Estate. State registration of the transfer of the right to a real estate object, restriction of the right to a real estate object, encumbrance of a real estate object or a transaction with a real estate object is carried out subject to the existence of state registration of the right to this object in the Unified State Register of Real Estate, unless otherwise established by Federal Law.
The grounds for the implementation of state cadastral registration and (or) state registration of rights are, in particular, acts issued by state authorities or local government bodies within their competence and in the manner established by the legislation in force at the place of publication of such acts at the time of their publication, and establishing the existence, emergence, transfer, termination of a right or restriction of a right and encumbrance of a property (clause 1, part 2, article 14 of Law 218-FZ from

According to the Procedure for registering ownerless real estate, established by Order of Rosreestr No. P/0086 dated
DD.MM.YYYY
, the registration of ownerless real estate is carried out by the federal executive body, which carries out the functions of developing state policy and legal regulation in the field of state cadastral registration of real estate and state registration of rights to real estate and transactions with it, its territorial bodies, executive authorities of the Republic of Crimea and the federal city of Sevastopol when transferring to them the powers of the body for registration of rights by resolutions of the Government of the Russian Federation in accordance with Part 8 Article 72 of Federal Law No. 218-FZ (hereinafter referred to as the rights registration authority). Buildings (structures), structures, premises, parking spaces, objects of unfinished construction (hereinafter referred to as real estate objects), information about which is included in the Unified State Register of Real Estate (hereinafter referred to as the Unified State Register of Real Estate) and which do not have owners or whose owners are unknown, or, unless otherwise provided by law, the ownership rights to which the owners have renounced, are accepted for registration. The decision to register a real estate property as ownerless is made no later than fifteen working days from the date of receipt of the application and necessary documents by the rights registration authority. Registration of a real estate property as ownerless is carried out by entering the relevant information into the Unified State Register of Real Estate in accordance with the procedure for maintaining the Unified State Register of Real Estate, approved in accordance with Part 7 of Article 7 of Federal Law No. 218-FZ (clauses 2, 3, 12, 13).
