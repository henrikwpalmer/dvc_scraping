According to Part 1 of Art. 263 of the Code of Civil Procedure of the Russian Federation, cases of special proceedings are considered and resolved by the court according to the general rules of claim proceedings with the features established by this chapter and Chapters 28 - 38 of this Code.
According to parts 1 and 2 of Article 39 of the Code of Civil Procedure of the Russian Federation, the plaintiff has the right to abandon the claim. The court does not accept the plaintiff’s refusal of the claim if it is contrary to the law or violates the rights and legitimate interests of other persons.
In accordance with paragraph 4 of Art. 220 of the Code of Civil Procedure of the Russian Federation, the court terminates the proceedings if the plaintiff renounces the claim and the refusal is accepted by the court.
Having examined the materials of this civil case, having listened to the opinion of the prosecutor and the representative of the interested party, who did not object to accepting the applicant’s refusal from the demand and termination of the proceedings, I believe that the refusal of the representative of the Ministry of Property and Land Relations of the Donetsk People’s Republic from the stated demands does not contradict the law and does not violate the rights and legitimate interests of other persons, in connection with which I consider it possible to accept it and terminate the proceedings.
The consequences of abandoning a claim in the form of the inadmissibility of re-going to court in a dispute between the same parties, about the same subject and on the same grounds, provided for in Art. 221 of the Code of Civil Procedure of the Russian Federation, known to the applicant.
Guided by the above and in accordance with Article 173, Part 2 of Article 39, Article 221, Article 224-225 of the Code of Civil Procedure of the Russian Federation, the court –
defined:
