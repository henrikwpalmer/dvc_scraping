To case No. 2 – 214 / 2025
DEFINITION
January 31, 2025 Mariupol
Ordzhonikidze District Court of Mariupol, consisting of:
presiding judge Pavlenko D.K.,
when keeping minutes of a court session
secretary of the court session Novikova Yu.V.,
with the assistant judge Russu A.I.,
having considered in open court the case on the application of the administration of the city district of Mariupol of the Donetsk People's Republic for recognition of the right of municipal ownership of ownerless real estate,
INSTALLED:
City administration Mariupol DPR appealed to the court with an application to the Rosreestr office for the DPR to recognize the right of municipal ownership of ownerless real estate. Asks the court to recognize the immovable property as ownerless, the purpose of which is non-residential premises, with an area of 70 sq.m., cadastral

<address>
; asks the court to recognize the administration of the city. Mariupol DPR right of municipal ownership of an ownerless immovable property, purpose - non-residential premises, area 70 sq.m., cadastral
№
, located at:
<address>
. At the court hearing Kravchenko E.V. representative by proxy of the city administration Mariupol DPR did not appear, was duly notified of the day, time and place of its holding, submitted to the court a statement of refusal of the stated demands, and asked to consider the case on the merits in her absence. The representative of the Rosreestr department for the DPR did not appear at the court hearing, was duly notified of the day, time and place of its holding, and did not report the reasons for the non-appearance. Taking into account the fact that the parties were properly notified of the day, time and place of the court hearing in the case, the court considers it possible to consider the case on the merits in their absence. Having examined the case materials, the court comes to the following: As was established at the court hearing, the administration of the city. Mariupol DPR refuses its claims for recognition of ownerless real estate, purpose - non-residential premises, area 70 sq.m., cadastral
№
, located at:
<address>
; and on recognition by the administration of the city. Mariupol DPR municipal property rights to an ownerless immovable property, purpose - non-residential premises, area 70 sq.m., cadastral
№
, located at:
<address>
. In accordance with Art. 220 of the Civil Procedure Code of the Russian Federation, the court terminates the proceedings if the plaintiff renounces the claim and the refusal is accepted by the court /p. 4/. Taking into account the requirements of the current legislation, as well as the fact that the refusal of the city administration Mariupol DPR from the demands made to it does not contradict the law and does not violate the rights and legitimate interests of third parties; the court considers it possible to accept this refusal and terminate the proceedings. Based on the above, guided by Articles 39.220, 224 – 225 of the Civil Procedure Code of the Russian Federation, the court
DEFINED:
Accept the refusal of the administration of the city district of Mariupol of the Donetsk People's Republic to recognize the right of municipal ownership of ownerless real estate - non-residential premises with an area of 70 sq.m., cadastral
№
, located at:
<address>
.
Terminate the proceedings on the application of the administration of the city district of Mariupol of the Donetsk People's Republic to recognize the right of municipal ownership of ownerless real estate.
The ruling can be appealed by filing a private complaint with the Supreme Court of the Donetsk People's Republic through the Ordzhonikidze District Court of Mariupol within fifteen working days from the date of its issuance.
