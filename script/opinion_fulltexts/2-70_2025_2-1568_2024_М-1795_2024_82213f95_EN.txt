In accordance with Part 1 of Article 1 of the Federal Constitutional Law of October 4, 2022 No. 5-FKZ “On the admission of the Donetsk People's Republic to the Russian Federation and the formation of a new subject within the Russian Federation - the Donetsk People's Republic”, the Donetsk People's Republic is admitted to the Russian Federation.
By virtue of Part 7 of Article 12 of the Federal Constitutional Law of October 4, 2022 No. 5-FKZ “On the admission of the Donetsk People's Republic into the Russian Federation and the formation of a new subject within the Russian Federation - the Donetsk People's Republic”, management of property located on the territory of the Donetsk People's Republic, which has signs of ownerlessness and is not classified as federal property, the property of a subject of the Russian Federation or municipal property, is carried out by the authorities of the Donetsk of the People's Republic in the manner established by the Government of the Russian Federation, unless otherwise provided by this Federal Constitutional Law. The procedure for recognizing such property as ownerless, established by the regulatory legal acts of the Donetsk People's Republic, is applied until January 1, 2028, in agreement with the federal executive body exercising the functions of managing state property.
In accordance with paragraph 6 of part 1 of Article 262 of the Code of Civil Procedure of the Russian Federation, cases considered by a court of general jurisdiction in special proceedings include cases of recognition of a movable thing as ownerless and recognition of the right of municipal ownership of an ownerless immovable thing.
In paragraph 59 of the Resolution of the Plenum of the Supreme Court of the Russian Federation No. 10, the Plenum of the Supreme Arbitration Court of the Russian Federation No. 22 of April 29, 2010 “On some issues arising in judicial practice when resolving disputes related to the protection of property rights and other property rights” it is explained that, unless otherwise provided by law, a claim for recognition of a right is subject to satisfaction if the plaintiff provides evidence of the emergence of the corresponding right.
According to paragraph 3 of Article 218 of the Civil Code of the Russian Federation, in cases and in the manner provided for by this Code, a person may acquire ownership of property that does not have an owner, property whose owner is unknown, or property that the owner has abandoned or to which he has lost ownership on other grounds provided by law.
In accordance with Article 219 of the Civil Code of the Russian Federation, ownership of buildings, structures and other newly created real estate, subject to state registration, arises from the moment of such registration.
Paragraph 1 of Article 225 of the Civil Code of the Russian Federation states that an ownerless thing is a thing that does not have an owner or the owner of which is unknown or, unless otherwise provided by law, the right of ownership to which the owner has renounced.
Ownerless real estate is accepted for registration by the body that carries out state registration of rights to real estate, upon application from the local government body in whose territory they are located. After a year has passed from the date of registration of an ownerless immovable property, the body authorized to manage municipal property may apply to the court with a request to recognize the right of municipal ownership of this thing (clause 3 of Article 225 of the Civil Code of the Russian Federation).
The procedure for recognizing the ownership of an ownerless immovable property by the body authorized to manage municipal property is defined in Chapter 33 of the Civil Procedure Code of the Russian Federation (hereinafter referred to as the Code of Civil Procedure of the Russian Federation).
Based on Part 2 of Article 293 of the Code of Civil Procedure of the Russian Federation, the court, having recognized that the immovable thing does not have an owner or the owner of the immovable thing is unknown, and it was registered in the prescribed manner, makes a decision to recognize the right of municipal property or the property of the federal city of Moscow or St. Petersburg to this thing.
By Decree of the Acting Head of the Donetsk People's Republic of May 10, 2023. No. 140 (hereinafter referred to as Decree No. 140 dated May 10, 2023) establishes the specifics of acquiring the right of state and municipal ownership of ownerless real estate on the territory of the Donetsk People’s Republic.
According to clause 1.1 of the Decree of May 10, 2023. No. 140, the body and officials of the Donetsk People's Republic exercising powers classified by the legislation of the Russian Federation as the powers of local self-government bodies, before the completion of the formation of local self-government bodies, the local government body of the municipal formation in the Donetsk People's Republic, authorized to manage municipal property, has the right to apply to the court with a demand for recognition of the right of municipal ownership of ownerless buildings, structures (except for linear objects), premises, objects of unfinished construction that do not have an owner or the owner of which is unknown, or the ownership of which the owner has refused, located on the territory of such a municipal entity, after three months from the date of their registration as ownerless real estate by the body carrying out state registration of rights to real estate, unless otherwise provided by this Decree.
In accordance with paragraph 2.12 of the Decree of the Head of the Donetsk People's Republic of April 28, 2021. No. 116 “On the identification, registration and acceptance into municipal ownership of ownerless real estate and escheat property”, in order to register an immovable object as ownerless, the administration issues a corresponding order and applies to the territorial body of the federal executive body carrying out state cadastral registration, state registration of rights, maintaining the Unified State Register of Real Estate, with an application to register the immovable object as ownerless and other documents provided for by current legislation.
According to paragraph 4 of the Procedure for registering ownerless real estate, established by the Order of the Federal Service for State Registration, Cadastre and Cartography dated March 15, 2023. No. P/0086, registration of ownerless real estate objects is carried out in the following order: acceptance of an application for registration of ownerless real estate objects, for taking the real estate object back into possession, use and disposal (hereinafter referred to as the application) and the documents provided for in paragraph 10 of the Procedure for registering ownerless real estate objects, checking documents for compliance with the requirements of Federal Law No. 218-FZ to documents received by the rights registration authority, making entries in the Unified State Register of Real Estate on the registration of ownerless real estate objects.
By the decision of the Mariupol City Council of the DPR dated November 30, 2023. No. I-8/3 “On issues of legal succession in the municipality of the urban district of Mariupol of the Donetsk People's Republic”, the successor to the administration of the city of Mariupol DPR is the administration of the urban district of Mariupol DPR.
By resolution of the administration of the city district of Mariupol dated May 29, 2024. No. 163 approved the regulation “On the procedure for identifying, recording and accepting into municipal ownership property that has signs of ownerlessness (with the exception of real estate objects, including escheat, in the form of residential premises, garden houses, outbuildings intended to satisfy citizens' household and other needs, linear objects), located on the administrative territory of the municipality of the Mariupol urban district of the Donetsk People's Republic.
In accordance with clause 1.2 of the regulation, a unified procedure is determined for identifying, registering, managing and accepting into municipal ownership property that has signs of ownerlessness (with the exception of real estate objects, including escheat, in the form of residential premises, garden houses, outbuildings intended to satisfy citizens' household and other needs, linear objects), located on the administrative territory of the municipality of Mariupol.
By virtue of clause 2.1 of the regulation, the administration of the city district of Mariupol DPR, represented by the authorized body of the Department of Property and Land Relations of the administration of the city district of Mariupol DPR, organizes activities to identify, record and acquire ownerless real estate into the municipal ownership of the municipality. Information about real estate that has signs of ownerlessness may come from: executive bodies of state power of the Russian Federation and the Donetsk People's Republic; constituent entities of the Russian Federation and the Donetsk People's Republic; local government bodies; as a result of inventory; based on applications from legal entities and individuals; in other ways.
By virtue of clause 1.1 of the provision, real estate objects that do not have owners, or whose owners are unknown, or whose ownership rights have been renounced, are accepted into the municipal ownership of the municipality.
By virtue of clause 1.5 of the regulation, the main goals and objectives of identifying ownerless real estate objects are: involving unused objects in free civil circulation; ensuring normal and safe technology in the operation of facilities; increasing the efficiency of property use.
By virtue of clause 2.9.2 of the regulation, after 30 days from the date of publication of the announcement specified in clause 2.9.1 of the regulation, in the event of the failure of a person who considers himself the owner of the real estate property to appear, within 5 days he draws up an act on the impossibility of identifying the owner of this property or another act appropriate to the situation.
By virtue of clause 3.1 of the regulation, an ownerless real estate object is taken into account in the register of identified ownerless real estate on the territory of the municipality (Appendix No. 4 to the regulations) from the date of registration of the real estate object as ownerless by the Office of the Federal Service for State Registration, Cadastre and Cartography of the Donetsk People's Republic, until the moment the right of municipal ownership to such an object arises, or until deregistration with the body carrying out state registration of rights to real estate and transactions with it when declaring owners.
By virtue of clause 4.5 of the regulation (taking into account changes by the decree of the administration of the city district of Mariupol dated September 26, 2024 No. 438), after three months from the date of registration of an ownerless real estate object by the Office of the Federal Service for State Registration, Cadastre and Cartography of the Donetsk People's Republic, the administration applies to the court with an application for recognition of the right of municipal ownership of the municipality to this object and the ownerless property contained within it movable things (if any) in the manner prescribed by the legislation of the Russian Federation.
