Having studied the case materials, the court comes to the following conclusion.
In accordance with paragraph 1 of Art. 225 of the Civil Code of the Russian Federation, ownerless is a thing that does not have an owner or the owner of which is unknown or, unless otherwise provided by law, the owner of which has renounced the right of ownership.
According to paragraph 1, clause 3, art. 225 of the Civil Code of the Russian Federation, ownerless real estate is accepted for registration by the body that carries out state registration of rights to real estate, upon application from the local government body in whose territory they are located.
Paragraph 2, paragraph 3, art. 225 of the Civil Code of the Russian Federation provides that after a year from the date of registration of an ownerless immovable property, and in the case of registration of a linear object, after three months from the date of registration, the body authorized to manage municipal property may apply to the court with a demand for recognition of the right of municipal ownership of this thing.
In accordance with paragraph 5 of part 2 of Article 14 of the Federal Law of July 13, 2015 N 218-FZ “On State Registration of Real Estate” (hereinafter referred to as the Registration Law), the grounds for state registration of rights are, among other things, judicial acts that have entered into legal force. Rights to real estate established by a court decision are subject to state registration in accordance with this Federal Law, that is, when the applicant applies to the rights registration authority, with an application for state registration of the right and the submission of other documents necessary for its implementation (Article 18 of the Registration Law).
In accordance with part 2 of article 4, part 7 of article 12 of the Federal Constitutional Law of October 4, 2022 No. 5-FKZ “On the admission of the Donetsk People's Republic to the Russian Federation and the formation of a new subject within the Russian Federation - the Donetsk People's Republic” (hereinafter referred to as 5-FKZ), the regulatory legal acts of the Donetsk People's Republic are valid on the territory of the Donetsk People's Republic until the end of the transition period or until the adoption of the relevant regulatory legal act of the Russian Federation and (or) the regulatory legal act of the Donetsk People's Republic.
Management of property located on the territory of the Donetsk People's Republic, which has signs of ownerlessness and is not classified as federal property, the property of a constituent entity of the Russian Federation or municipal property, is carried out by the authorities of the Donetsk People's Republic in the manner established by the Government of the Russian Federation, unless otherwise provided by 5-FKZ.
The procedure for recognizing such property as ownerless, established by the regulatory legal acts of the Donetsk People's Republic, applies until 01/01/2028, in agreement with the federal executive body performing the functions of managing state property.
In accordance with the Federal Constitutional Law of 10/04/2022 No. 5-FKZ “On the admission of the Donetsk People’s Republic to the Russian Federation and the formation of a new subject within the Russian Federation - the Donetsk People’s Republic”, Federal Law of 10/06/2003 No. 131-FZ “On the general principles of the organization of local self-government in the Russian Federation”, the Law of the Donetsk People's Republic dated 08/17/2023 No. 468-PNS "On local self-government in the Donetsk People's Republic", Law of the Donetsk People's Republic dated 03/31/2023 No. 438-PNS "On the formation of urban and municipal districts on the territory of the Donetsk People's Republic, establishing their boundaries", the Charter of the municipal formation Shakhtarsky Municipal District of the Donetsk People's Republic, adopted by the decision of the Shakhtarsky Municipal Council of the Donetsk People's Republic dated October 25, 2023 No. 21 The local government bodies on the territory of the municipal formation of the Shakhtarsky Municipal District of the Donetsk People's Republic are: Head of the municipal formation of the Shakhtarsky Municipal District of the Donetsk People's Republic; Miners Municipal Council of the Donetsk People's Republic; Administration of the Shakhtarsky Municipal District of the Donetsk People's Republic.
The local government body on the territory of the municipality of the Shakhtarsky Municipal District of the Donetsk People's Republic is, in particular, the Administration of the Shakhtarsky Municipal District of the Donetsk People's Republic, which represents the Shakhtarsky Municipal District in state, judicial and other law enforcement agencies within its competence.
According to Art. 3 of the Law of the Donetsk People's Republic "On the specifics of identifying, using and recognizing the right of municipal property of municipalities of the Donetsk People's Republic to residential premises with signs of ownerless property located on the territory of the Donetsk People's Republic" dated 03/21/2024 No. 66-RZ (hereinafter referred to as the DPR Law of 03/21/2024 No. 66-RZ), local government bodies of Donetsk The People's Republic, represented by the bodies authorized by them (hereinafter referred to as the authorized bodies), independently carry out actions to identify, record and accept residential premises that have signs of ownerless property into the municipal ownership of the municipalities of the Donetsk People's Republic, on the territory of which they are located, on the grounds provided for by law.
Article 2 of DPR Law No. 66-RZ dated March 21, 2024 establishes signs of ownerless property in relation to residential premises:
failure to pay for residential premises and utilities within one year before the date of identification of residential premises that have signs of ownerlessness;
lack of information about the registered ownership of residential premises in the Unified State Register of Real Estate;
non-use of residential premises, which, among other things, creates a threat to its safety (or an apartment building if the premises is an apartment, a room in an apartment building), safety for the life and health of other persons (including the rights holders of other premises in an apartment building if the premises is an apartment, a room in an apartment building).
In accordance with Article 10 of the Law of the DPR dated March 21, 2024 No. 66-RZ, after three months from the date of registration of a residential premises as an ownerless territorial body of the federal executive body carrying out state cadastral registration, state registration of rights, maintaining the Unified State Register of Real Estate, the authorized body applies to the court with an application for recognition of the right of municipal ownership of the municipality to this residential premises in the manner provided for by the legislation of the Russian Federation.
