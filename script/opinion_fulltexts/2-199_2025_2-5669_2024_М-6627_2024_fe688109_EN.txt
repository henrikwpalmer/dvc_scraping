The participants in the process were notified of the date and time of the hearing, but did not appear at the court hearing.
The representative of the plaintiff submitted an application to abandon the claim and terminate the proceedings on the recognition of the right of municipal ownership of ownerless real estate.
The court considers it possible to consider the petition in the absence of persons participating in the case in accordance with Art. 167 Code of Civil Procedure of the Russian Federation.
The statement of the representative of the plaintiff to abandon the claim and terminate the proceedings for recognition of the right of municipal ownership of ownerless real estate in the case is attached to the case materials.
The court explained to the participants in the process the consequences of abandoning the claim and terminating the proceedings, provided for in Art. 221 of the Code of Civil Procedure of the Russian Federation that a repeated appeal to the court on the same subject, on the same grounds between the same parties is not allowed.
Refusal of the claim does not contradict the law, does not violate anyone’s rights or interests protected by law, therefore there are grounds for its acceptance and termination of the proceedings in accordance with Part 2 of Article 39, Part 3 of Article 173 and Paragraph 4 of Article 220 of the Code of Civil Procedure of the Russian Federation.
Based on the above and guided by Art. 224-225 Code of Civil Procedure of the Russian Federation, court
