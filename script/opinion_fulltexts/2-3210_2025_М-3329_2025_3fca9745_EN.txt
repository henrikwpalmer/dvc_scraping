INSTALLED:
The administration of the Yenakievo urban district of the Donetsk People's Republic applied to the court for recognition of the right of municipal ownership of an ownerless real estate property, asking to recognize for the municipal formation of the Yenakievo urban district of the Donetsk People's Republic the right of municipal ownership of an ownerless real estate object (apartment), located at the address:
<address>
The requirements are motivated by the fact that on March 12, 2025, the Commission for the identification, registration and acceptance into municipal ownership of ownerless real estate and escheatable property in the administrative territory of the city of Yenakievo decided to recognize the above-mentioned property as ownerless, to organize a commission inspection of the thing that has signs of being ownerless with the drawing up of the corresponding act. On April 10, 2025, the property was registered as ownerless. In relation to the specified object in the Unified State Register there is no information about the rights to it.
The applicant's representative for the administration of the Yenakievo urban district of the Donetsk People's Republic did not appear at the court hearing, was notified in advance and properly about the time and place of the consideration of the case, submitted a petition to consider the case in his absence, and fully supports the stated requirements.
The representative of the interested party, the Office of the Federal Service for State Registration, Cadastre and Cartography for the Donetsk People's Republic, did not appear at the court hearing and submitted a petition to consider the case in his absence.
Interested parties Bakhilova M.M., Patsula B.N. did not appear at the court hearing, were duly notified of the time and place of the court session, were not informed of valid reasons for failure to appear, and were not asked to consider the case in their absence.
Having studied the arguments of the application and examined the written materials of the case in their entirety, the court comes to the following conclusions.
According to Part. 1 and 3 tbsp. 35 of the Constitution of the Russian Federation, the right to private property is protected by law; no one can be deprived of their property except by a court decision.
From paragraph 1 of Art. 1 of the Civil Code of the Russian Federation (hereinafter referred to as the Civil Code of the Russian Federation) it follows that civil legislation is based on the recognition of the equality of participants in the relations regulated by it, the inviolability of property, freedom of contract, the inadmissibility of arbitrary interference by anyone in private affairs, the need for the unhindered exercise of civil rights, ensuring the restoration of violated rights, and their judicial protection.
By virtue of paragraph 2 of Art. 1 of the Civil Code of the Russian Federation, citizens (individuals) and legal entities acquire and exercise their civil rights of their own will and in their own interest. They are free to establish their rights and obligations on the basis of the contract and to determine any terms of the contract that do not contradict the law.
In accordance with paragraph 1 of Art. 209 of the Civil Code of the Russian Federation, the owner has the rights to own, use and dispose of his property.
In Art. 210 of the Civil Code of the Russian Federation states that the owner bears the burden of maintaining the property he owns, unless otherwise provided by law or contract.
According to paragraph 3 of Art. 218 of the Civil Code of the Russian Federation, in cases and in the manner provided for by this Code, a person may acquire the right of ownership to property that does not have an owner, to property whose owner is unknown, or to property that the owner has abandoned or to which he has lost the right of ownership on other grounds provided by law.
An ownerless thing is a thing that does not have an owner or the owner of which is unknown or, unless otherwise provided by law, the owner of which has renounced the right of ownership, as provided for in Part 1 of Art. 225 Civil Code of the Russian Federation
As follows from Part 3 of Art. 225 of the Civil Code of the Russian Federation, ownerless real estate is accepted for registration by the body that carries out state registration of rights to real estate, upon application from the local government body in whose territory they are located. After a year has passed from the date of registration of an ownerless immovable property, and in the case of registration of a linear object, after three months from the date of registration, the body authorized to manage municipal property may apply to the court with a request to recognize the right of municipal ownership of this thing.
By virtue of the provisions of Art. 215 of the Civil Code of the Russian Federation, property owned by right of ownership to urban and rural settlements, as well as other municipal entities, is municipal property. On behalf of the municipality, the rights of the owner are exercised by local government bodies and the persons specified in Art. 125 of the Civil Code of the Russian Federation.
According to Part 1 of Art. 130 of the Civil Code of the Russian Federation, immovable things (real estate, real estate) include land plots, subsoil plots and everything that is firmly connected to the land, that is, objects whose movement without disproportionate damage to their purpose is impossible, including buildings, structures, unfinished construction objects. Real estate includes residential and non-residential premises, as well as parts of buildings or structures intended to accommodate vehicles (car spaces), if the boundaries of such premises, parts of buildings or structures are described in the manner established by the legislation on state cadastral registration.
From Part 3 of Art. 293 of the Civil Procedure Code of the Russian Federation (hereinafter - the Code of Civil Procedure of the Russian Federation) it follows that the court, having recognized that the immovable thing does not have an owner or the owner of the immovable thing is unknown and it has been registered in the prescribed manner, makes a decision to recognize the right of municipal ownership of this thing.
Thus, within the meaning of the stated legal norms, the registration of an object of real estate as ownerless, as well as the subsequent recognition of the body authorized to manage municipal property, the right of ownership of an ownerless real estate in court on the basis of Art. 225 of the Civil Code of the Russian Federation is possible only in the absence of legal claims of other persons to such property. At the same time, the absence of state registration of ownership of a disputed object cannot in itself be a basis for recognizing it as ownerless.
At the same time, in accordance with Part 7 of Article 12 of the Federal Constitutional Law of October 4, 2022 N 5-FKZ (as amended on December 25, 2023) “On the admission of the Donetsk People’s Republic into the Russian Federation and the formation of a new subject within the Russian Federation - the Donetsk People’s Republic,” management of property located on the territory of the Donetsk People’s Republic that has signs of being ownerless and not assigned to federal property, property of a constituent entity of the Russian Federation or municipal property, is carried out by the bodies of the Donetsk People's Republic in the manner established by the Government of the Russian Federation, unless otherwise provided by this Federal Constitutional Law. The procedure for recognizing such property as ownerless, established by the regulatory legal acts of the Donetsk People's Republic, is applied until January 1, 2028, in agreement with the federal executive body exercising the functions of managing state property.
In accordance with clause 1.1 of Decree of the Acting Head of the Donetsk People's Republic No. 140 dated 05/10/2023 “On the specifics of acquiring the right of state and municipal ownership of ownerless real estate on the territory of the Donetsk People's Republic” /hereinafter - Decree No. 140 dated 05/10/2023/ the body and officials of the Donetsk People's Republic exercising the powers assigned legislation of the Russian Federation to the powers of local self-government bodies, before the completion of the formation of local self-government bodies, the local government body of a municipal formation in the Donetsk People's Republic, authorized to manage municipal property, has the right to apply to the court with a demand for recognition of the right of municipal ownership of ownerless buildings, structures (except for linear objects), premises, objects of unfinished construction that do not have an owner or the owner of which is unknown or from the ownership of which the owner refused, located on the territory of such a municipal entity, after three months from the date of their registration as ownerless real estate by the body carrying out state registration of rights to real estate, unless otherwise provided by this Decree.
Clause 1.2. Decree No. 140 of May 10, 2023 provides that the executive body of the Donetsk People's Republic authorized to manage state property has the right to apply to the court with a demand for recognition of the right of state ownership of the objects defined in subclause 1.1 of this clause (with the exception of residential premises, outbuildings intended to satisfy citizens' household and other needs), after three months from the date of their registration as ownerless immovable things by the body carrying out state registration of rights to real estate.
According to Article 2 of the Law of the Donetsk People's Republic "On the specifics of identifying, using and recognizing the right of municipal property of municipalities of the Donetsk People's Republic to residential premises that have signs of ownerless property located on the territory of the Donetsk People's Republic" No. 66-RZ dated 03/21/2024 /hereinafter referred to as DPR Law No. 66-RZ dated 03/21/2024/ signs of ownerless property property in relation to residential premises are:
1) failure to pay for residential premises and utilities within one year before the day of identification of residential premises that have signs of ownerlessness;
2) lack of information about the registered ownership of residential premises in the Unified State Register of Real Estate;
3) non-use of residential premises, which, among other things, creates a threat to its safety (or an apartment building if the premises is an apartment, a room in an apartment building), safety for the life and health of other persons (including the rights holders of other premises in an apartment building if the premises is an apartment, a room in an apartment building).
According to Article 3 of the DPR Law No. 66-RZ dated March 21, 2024, local government bodies of the Donetsk People's Republic, represented by the bodies authorized by them (hereinafter referred to as the authorized bodies), independently carry out actions to identify, register and accept residential premises that have signs of ownerless property into the municipal ownership of the municipalities of the Donetsk People's Republic, on the territory of which they are located, on the grounds provided for by law.
According to Article 9 of DPR Law No. 66-RZ dated March 21, 2024, the procedure for recognizing ownerless residential premises as municipal property is terminated upon personal announcement of the owner of such premises and confirmation of ownership of such premises on the basis of documents provided for in subparagraphs “a”, “b” of paragraph 3 of Article 5 of this Law. Within 5 days from the date of confirmation of ownership of the residential premises, the authorized body makes a decision to terminate the procedure for recognizing the residential premises as municipal property and to deregister the residential premises from the register of identified ownerless real estate on the territory of the municipality. Within 3 days from the date of adoption of the decision determined by part 2 of this article, the authorized body, in the manner prescribed by law, applies to the territorial body of the federal executive body carrying out state cadastral registration, state registration of rights, maintenance of the Unified State Register of Real Estate, in order to deregister the residential premises as ownerless. The contract for ensuring the safety and maintenance of residential premises is terminated from the date of adoption of the decision provided for in Part 2 of this article, and the residential premises must be vacated within 3 days from the date of such decision. If the owner submits documents confirming the ownership of the residential premises, it is transferred to the owner by the authorized body under the acceptance and transfer act, which specifies the condition for compensation by the owner within 3 months from the date of signing the acceptance and transfer certificate of the residential premises to the authorized body for the costs incurred for repairs and maintenance of this object. If the owner of the residential premises does not reimburse the costs provided for in Part 5 of this article, they are subject to recovery in court in accordance with the law.
According to Article 10 of the DPR Law No. 66-RZ dated March 21, 2024, after three months from the date of registration of the residential premises as ownerless by the territorial body of the federal executive body carrying out state cadastral registration, state registration of rights, maintaining the Unified State Register of Real Estate, the authorized body applies to the court with an application for recognition of the right of municipal ownership of the municipality to this residential premises in the manner prescribed legislation of the Russian Federation. Recognition of ownership of ownerless movable things located in residential premises is carried out in the manner prescribed by the Law of the Donetsk People's Republic of February 13, 2024 No. 52-RZ “On the peculiarities of regulation of property rights in relation to ownerless movable things located on real estate objects located on the territory of the Donetsk People's Republic.” The right of municipal ownership of residential premises established by a court decision is subject to state registration. After the entry into force of a court decision recognizing the right of municipal ownership of a municipal entity to residential premises and the state registration of such a right, the authorized body shall take this residential premises into municipal ownership of the municipal entity in the manner determined by the local government body of the relevant municipal entity, and also carry out other measures provided for by law. The disposal of residential premises accepted into municipal ownership on the basis of a court decision is carried out in accordance with municipal acts of municipal entities of the Donetsk People's Republic.
