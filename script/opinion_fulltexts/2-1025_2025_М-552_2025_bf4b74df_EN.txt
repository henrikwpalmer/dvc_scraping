The court, having examined the written evidence in the case, analyzing them both individually and in aggregate, evaluating the presented evidence according to its internal conviction, based on a comprehensive study of the evidence, comes to the following conclusions.
According to the first and third parts of Article 1 of the Federal Constitutional Law of the Russian Federation dated October 4, 2022 No. 5-FKZ “On the admission of the Donetsk People's Republic into the Russian Federation and the formation of a new subject within the Russian Federation - the Donetsk People's Republic (hereinafter referred to as the Federal Constitutional Law of October 4, 2022 No. 5-FKZ), the Donetsk People's Republic is admitted to the Russian Federation in accordance with the Constitution of the Russian Federation and Article 4 of the Federal Constitutional Law of December 17, 2001 No. 6-FKZ “On the procedure for admission to the Russian Federation and the formation of a new subject of the Russian Federation within it.” The Donetsk People's Republic is considered accepted into the Russian Federation from the date of signing the Agreement between the Russian Federation and the Donetsk People's Republic on the admission of the Donetsk People's Republic to the Russian Federation and the formation of a new entity within the Russian Federation.
Article one of the Federal Law of the Russian Federation "On the ratification of the Treaty between the Russian Federation and the Donetsk People's Republic and the admission of the Donetsk People's Republic into the Russian Federation and the formation of a new subject within the Russian Federation" dated October 4, 2022 No. 372-FZ, provides that the Treaty between the Russian Federation and the Donetsk People's Republic and the admission of Donetsk to the Russian Federation has been ratified People's Republic and the formation of a new entity within the Russian Federation, signed on September 30, 2022, October 4, 2022. And article two of the named law shows that Federal Law No. 372-FZ of October 4, 2022 comes into force from the moment of official publication. Federal Law No. 372-FZ of October 4, 2022, published on October 5, 2022.
Part seven of Article 12 of the Federal Law No. 5-FKZ dated October 4, 2022, establishes that the management of property located on the territory of the Donetsk People's Republic, which has signs of ownerlessness and is not classified as federal property, the property of a constituent entity of the Russian Federation or municipal property, is carried out by the authorities of the Donetsk People's Republic in the manner established by the Government of the Russian Federation, unless otherwise provided by this Federal Constitutional Law. The procedure for recognizing such property as ownerless, established by the regulatory legal acts of the Donetsk People's Republic, is applied until January 1, 2028, in agreement with the federal executive body exercising the functions of managing state property.
Part one of Article 4 of the Law of the Donetsk People's Republic dated March 21, 2024 No. 66-RZ “On the specifics of identifying, using and recognizing the right of municipal ownership of municipalities of the Donetsk People's Republic to residential premises with signs of ownerless property located on the territory of the Donetsk People's Republic” (hereinafter referred to as the Law of the Donetsk People's Republic dated March 21, 2024 No. 66-RZ), it is determined that information about residential premises that have signs of ownerlessness may come from individuals and legal entities, including public authorities, who have become aware of such property.
According to the Charter of the municipal formation of the municipal formation of the Donetsk city district of the Donetsk People's Republic, adopted by the decision of the Donetsk city council of the Donetsk People's Republic dated November 09, 2023 No. I/5-1, issues of local importance of the city district include, among other things, the ownership, use and disposal of property in municipal ownership, the implementation of municipal housing control.
The procedure for recognizing such property as ownerless, established by the regulatory legal acts of the Donetsk People's Republic, applies until 01/01/2028, in agreement with the federal executive body performing the functions of managing state property.
In accordance with subparagraph 3.1 of paragraph 3 of the Decree of the Acting Head of the Donetsk People's Republic dated 02.27.2023 No. 64 “On some issues related to the management of property that has signs of ownerlessness, located on the territory of the Donetsk People's Republic, and the recognition of such property as ownerless” (hereinafter referred to as Decree No. 64), administrations of cities and districts are bodies for managing property that has signs of ownerlessness ownerless and not classified as federal property, located on the territory of the Donetsk People's Republic, in accordance with the Rules approved by the Decree of the Government of the Russian Federation of December 28, 2022 No. 2474 “On approval of the Rules for the management of bodies of the Donetsk People's Republic, Lugansk People's Republic, Zaporozhye region and Kherson region having signs of ownerlessness and not classified as federal property, property subject of the Russian Federation or municipal property with property located on the territories of the specified subjects of the Russian Federation.”
Subclause 3.2 of clause 3 of Decree No. 64 determines that until 01/01/2028, in relation to the property specified in subclause 3.1 of clause 3, the procedure for recognizing it as ownerless is applied, established by Decree of the Head of the Donetsk People's Republic of 04/28/2021 No. 116 “On identifying, recording and accepting ownerless property into municipal ownership.” real estate and escheat property" (hereinafter referred to as Standard Procedure No. 116), taking into account the changes introduced by Decree No. 64.
In accordance with the decision of the Donetsk City Council of the Donetsk People's Republic dated November 30, 2023 No. I/8-3 “On issues of legal succession in the municipal formation of the Donetsk City District of the Donetsk People's Republic”, when performing the functions of managing property that has signs of ownerlessness, the administration of the Donetsk City District performs the functions of identifying, recording and accepting ownerless things into municipal ownership in accordance with the requirements of the Standard Procedure No. 116.
