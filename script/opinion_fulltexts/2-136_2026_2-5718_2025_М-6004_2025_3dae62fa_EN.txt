The court, having examined the case materials, came to the conclusion that the demands of the Administration of the Makeyevka Urban District must be satisfied on the following grounds:
According to clause 6, part 1, art. 262 of the Code of Civil Procedure of the Russian Federation, by way of special proceedings, the court considers cases of recognition of the right of municipal ownership of an ownerless immovable property.
According to Part 1 of Art. 225 of the Civil Code of the Russian Federation, ownerless is a thing that does not have an owner or the owner of which is unknown, or, unless otherwise provided by law, the owner of which has renounced the right of ownership.
Clause 3 of Art. 218 of the Civil Code of the Russian Federation provides that a person can acquire ownership of property that does not have an owner, property whose owner is unknown, or property that the owner has abandoned or to which he has lost ownership on other grounds provided by law.

According to Article 4 of the Federal Constitutional Law of the Russian Federation of October 4, 2022 No. 5-FKZ “On the admission into the Russian Federation of the Donetsk People's Republic and the formation within the Russian Federation of a new subject - the Donetsk People's Republic”, legislative and other regulatory legal acts of the Russian Federation are valid on the territory of the Donetsk People's Republic from the date of admission to the Russian Federation of the Donetsk People's Republic and the formation of a new entity within the Russian Federation subject, unless otherwise provided by this Federal Constitutional Law.
Regulatory legal acts of the Donetsk People's Republic are valid on the territory of the Donetsk People's Republic until the end of the transition period or until the adoption of the corresponding regulatory legal act of the Russian Federation and (or) the regulatory legal act of the Donetsk People's Republic.
According to Part 7 of Article 12 of Law No. 5-FKZ, management of property located on the territory of the Donetsk People's Republic, which has signs of ownerlessness and is not classified as federal property, the property of a constituent entity of the Russian Federation or municipal property, is carried out by the authorities of the Donetsk People's Republic in the manner established by the Government of the Russian Federation, unless otherwise provided by this Federal Constitutional Law. The procedure for recognizing such property as ownerless, established by the regulatory legal acts of the Donetsk People's Republic, is applied until January 1, 2028, in agreement with the federal executive body exercising the functions of managing state property.
According to paragraph 1.2 of the Decree of the Head of the Donetsk People's Republic of May 10, 2023 No. 140, the executive body of the Donetsk People's Republic authorized to manage state property has the right to apply to the court with a demand for recognition of the right of state ownership of the objects defined in subparagraph 1.1 of this paragraph (with the exception of residential premises, outbuildings intended to satisfy citizens' household and other needs), after three months from the date their registration as ownerless real estate by the body carrying out state registration of rights to real estate.
By Decree of the Acting Head of the Donetsk People's Republic of February 27, 2023 No. 64 “On some issues related to the management of property that has signs of ownerlessness, located on the territory of the Donetsk People’s Republic, and the recognition of such property as ownerless,” the Government of the Donetsk People’s Republic is vested with the authority to establish (adopt) a procedure for recognizing as ownerless property that has signs of ownerlessness and not classified as federal property, property of a constituent entity of the Russian Federation or municipal property located on the territory of the Donetsk People's Republic, with the exception of real estate in the form of residential premises, residential buildings, and outbuildings.
In accordance with paragraph 20 of the Resolution of the Administration of the urban district of Makeyevka of the Donetsk People's Republic "On approval of the procedure for identifying, recording and accepting ownerless real estate into the ownership of the municipal formation of the urban district of Makeyevka of the Donetsk People's Republic" dated June 3, 2024 No. 355 (hereinafter referred to as Resolution No. 355) after the expiration of the period provided for by current legislation from the date of registration ownerless real estate is registered as ownerless by the territorial body of the federal executive body that carries out state accounting, state registration of rights, maintaining the Unified State Register of Real Estate, the Administration applies to the court with an application for recognition of the right of municipal ownership of the property in the manner prescribed by current legislation.
In accordance with paragraph 7 of Resolution No. 355, the Administration of the Makeyevka Urban District of the Donetsk People's Republic independently carries out actions to identify, record and accept ownerless real estate into the ownership of the municipality of the Makeyevka urban district.
In accordance with paragraph 8 of Resolution No. 355, information about real estate that has signs of ownerlessness can come from legal entities, regardless of organizational and legal forms and form of ownership, individual entrepreneurs, individuals, including public authorities, who have become aware of such property.
In accordance with paragraph 14 of Resolution No. 355, the Administration of the Makeevka urban district of the Donetsk People's Republic carried out all the prescribed measures to identify ownerless real estate that has signs of ownerlessness.
