The applicant and the interested party did not appear at the court hearing, were duly notified, and asked to consider the case in the absence of representatives, for which they submitted written statements.
In accordance with Art. 167 of the Civil Procedure Code of the Russian Federation (hereinafter referred to as the Civil Procedure Code of the Russian Federation), the court considers it possible to consider the case in the absence of the participants in the process who failed to appear.
Having examined the case materials, the court comes to the conclusion that the application must be granted for the following reasons.
According to Art. 3 of the Code of Civil Procedure of the Russian Federation, an interested person has the right, in the manner established by the legislation on civil proceedings, to apply to the court for the protection of violated or disputed rights, freedoms or legitimate interests.
In accordance with Part 2 of Art. 290 of the Code of Civil Procedure of the Russian Federation, an application for recognition of ownership of an ownerless immovable property is submitted to the court at its location by the body authorized to manage municipal property or property owned by the federal city of Moscow, St. Petersburg or Sevastopol, or by a person obliged in accordance with the law to operate linear facilities.
According to Art. 2 293 of the Code of Civil Procedure of the Russian Federation, the court, having recognized that the immovable thing does not have an owner or the owner of the immovable thing is unknown and it has been registered in the prescribed manner, makes a decision to recognize the right of municipal property or the property of the federal city of Moscow, St. Petersburg or Sevastopol or a person obliged in accordance with the law to operate linear objects to this thing.
By virtue of Art. 225 of the Civil Code of the Russian Federation (hereinafter referred to as the Civil Code of the Russian Federation), ownerless is a thing that does not have an owner or the owner of which is unknown or, unless otherwise provided by law, the owner of which has renounced the right of ownership (clause 1).
Ownerless real estate is accepted for registration by the body carrying out state registration of rights to real estate, upon application from the local government body in whose territory they are located (clause 2).
Clause 3 of Art. 218 of the Civil Code of the Russian Federation establishes that in cases and in the manner provided for by the said Code, a person can acquire ownership of property that does not have an owner, of property whose owner is unknown, or of property that the owner has refused or for which he has lost ownership on other grounds provided by law.
According to Art. 1 of the Federal Constitutional Law of October 4, 2022 No. 5-FKZ “On the admission of the Donetsk People’s Republic to the Russian Federation and the formation of a new subject within the Russian Federation - the Donetsk People’s Republic” The Donetsk People’s Republic is admitted to the Russian Federation in accordance with the Constitution of the Russian Federation and Article 4 of the Federal Constitutional Law of December 17, 2001 No. 6-FKZ “On the Procedure admission to the Russian Federation and the formation within it of a new subject of the Russian Federation.”
Article 4 of the above Federal Constitutional Law determines that legislative and other regulatory legal acts of the Russian Federation are in force on the territory of the Donetsk People's Republic from the date of admission of the Donetsk People's Republic to the Russian Federation and the formation of a new subject within the Russian Federation, unless otherwise provided by this Federal Constitutional Law (Part 1).
Regulatory legal acts of the Donetsk People's Republic are valid on the territory of the Donetsk People's Republic until the end of the transition period or until the adoption of the corresponding regulatory legal act of the Russian Federation and (or) the regulatory legal act of the Donetsk People's Republic (part 2).
According to Part 7 of Art. 12 of the above Federal Constitutional Law, management of property located on the territory of the Donetsk People's Republic, which has signs of ownerlessness and is not classified as federal property, the property of a constituent entity of the Russian Federation or municipal property, is carried out by the authorities of the Donetsk People's Republic in the manner established by the Government of the Russian Federation, unless otherwise provided by this Federal Constitutional Law. The procedure for recognizing such property as ownerless, established by the regulatory legal acts of the Donetsk People's Republic, is applied until January 1, 2028, in agreement with the federal executive body exercising the functions of managing state property.
By Decree of the Acting Head of the Donetsk People's Republic dated 02.27.2023 No. 64 “On some issues related to the management of property that has signs of ownerlessness, located on the territory of the Donetsk People’s Republic, and the recognition of such property as ownerless,” the Government of the Donetsk People’s Republic is vested with the authority to establish (adopt) a procedure for recognizing as ownerless property that has signs of ownerlessness and not classified as federal property, property of a constituent entity of the Russian Federation or municipal property located on the territory of the Donetsk People's Republic, with the exception of real estate in the form of residential premises, residential buildings, and outbuildings.
In accordance with Art. 10 Law of the Donetsk People's Republic dated March 22, 2024 No. 66-RZ “On the specifics of identifying, using and recognizing the right of municipal ownership of municipalities of the Donetsk People's Republic to residential premises that have signs of ownerless property located on the territory of the Donetsk People's Republic” after three months from the date of designation of the residential premises as ownerless by the territorial body of the federal executive body carrying out state cadastral registration, state registration of rights, maintenance of the Unified State Register of Real Estate, the authorized body applies to the court with an application for recognition of the right of municipal property of a municipal entity to this residential premises in the manner prescribed by the legislation of the Russian Federation.
