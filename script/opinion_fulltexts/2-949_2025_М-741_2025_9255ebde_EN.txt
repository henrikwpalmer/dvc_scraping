The court, having listened to the applicant, studied the case materials and, based on its internal conviction, based on a comprehensive, complete and direct examination of the evidence available in the case, the relevance, admissibility, reliability of each evidence separately, as well as the sufficiency and interconnection of the evidence in their totality, comes to the following.
According to paragraph 1 of Art. 225 of the Civil Code of the Russian Federation, ownerless is a thing that does not have an owner or the owner of which is unknown or, unless otherwise provided by law, the owner of which has renounced the right of ownership.
In accordance with Art. 226 of the Civil Code of the Russian Federation, movable things abandoned by the owner or otherwise left by him for the purpose of renouncing the ownership of them (abandoned things) can be turned into their property by other persons if, at the request of this person, they are recognized by the court as ownerless.
Part 3 of Art. 218 of the Civil Code of the Russian Federation establishes that in cases and in the manner provided for by the Civil Code of the Russian Federation, a person can acquire ownership of property that does not have an owner, of property whose owner is unknown, or of property that the owner has abandoned or for which he has lost ownership on other grounds provided by law.
Based on Part 1 of Art. 293 of the Code of Civil Procedure of the Russian Federation, the court, having recognized that the owner has renounced the right of ownership of the movable thing, makes a decision to recognize the movable thing as ownerless and transfer it to the ownership of the person who has taken possession of it.
In accordance with Articles 212, 214 of the Civil Code of the Russian Federation, it is a subject of property rights on an equal basis with citizens, legal entities, constituent entities of the Russian Federation and municipalities.
According to Part 1 of Art. 290 of the Code of Civil Procedure of the Russian Federation, an application to recognize a movable thing as ownerless is submitted to the court at the location of the thing.
According to paragraph 1 of Art. 6 of the Federal Law of June 13, 2023 No. 203 “On state regulation of the production and circulation of tobacco products, tobacco products, nicotine-containing products and raw materials for their production” on the territory of the Russian Federation is not allowed: the introduction and circulation of tobacco products and nicotine-containing products subject to marking with identification means, without identification means applied to such products and) or) without providing information about the marking with identification means to the monitoring information system; production and (or) circulation of tobacco products, tobacco products, raw materials, nicotine-containing products and nicotine raw materials subject to licensing without an appropriate license; purchase and (or) supply of tobacco products and nicotine-containing products by a person not registered in the monitoring information system.
Seized tobacco products that are in illegal circulation are subject to destruction by a court decision in accordance with the Rules approved by Decree of the Government of the Russian Federation of April 23, 2024 No. 528 “On the implementation of measures to suppress the illegal production and (or) circulation of tobacco products, tobacco products, nicotine-containing products and raw materials for their production.”
