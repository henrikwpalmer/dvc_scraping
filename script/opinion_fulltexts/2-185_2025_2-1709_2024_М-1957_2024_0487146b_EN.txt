In accordance with Part 1 of Article 1 of the Federal Constitutional Law of October 4, 2022 No. 5-FKZ “On the admission of the Donetsk People's Republic to the Russian Federation and the formation of a new subject within the Russian Federation - the Donetsk People's Republic”, the Donetsk People's Republic is admitted to the Russian Federation.
By virtue of Part 7 of Article 12 of the Federal Constitutional Law of October 4, 2022 No. 5-FKZ “On the admission of the Donetsk People's Republic into the Russian Federation and the formation of a new subject within the Russian Federation - the Donetsk People's Republic”, management of property located on the territory of the Donetsk People's Republic, which has signs of ownerlessness and is not classified as federal property, the property of a subject of the Russian Federation or municipal property, is carried out by the authorities of the Donetsk of the People's Republic in the manner established by the Government of the Russian Federation, unless otherwise provided by this Federal Constitutional Law. The procedure for recognizing such property as ownerless, established by the regulatory legal acts of the Donetsk People's Republic, is applied until January 1, 2028, in agreement with the federal executive body exercising the functions of managing state property.
In accordance with paragraph 6 of part 1 of Article 262 of the Code of Civil Procedure of the Russian Federation, cases considered by a court of general jurisdiction in special proceedings include cases of recognition of a movable thing as ownerless and recognition of the right of municipal ownership of an ownerless immovable thing.
In paragraph 59 of the Resolution of the Plenum of the Supreme Court of the Russian Federation No. 10, the Plenum of the Supreme Arbitration Court of the Russian Federation No. 22 of April 29, 2010 “On some issues arising in judicial practice when resolving disputes related to the protection of property rights and other property rights” it is explained that, unless otherwise provided by law, a claim for recognition of a right is subject to satisfaction if the plaintiff provides evidence of the emergence of the corresponding right.
According to paragraph 3 of Article 218 of the Civil Code of the Russian Federation, in cases and in the manner provided for by this Code, a person may acquire ownership of property that does not have an owner, property whose owner is unknown, or property that the owner has abandoned or to which he has lost ownership on other grounds provided by law.
In accordance with Article 219 of the Civil Code of the Russian Federation, ownership of buildings, structures and other newly created real estate, subject to state registration, arises from the moment of such registration.
Paragraph 1 of Article 225 of the Civil Code of the Russian Federation states that an ownerless thing is a thing that does not have an owner or the owner of which is unknown or, unless otherwise provided by law, the right of ownership to which the owner has renounced.
Ownerless real estate is accepted for registration by the body that carries out state registration of rights to real estate, upon application from the local government body in whose territory they are located. After a year has passed from the date of registration of an ownerless immovable property, the body authorized to manage municipal property may apply to the court with a request to recognize the right of municipal ownership of this thing (clause 3 of Article 225 of the Civil Code of the Russian Federation).
The procedure for recognizing the ownership of an ownerless immovable property by the body authorized to manage municipal property is defined in Chapter 33 of the Civil Procedure Code of the Russian Federation (hereinafter referred to as the Code of Civil Procedure of the Russian Federation).
Based on Part 2 of Article 293 of the Code of Civil Procedure of the Russian Federation, the court, having recognized that the immovable thing does not have an owner or the owner of the immovable thing is unknown, and it was registered in the prescribed manner, makes a decision to recognize the right of municipal property or the property of the federal city of Moscow or St. Petersburg to this thing.
By virtue of paragraph 2 of Article 1 of the Law of the DPR of March 21, 2024. No. 66-RZ “On the peculiarities of identifying, using and recognizing the right of municipal property of municipalities of the Donetsk People’s Republic to residential premises that have signs of ownerless property located on the territory of the Donetsk People’s Republic”, this law applies to residential premises in the form of residential buildings, apartments, rooms.
By virtue of Art. 2 of the DPR Law of March 21, 2024 No. 66-RZ “On the peculiarities of identifying, using and recognizing the right of municipal property of municipalities of the Donetsk People's Republic to residential premises that have signs of ownerless property located on the territory of the Donetsk People's Republic” signs of ownerless property in relation to residential premises are: - failure to pay for residential premises and utilities within one year before the day of identification of a residential premises that has signs of ownerlessness; - lack of information about the registered ownership of residential premises in the Unified State Register of Real Estate; - non-use of residential premises, which, among other things, creates a threat to its safety (or an apartment building if the premises is an apartment, a room in an apartment building), safety for the life and health of other persons (including the rights holders of other premises in an apartment building if the premises is an apartment, a room in an apartment building).
In accordance with paragraph 2 of the Decree of the Head of the Donetsk People's Republic dated April 28, 2021. No. 116 “On the identification, registration and acceptance into municipal ownership of ownerless real estate and escheat property” by order of the administration of Mariupol dated December 5, 2023. No. 766 approved the procedure for identifying, recording and accepting into municipal ownership ownerless real estate and escheat property in the administrative territory of the municipality of Mariupol.
By virtue of clause 2.2 of the procedure, the Administration of the city of Mariupol (hereinafter referred to as the administration) independently carries out actions to identify, record and accept ownerless real estate into the municipal ownership of the administration of the city of Mariupol, located on the administrative territory of the municipality of the city of Mariupol.
Clause 2.3 of the procedure Information about immovable things that have signs of being ownerless can come from legal entities and individuals who have become aware of property that does not have an owner or the owner of which is unknown, or the ownership of which the owner has refused, including by ceasing to bear the burden of maintaining such property (in particular, through non-payment of utilities for the last three years), provided that the owner has been outside the territory of the Russian Federation for the last six months.
clause 2.4 of the procedure for the applicants specified in clause 2.3. of this Procedure, in any form (on paper or electronic media), requests are sent to the administration regarding identified real estate items that have signs of being ownerless.
subp. “a” clause 9 of the procedure The administration, within 10 calendar days from the date of discovery of an object of real estate that has signs of ownerlessness, places on the official website of the administration on the Internet an announcement about the identification of a real estate property on the administrative territory of the municipality of the city of Mariupol that has signs of ownerlessness, and about the need for the appearance of a person (persons) who considers themselves its owner or has (have) rights to it, contact administration with documents excluding the possibility of considering the specified real estate object ownerless. In case of failure of the summoned person(s), the specified immovable thing will, upon application of the administration, be registered with the department of the Federal Service for State Registration, Cadastre and Cartography in the Donetsk People's Republic (Rosreestr Department in the Donetsk People's Republic), which carries out state registration of rights, maintaining the Unified State Register of Real Estate, real rights to real estate and their encumbrances, as ownerless.
subp. “a” clause 11 of the procedure Administration after 30 calendar days from the date of publication of the announcement in the event of the failure of the person (persons) who consider themselves to be the owner of the immovable thing, and after receipt from the authorities specified in subclause 2.9. of this Procedure, answers indicating the absence of the owner of an immovable property that has signs of ownerlessness, or that he is located outside the territory of the Russian Federation, the administration: Within 5 working days, draws up a report on the impossibility of identifying the owner of a real estate property that has signs of ownerlessness or escheat in the administrative territory of the municipality of the city of Mariupol (Appendix 3 to the Procedure), or about his location outside the territory of the Russian Federation and failure to ensure the maintenance of such immovable property.
Clause 3.1 of the procedure, after three months from the date of registration of an ownerless immovable property, the administration is obliged to apply to the court with a claim for recognition of the right of municipal ownership of this thing.
