considers the stated requirements to be justified and subject to satisfaction, for the following reasons.
According to Art. 12 of the Civil Code of the Russian Federation, the protection of civil rights is carried out through the recognition of rights.
As provided in paragraph 3 of Art. 218 of the Civil Code of the Russian Federation, in cases and in the manner provided for by this Code, a person may acquire the right of ownership to property that does not have an owner, to property whose owner is unknown, or to property that the owner has abandoned or to which he has lost the right of ownership on other grounds provided by law.
In accordance with Part 1 of Article 225 of the Civil Code of the Russian Federation, an ownerless thing is a thing that does not have an owner, the owner of which is unknown, or the right of ownership to which the owner has refused.
Based on the provisions of Part 3 of Article 225 of the Civil Code of the Russian Federation, ownerless real estate is accepted for registration by the body that carries out state registration of rights to real estate, upon application from the local government body in whose territory they are located. After a year has passed from the date of registration of an ownerless immovable property, and in the case of registration of a linear object, after three months from the date of registration, the body authorized to manage municipal property may apply to the court with a request to recognize the right of municipal ownership of this thing.
Thus, in order to recognize the right of municipal ownership of an ownerless immovable property in a special manner, a set of mandatory conditions listed above is necessary.
Part 1 of Art. 292 of the Civil Procedure Code of the Russian Federation stipulates that when preparing a case for recognition of ownership of an ownerless immovable property, the court must find out which persons (owners, actual owners and others) can provide information about the ownership of the property, and also request relevant organizations about the information available about it.
According to Article 130 of the Civil Code of the Russian Federation, immovable things (real estate, real estate) include land plots, subsoil plots and everything that is firmly connected to the land, that is, objects whose movement without disproportionate damage to their purpose is impossible, including buildings, structures, unfinished construction projects. Real estate includes residential and non-residential premises, as well as parts of buildings or structures intended to accommodate vehicles (car spaces), if the boundaries of such premises, parts of buildings or structures are described in the manner established by the legislation on state cadastral registration. Items not related to real estate, including money and securities, are recognized as movable property. Registration of rights to movable things is not required, except in cases specified in the law.
Recognition of ownership of an ownerless immovable property is carried out in a special proceeding upon the application of the body authorized to manage municipal property, in accordance with Articles 264-293 of the Civil Procedure Code of the Russian Federation.
In accordance with Part 1 of Article 4 of the Federal Constitutional Law No. 5-FKZ of October 4, 2022 “On the admission of the Donetsk People's Republic into the Russian Federation and the formation of a new subject within the Russian Federation - the Donetsk People's Republic” (hereinafter referred to as Law No. 5-FKZ), legislative and other regulatory legal acts of the Russian Federation are in force on the territory of the Donetsk People's Republic from the date of admission of the Donetsk People's Republic to the Russian Federation of the People's Republic and the formation of a new subject within the Russian Federation, unless otherwise provided by Law No. 5-FKZ.
According to Part 2 of Article 4 of Law No. 5-FKZ, the regulatory legal acts of the Donetsk People's Republic are valid on the territory of the Donetsk People's Republic until the end of the transition period or until the adoption of the relevant regulatory legal act of the Russian Federation and (or) the regulatory legal act of the Donetsk People's Republic, while Article 21 of Law No. 5-FKZ establishes the specifics of regulating relations in the sphere of real estate turnover on the territory of the Donetsk People's Republic.
In accordance with paragraph 2 of part 1 of Article 21 of Law No. 5-FKZ, until January 1, 2028, on the territory of the Donetsk People's Republic, regulatory legal acts of the Donetsk People's Republic may establish features for regulating property relations, including land turnover, in agreement with the federal executive body performing the functions of managing state property.
By virtue of Part 7 of Article 12 of Law No. 5-FKZ, the procedure for recognizing property that is not classified as federal property, the property of a constituent entity of the Russian Federation or municipal property as ownerless, established by the regulatory legal acts of the Donetsk People's Republic, is applied until January 1, 2028, in agreement with the federal executive body performing the functions of managing state property.
Decree of the Acting Head of the Donetsk People's Republic dated 05/10/2023 No. 140 (hereinafter referred to as Decree No. 140 dated 05/10/2023) established the specifics of acquiring state and municipal property rights to ownerless real estate on the territory of the Donetsk People's Republic.
According to clause 1.1 of Decree No. 140 dated 10.05.2023, the body and officials of the Donetsk People's Republic exercising powers classified by the legislation of the Russian Federation as the powers of local government bodies, before the completion of the formation of local government bodies, the local government body of the municipal formation in the Donetsk People's Republic, authorized to manage municipal property, has the right to apply to the court with a demand for recognition of the right of municipal ownership of ownerless buildings, structures (except for linear objects), premises, objects of unfinished construction that do not have an owner or the owner of which is unknown or the owner of which has refused ownership, located on the territory of such a municipal entity, after three months from the date of their registration as ownerless real estate by the body carrying out state registration of rights to real estate, unless otherwise provided by this Decree.
In accordance with paragraph 2.12 of the Decree of the Head of the Donetsk People's Republic of April 28, 2021 No. 116 “On the identification, registration and acceptance into municipal ownership of ownerless real estate and escheat property”, in order to register an immovable object as ownerless, the administration issues a corresponding order and applies to the territorial body of the federal executive body that carries out state cadastral registration, state registration of rights, maintenance of the Unified State Register of Real Estate, with an application for registration of an immovable property as ownerless and other documents provided for by current legislation.
