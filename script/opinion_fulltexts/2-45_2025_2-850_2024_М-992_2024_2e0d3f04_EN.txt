U S T A N O V I L:
The administration of the Amvrosievsky municipal district of the Donetsk People's Republic applied to the district court with an application for recognition of the right of municipal ownership of ownerless property (the interested party is the Office of the Federal Service for State Registration, Cadastre and Cartography for the Donetsk People's Republic), in which it asks the court to recognize the right of municipal ownership of the municipal formation of the Amvrosievsky municipal district of the Donetsk People's Republic for ownerless real estate - an apartment located at the address: Russian Federation, Donetsk People's Republic, Amvrosievsky municipal district,
<address>
, cadastral

.
On January 21, 2025, the court received a statement from the representative of the applicant of the administration of the Amvrosievsky municipal district, O.I. Podlesnaya, acting by proxy, in which the applicant renounces the stated claims in full, asks to accept the waiver of the claim, indicating that the consequences of abandonment of the claim, provided for in Article 173 of the Code of Civil Procedure of the Russian Federation, are known and understandable to her.
The persons participating in the case, having been duly notified, did not appear at the court hearing, did not file motions to postpone the case, the case was considered in their absence in accordance with Article 167 of the Code of Civil Procedure of the Russian Federation.
In accordance with Article 39, 173 of the Code of Civil Procedure of the Russian Federation, the plaintiff has the right to abandon the claim, and the court, having determined that such a refusal does not contradict the law and does not violate the rights and legitimate interests of other persons, accepts it and, on the basis of Article 220 of the Code of Civil Procedure of the Russian Federation, terminates the proceedings.
Having studied the case materials, the court comes to the conclusion that the proceedings in the case are terminated due to the provisions of Article 220 of the Code of Civil Procedure of the Russian Federation, since the refusal of the stated demands does not contradict the law and does not infringe on the rights and legitimate interests of other persons, the court considers it possible to accept the refusal of the representative of the applicant of the administration of the Amvrosievsky municipal district O.I. Podlesnoy. from the stated demands and terminate the proceedings.
Guided by Articles 220, 221, 224-225 of the Code of Civil Procedure of the Russian Federation,
O P R E D E L I L:
accept the refusal of the representative of the applicant of the administration of the Amvrosievsky municipal district of the Donetsk People's Republic Podlesnaya O.I. from the stated requirements for recognition of the right of municipal ownership of ownerless real estate.
