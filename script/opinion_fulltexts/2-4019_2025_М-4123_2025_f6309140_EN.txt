According to Part 3 of Article 218 of the Civil Code of the Russian Federation, in cases and in the manner provided for by this Code, a person may acquire ownership of property that does not have an owner, property whose owner is unknown, or property that the owner has abandoned or to which he has lost ownership on other grounds provided by law.
In accordance with Art. 225 of the Civil Code of the Russian Federation, an ownerless thing is a thing that does not have an owner or the owner of which is unknown, or, unless otherwise provided by law, the right of ownership to which the owner has refused.
In accordance with Part 3 of Art. 225 of the Civil Code of the Russian Federation, ownerless real estate is accepted for registration by the body that carries out state registration of rights to real estate, upon application from the local government body in whose territory they are located.
By virtue of the provisions of Art. 236 of the Civil Code of the Russian Federation, a citizen or legal entity may renounce the right of ownership of property belonging to him by declaring this or taking other actions that definitely indicate his removal from ownership, use and disposal of property without the intention of retaining any rights to this property.
In accordance with Part 1 of Article 4 of the Federal Constitutional Law of October 4, 2022 No. 5-FKZ “On the admission of the Donetsk People's Republic into the Russian Federation and the formation of a new subject within the Russian Federation - the Donetsk People's Republic”, legislative and other regulatory legal acts of the Russian Federation are in force on the territory of the Donetsk People's Republic from the date of admission of the Donetsk People's Republic into the Russian Federation and the formation of a new entity within the Russian Federation subject, unless otherwise provided by this Federal Constitutional Law.
According to Part 2 of Art. 4 of Law No. 5-FKZ, the regulatory legal acts of the Donetsk People's Republic are valid on the territory of the Donetsk People's Republic until the end of the transition period or until the adoption of the relevant regulatory legal act of the Russian Federation and (or) the regulatory legal act of the Donetsk People's Republic.
At the same time, Art. 21 of Law No. 5-FKZ establishes the specifics of regulating relations in the sphere of real estate turnover on the territory of the Donetsk People's Republic.
In accordance with clause 2, part 1, art. 21 of Law No. 5-FKZ, until January 01, 2028, on the territory of the Donetsk People's Republic, regulatory legal acts of the Donetsk People's Republic may establish features of the regulation of property relations, including the circulation of land, in agreement with the federal executive body performing the functions of managing state property.
By virtue of Part 7 of Art. 12 of Law No. 5-FKZ, the procedure for recognizing property that is not classified as federal property, the property of a constituent entity of the Russian Federation or municipal property as ownerless, established by the regulatory legal acts of the Donetsk People's Republic, is applied until January 1, 2028 in agreement with the federal executive body performing the functions of managing state property.
In pursuance of the above federal constitutional law, on May 10, 2023, the Acting Head of the Donetsk People's Republic signed Decree No. 140 “On the specifics of acquiring the right of state and municipal ownership of ownerless real estate on the territory of the Donetsk People’s Republic,” according to which the executive body of the Donetsk People’s Republic authorized to manage state property has the right to go to court with a demand for recognition of the right of state ownership of ownerless buildings according to within three months from the date of their registration as ownerless real estate by the body carrying out state registration of rights to real estate. The executive body of the Donetsk People's Republic authorized to manage state property is the State Property Fund of the Donetsk People's Republic (clause 1.2 of the Decree).
By Decree of the Acting Head of the Donetsk People's Republic of February 27, 2023 No. 64 “On some issues related to the management of property that has signs of ownerlessness, located on the territory of the Donetsk People's Republic, and the recognition of such property as ownerless” (hereinafter referred to as Decree No. 64), the Government of the Donetsk People's Republic is vested with the authority to establish (adopt) a procedure for recognizing property as ownerless, having signs of being ownerless and not classified as federal property, property of a constituent entity of the Russian Federation or municipal property located on the territory of the Donetsk People's Republic, with the exception of real estate in the form of residential premises, residential buildings, and outbuildings.
As part of the implementation of Decree No. 140, as well as Decree No. 64, by Decree of the Government of the Donetsk People's Republic of June 22, 2023 No. 45-5, the executive body of the Donetsk People's Republic authorized to manage state real estate established the State Property Fund of the Donetsk People's Republic and approved the Procedure for recognizing real estate located on the territory of the Donetsk People's Republic as ownerless, with the exception of residential houses, residential premises, garden houses and outbuildings intended to satisfy citizens' household and other needs (hereinafter referred to as the Procedure), which regulates legal relations and establishes the procedure for recognizing property on the territory of the Donetsk People's Republic as ownerless, as well as recognizing the right of state ownership of the Donetsk People's Republic to ownerless real estate.
Clause 7 of the Procedure establishes that no later than the next working day after receiving an application for the identification of an ownerless immovable property from the body that identified the object, the State Property Fund of the Donetsk People's Republic sends to the executive body of the Donetsk People's Republic implementing state policy in the field of mass communications information about the identified ownerless immovable property, with the aim of posting this information on the official website of the Donetsk People's Republic in the information - telecommunications network "Internet" to identify and inform property owners.
From the content of these norms it follows that the current legislation provides for the possibility of terminating the right of ownership of a particular property by the owner performing actions indicating his renunciation of his property right.
According to Art. 290 of the Code of Civil Procedure of the Russian Federation, an application to recognize a movable thing as ownerless is submitted to the court by the person who took possession of it at the place of residence or location of the applicant. An application for recognition of ownership of an ownerless immovable property is submitted to the court at its location by the body authorized to manage municipal property or property owned by the federal city of Moscow, St. Petersburg or Sevastopol.
According to Part 1 of Article 291 of the Code of Civil Procedure of the Russian Federation, an application to recognize a movable thing as ownerless must indicate which movable thing is subject to recognition as ownerless, its main features must be described, as well as provide evidence indicating that the owner has renounced ownership of it, and evidence indicating that the applicant has taken possession of this thing.
According to Part 4 of Art. 1 of Federal Law No. 218-FZ of July 13, 2015 “On State Registration of Real Estate”, state registration of rights is carried out by making a record in the Unified State Register of Real Estate of the right to real estate, information about which is included in the Unified State Register of Real Estate. State registration of the transfer of the right to a real estate object, restriction of the right to a real estate object, encumbrance of a real estate object or a transaction with a real estate object is carried out subject to the existence of state registration of the right to this object in the Unified State Register of Real Estate, unless otherwise established by federal law.
The grounds for the implementation of state cadastral registration and (or) state registration of rights are, in particular, acts issued by state authorities or local governments within the framework of their competence and in the manner established by the legislation in force at the place of publication of such acts at the time of their publication, and establishing the existence, emergence, transfer, termination of a right or restriction of a right and encumbrance of a property (clause 1, part 2, article 14 of Law 218-FZ dated July 13, 2015).
According to the Procedure for registering ownerless real estate, established by order of Rosreestr No. P/0086 dated March 15, 2023, registration of ownerless real estate is carried out by the federal executive body, which carries out the functions of developing state policy and legal regulation in the field of state cadastral registration of real estate and state registration of rights to real estate and transactions with it, its territorial bodies, executive authorities of the Republic of Crimea and the federal city of Sevastopol when transferring to them the powers of the rights registration body by resolutions of the Government of the Russian Federation in accordance with Part 8 of Art. 72 of Federal Law No. 218-FZ (hereinafter referred to as the rights registration authority). Buildings (structures), structures, premises, parking spaces, objects of unfinished construction (hereinafter referred to as real estate objects), information about which is included in the Unified State Register of Real Estate (hereinafter referred to as the Unified State Register of Real Estate) and which do not have owners or whose owners are unknown, or, unless otherwise provided by law, the owners of which have renounced their ownership rights, are accepted for registration. The decision to register a real estate property as ownerless is made no later than fifteen working days from the date of receipt of the application and necessary documents by the rights registration authority. Registration of a real estate property as ownerless is carried out by entering the relevant information into the Unified State Register of Real Estate in accordance with the procedure for maintaining the Unified State Register of Real Estate, approved in accordance with Part 7 of Art. 7 of Federal Law No. 218-FZ (clauses 2, 3, 12, 13).
