Having studied the case materials, the court finds the stated demands to be satisfied for the following reasons.
On November 10, 2023, by decision of the Ilovaisk City Council of the Donetsk People's Republic No. 25, the Regulations on the Administration of the Ilovaisk Urban District of the Donetsk People's Republic were approved, and according to clause 2, clause 2.4, the administration has the authority to own, use and dispose of property that is municipally owned by the Ilovaisk Urban District.
On November 30, 2023, the Ilovaisk City Council of the Donetsk People's Republic adopted Decision No. 39 on issues of legal succession in the municipal formation of the Ilovaisk urban district of the Donetsk People's Republic, which determined that the legal successor of the property rights and obligations of the bodies exercising on the territory of the Donetsk People's Republic on the day of the admission of the Donetsk People's Republic to the Russian Federation and the formation of a new subject within the Russian Federation the powers assigned According to the legislation of the Russian Federation, the authorities of local governments are the administration of the city. Ilovaisk in terms of property necessary to support its activities, as well as other property that is in the municipal ownership of the municipality (treasury, property transferred to operational management, economic management)
Part 1 of Article 215 of the Civil Code of the Russian Federation establishes that property owned by right of ownership to urban and rural settlements, as well as other municipal entities, is municipal property.
Part 2 of Article 215 of the Civil Code of the Russian Federation establishes that on behalf of the municipality, the rights of the owner are exercised by local government bodies and the persons specified in Article 125 of this Code.
According to paragraph 3 of Article 218 of the Civil Code of the Russian Federation, in cases and in the manner provided for by the Civil Code of the Russian Federation, a person can acquire ownership of property that does not have an owner, property whose owner is unknown, or property that the owner has abandoned or to which he has lost ownership on other grounds provided by law.
In accordance with clause 7. Article 12 of the Federal Constitutional Law of October 4, 2022 N 5-FKZ (as amended on December 25, 2023) "On the admission of the Donetsk People's Republic to the Russian Federation and the formation of a new subject within the Russian Federation - the Donetsk People's Republic", management of property located on the territory of the Donetsk People's Republic, which has signs of ownerlessness and is not classified as federal property, the property of a Russian subject Federation or municipal property, is carried out by the bodies of the Donetsk People's Republic in the manner established by the Government of the Russian Federation, unless otherwise provided by this Federal Constitutional Law. The procedure for recognizing such property as ownerless, established by the regulatory legal acts of the Donetsk People's Republic, is applied until January 1, 2028, in agreement with the federal executive body exercising the functions of managing state property.
Clause 1.1 of the Decree of the Acting Head of the Donetsk People's Republic No. 140 dated May 10, 2023 “On the specifics of acquiring the right of state and municipal property to ownerless real estate on the territory of the Donetsk People’s Republic” established that the body and officials of the Donetsk People’s Republic exercising the powers classified by the legislation of the Russian Federation as the powers of local self-government bodies, until the completion of the formation of local authorities self-government, a local government body of a municipal formation in the Donetsk People's Republic, authorized to manage municipal property, has the right to go to court with a demand for recognition of the right of municipal ownership of ownerless buildings, structures (except for linear objects), premises, objects of unfinished construction that do not have an owner or the owner of which is unknown or the owner of which has refused ownership, located on the territory of such a municipal formation, after three months from the date their registration as ownerless real estate by the body carrying out state registration of rights to real estate, unless otherwise provided by this Decree.
Clause 1.2. Decree of the Acting Head of the Donetsk People's Republic No. 140 of May 10, 2023 “On the peculiarities of acquiring the right of state and municipal ownership of ownerless real estate on the territory of the Donetsk People's Republic”, the executive body of the Donetsk People's Republic authorized to manage state property has the right to apply to the court with a demand for recognition of the right of state ownership of objects defined in subclause 1.1 of this clause (with the exception of residential premises, outbuildings intended to satisfy citizens' household and other needs), after three months from the date of their registration as ownerless real estate by the body carrying out state registration of rights to real estate.
In accordance with clause 3.1. Clause 3 of the Decree of the Acting Head of the Donetsk People's Republic of February 27, 2023 No. 64 “On some issues related to the management of property that has signs of ownerlessness, located on the territory of the Donetsk People’s Republic, and the recognition of such property as ownerless,” Administrations of districts and cities of the Donetsk People’s Republic are bodies for managing property that has signs of ownerlessness and is not classified as federal property, property Donetsk People's Republic or municipal property located on the territory of the Donetsk People's Republic, in accordance with the Rules regarding real estate in the form of residential premises, residential buildings, outbuildings.
In accordance with clause 3.2. Decree of the Acting Head of the Donetsk People's Republic of February 27, 2023 No. 64 “On some issues related to the management of property that has signs of ownerlessness, located on the territory of the Donetsk People’s Republic, and the recognition of such property as ownerless,” until January 1, 2028, in relation to the property specified in subclause 3.1 of this paragraph, the procedure for recognizing it as ownerless, established Decree of the Head of the Donetsk People's Republic of April 28, 2021 No. 116 “On the identification, recording and acceptance into municipal ownership of ownerless real estate and escheat property” (hereinafter referred to as Decree No. 116), taking into account the changes made by this Decree.
Decree of the Head of the Donetsk People's Republic dated April 28, 2021 No. 116 approved the Standard Procedure for identifying, recording and accepting into municipal ownership ownerless real estate and escheat property, which defines a unified procedure for identifying, registering, storing and accepting into municipal ownership ownerless real estate and escheat property identified on the territory of the corresponding administrative-territorial unit of the Donetsk People's Republic.
According to clause 1.2. The standard procedure applies to:
1.2.1. Residential premises, residential buildings, outbuildings and outbuildings that have signs of ownerless things (hereinafter referred to as ownerless real estate).
1.2.2. Escheatable property in the form of residential premises, land plots, as well as buildings, structures, and other real estate objects located on them, shares in the right of common shared ownership of the real estate objects specified in this subclause.
Clause 1.3.2 of the standard procedure establishes that immovable things (real estate, real estate) are land plots, subsoil plots and everything that is firmly connected to the land, that is, objects whose movement without disproportionate damage to their purpose is impossible, including buildings, structures, unfinished construction objects. Real estate includes residential and non-residential premises, as well as parts of buildings or structures intended to accommodate vehicles (car spaces), if the boundaries of such premises, parts of buildings or structures are described in the manner prescribed by law.

In accordance with clause 1 of Article 225 of the Civil Code of the Russian Federation, an ownerless thing is a thing that does not have an owner or the owner of which is unknown or, unless otherwise provided by law, the ownership of which the owner has refused.
By virtue of Part 1 of Article 130 of the Civil Code of the Russian Federation, immovable things (real estate, real estate) include land plots, subsoil plots and everything that is firmly connected to the land, that is, objects whose movement without disproportionate damage to their purpose is impossible, including buildings, structures, unfinished construction objects.
Thus, the court found that the above-mentioned real estate object does not have an owner; more than three months have passed since the moment of registration as an ownerless real estate object, and no one has declared their rights to it.
By virtue of Article 12 of the Civil Code of the Russian Federation, the protection of civil rights can be carried out through the recognition of rights.
Part 2 of Article 293 of the Code of Civil Procedure of the Russian Federation establishes that the court, having recognized that the immovable thing does not have an owner or the owner of the immovable thing is unknown and it has been registered in the prescribed manner, makes a decision to recognize the right of municipal property or the property of a federal city of Moscow, St. Petersburg or Sevastopol to this thing.
Taking into account the circumstances established by the court, the position of the interested parties, the absence of violations when registering the above-mentioned property as ownerless, the court comes to the conclusion that the stated requirements have been satisfied.
