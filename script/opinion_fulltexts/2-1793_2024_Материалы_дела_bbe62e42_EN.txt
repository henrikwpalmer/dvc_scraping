According to paragraph 3 of Art. 218 of the Civil Code of the Russian Federation, in cases and in the manner provided for by this Code, a person may acquire ownership of property that does not have an owner, of property whose owner is unknown, or of property that the owner has abandoned or to which he has lost ownership on other grounds provided by law.
The provisions of Art. 225 of the Civil Code of the Russian Federation establishes that the ownerlessness of real estate presupposes the absence or unknownness of its owner, and also the condition for considering an application to recognize the property as ownerless is the absence of a dispute about the right.
In accordance with paragraph 1 of Art. 225 of the Civil Code of the Russian Federation, ownerless is a thing that does not have an owner or the owner of which is unknown or, unless otherwise provided by law, the right of ownership to which the owner has refused.
By virtue of clause 3 of Art. 225 of the Civil Code of the Russian Federation, ownerless real estate is accepted for registration by the body that carries out state registration of rights to real estate, upon application from the local government body in whose territory they are located. After a year has passed from the date of registration of an ownerless immovable property, and in the case of registration of a linear object, after three months from the date of registration, the body authorized to manage municipal property may apply to the court with a request to recognize the right of municipal ownership of this thing.
According to Part 7 of Art. 12 of the Federal Constitutional Law “On the admission of the Donetsk People's Republic to the Russian Federation and the formation of a new subject within the Russian Federation - the Donetsk People's Republic No. 5-FKZ dated 10/04/2022, management of property located on the territory of the Donetsk People's Republic, which has signs of ownerlessness and is not classified as federal property, the property of a subject of the Russian Federation or municipal property, is carried out by the authorities of the Donetsk People's Republic in in the manner established by the Government of the Russian Federation, unless otherwise provided by this Federal Constitutional Law. The procedure for recognizing such property as ownerless, established by the regulatory legal acts of the Donetsk People's Republic, is applied until January 1, 2028, in agreement with the federal executive body exercising the functions of managing state property.
By Decree of the Acting Head of the Donetsk People's Republic dated 02.27.2023 No. 64 “On some issues related to the management of property that has signs of ownerlessness, located on the territory of the Donetsk People’s Republic, and the recognition of such property as ownerless,” the Government of the Donetsk People’s Republic is vested with the authority to establish (adopt) a procedure for recognizing as ownerless property that has signs of ownerlessness and not classified as federal property, property of a constituent entity of the Russian Federation or municipal property located on the territory of the Donetsk People's Republic, with the exception of real estate in the form of residential premises, residential buildings, and outbuildings.

Thus, legal relations related to the recognition of real estate as ownerless are regulated by regulatory legal acts of the Donetsk People's Republic.
According to Part 2 of Art. 290 of the Code of Civil Procedure of the Russian Federation, an application for recognition of the right of municipal ownership of an ownerless immovable property is submitted to the court at its location by the body authorized to manage municipal property.
According to Part 2 of Art. 293 of the Code of Civil Procedure of the Russian Federation, the court, having recognized that the immovable thing does not have an owner or the owner of the immovable thing is unknown and it was registered in the prescribed manner, makes a decision to recognize the right of municipal property or the property of the federal city of Moscow, St. Petersburg or Sevastopol to this thing.
