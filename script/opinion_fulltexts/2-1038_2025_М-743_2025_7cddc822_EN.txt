an object (real estate) was identified that had signs of an ownerless immovable property.
According to paragraph 1 of Article 225 of the Civil Code of the Russian Federation, an ownerless thing is a thing that does not have an owner or the owner of which is unknown or, unless otherwise provided by law, the right of ownership to which the owner has refused.
In accordance with paragraph 3 of Art. 225 of the Code of Civil Procedure of the Russian Federation, ownerless immovable things are accepted for registration by the body carrying out state registration of rights to real estate, upon application from the local government body in whose territory they are located.
According to paragraph 1 of Article 235 of the Civil Code of the Russian Federation, the right of ownership terminates when the owner alienates his property to other persons, the owner renounces the right of ownership, the destruction or destruction of property and when the right of ownership to property is lost in other cases provided for by law.
Part 7 of Article 12 of the Federal Constitutional Law of October 4, 2022 No. 5-FKZ “On the admission of the Donetsk People's Republic into the Russian Federation and the formation of a new subject within the Russian Federation - the Donetsk People's Republic” provides that the management of property located on the territory of the Donetsk People's Republic, which has signs of ownerlessness and is not classified as federal property, the property of a subject of the Russian Federation or municipal property, is carried out bodies of the Donetsk People's Republic in the manner established by the Government of the Russian Federation, unless otherwise provided by this Federal Constitutional Law. The procedure for recognizing such property as ownerless, established by the regulatory legal acts of the Donetsk People's Republic, is applied until January 1, 2028, in agreement with the federal executive body exercising the functions of managing state property.
According to paragraph 4 of the Procedure for registering ownerless real estate, established by Order No. P/0086 of the Federal Service for State Registration, Cadastre and Cartography dated March 15, 2023, registration of ownerless real estate is carried out in the following order: acceptance of an application for registration of an ownerless real estate object, for taking the real estate object back into possession, use and order (hereinafter referred to as the application) and documents provided for in paragraph 10 of the Procedure for registering ownerless movable things; checking documents for compliance with the requirements of Federal Law No. 218-FZ for documents received by the rights registration authority; making entries in the Unified State Register of Real Estate on the registration of ownerless real estate.
Thus, by registering an ownerless real estate property, the Federal Service for State Registration, Cadastre and Cartography of the Russian Federation confirms the fact that there is no state registration of ownership and derivative real rights to the specified real estate in the State Register of Rights.

Having examined the case materials and the evidence provided in accordance with the requirements of Art. 67 of the Code of Civil Procedure of the Russian Federation, the court believes that the application of the administration of the city of Mariupol must be satisfied.
According to paragraph 1 of Article 225 of the Civil Code of the Russian Federation, an ownerless thing is a thing that does not have an owner or the owner of which is unknown or, unless otherwise provided by law, the right of ownership to which the owner has refused.
According to paragraph 1 of Article 235 of the Civil Code of the Russian Federation, the right of ownership terminates when the owner alienates his property to other persons, the owner renounces the right of ownership, the destruction or destruction of property and when the right of ownership to property is lost in other cases provided for by law.
According to Art. 56 of the Code of Civil Procedure of the Russian Federation, each party must prove the circumstances to which it refers as the basis for its claims and objections.
By virtue of Art. 67 of the Code of Civil Procedure of the Russian Federation, the court evaluates evidence according to its internal conviction, based on a comprehensive, complete, objective and direct examination of the evidence available in the case.
The court found that part 7 of Article 12 of the Federal Constitutional Law of October 4, 2022 No. 5-FKZ “On the admission of the Donetsk People's Republic into the Russian Federation and the formation of a new subject within the Russian Federation - the Donetsk People's Republic” provides that the management of property located on the territory of the Donetsk People's Republic, which has signs of ownerlessness and is not classified as federal property, the property of a subject of the Russian Federation or municipal property, is carried out by the bodies of the Donetsk People's Republic in the manner established by the Government of the Russian Federation, unless otherwise provided by this Federal Constitutional Law. The procedure for recognizing such property as ownerless, established by the regulatory legal acts of the Donetsk People's Republic, is applied until January 1, 2028, in agreement with the federal executive body exercising the functions of managing state property.
In pursuance of the above norm, the Government of the Russian Federation adopted Decree No. 2474 dated December 28, 2022 “On approval of the Rules for the management of bodies of the Donetsk People’s Republic, Lugansk People’s Republic, Zaporozhye region and Kherson region, which have signs of being ownerless and not classified as federal property, property of a constituent entity of the Russian Federation or municipal property, property located on the territory of these constituent entities of the Russian Federation”, Law of Donetsk of the People's Republic of March 21, 2024 No. 66-RZ “On the peculiarities of identifying, using and recognizing the right of municipal ownership of municipalities of the Donetsk People's Republic to residential premises that have signs of ownerless property located on the territory of the Donetsk People's Republic” (hereinafter referred to as Law No. 66-RZ).
In order to accept, record and register ownerless real estate and escheat property, and increase the efficiency of its use, the Administration adopted order No. 766 dated 12/05/2023 “On approval of the Procedure for identifying, recording and accepting into municipal ownership ownerless real estate and escheat property on the administrative territory of the municipality of the city of Mariupol” (published on the Internet on the official website of the Municipal Formation of the City District) Mariupol - https://mariupol.gosuslugi.ru/) (hereinafter - the Procedure).
Guided by clause 2.9 of the Procedure, Article 5 of Law No. 66-RZ, on April 18, 2024, on the official website of the municipality of the urban district of Mariupol of the Donetsk People's Republic, an announcement was posted on the Internet about the identification of an immovable thing on the territory of Mariupol, Donetsk People's Republic, which has signs of being ownerless in relation to the Object, and about the need for the appearance of the person (persons) who considers (consider) themselves to be its owner or have (have) rights to it, contact the administration with documents excluding the possibility of considering the specified real estate object ownerless.
On May 20, 2024, an on-site inspection of the Property was carried out and a report was drawn up, according to which the following was established: the owner of the Property has not been identified, the apartment is closed, there is no free access to the living space, the location of the keys is unknown, repair and restoration work is being carried out in the house. It is not possible to establish the fact of residence and powers of the owner of the Property based on the results of the commission survey.
On May 20, 2024, an act was drawn up on the impossibility of identifying the owner (copyright holder) of a real estate property that has signs of ownerlessness or escheat in the administrative territory of the municipality of Mariupol.
In accordance with clause 2, part 1, article 5 of Law No. 66-RZ, in order to confirm information about the identified Object, which has signs of being ownerless, the Administration sent appropriate requests.
In accordance with the response of the Chairman of the Liquidation Commission of the State Property Fund of the Donetsk People's Republic (SPF DPR), the following was established: there is no information about the Object in the Unified Register of State Property Objects and objects for which temporary administration (operational management) has been introduced, as well as objects of other forms of ownership under state control.
In accordance with the response of the territorial administration of the Federal Agency for State Property Management in the Donetsk People's Republic (TU Rosimushchestvo in the DPR), there is no information about the Property in the Register of Federal Property.
According to information from the Unified State Register of Real Estate, there is no information about the property.
In accordance with the response from the Department of Property and Land Relations of the Administration of the Mariupol Urban District, the Property is not taken into account in the register of municipal property.
The Administration has sent requests to enterprises providing housing and communal services in relation to the Property. According to the responses received, the following was established: 1) Energosbyt Donetsk LLC was provided with information on making payments for the Facility; 2) GC "DONBASSGAZ" was provided with information on making payments for the Facility; 3) BRANCH “MARIUPOLTEPLOSET” SUE DPR “DONBASTEPLOENERGO” provided information on making payments for the Facility; 4) MUP AGM “KOMMUNALNIK” was provided with information on making payments for the Facility; 5) State Unitary Enterprise of the DPR “DONBASNABKOMPLEKT” provided information on making payments for the Object; 6) MUP "MARIUPOLZHILKOMPLEKS" provided information that there is no payment for the maintenance of residential premises in apartment buildings; 7) BRANCH “MARIUPOL PU WSS” SUE DPR “WATER OF DONBASS” provided information on making payments for the Facility.
According to the response of the State Ministry of Internal Affairs of Russia “Mariupol”, there is no information about persons registered at this address.
By Decree of the Administration dated August 16, 2024 No. 320 “On registering real estate as ownerless”, the property was registered as ownerless residential premises.
According to the Extract from the Unified State Register of Real Estate on the main characteristics and registered rights to the property, the property was registered as an ownerless property

By Decree of the Administration dated September 18, 2024 No. 415 “On recognizing real estate objects as ownerless and including them in the Register of ownerless real estate objects on the territory of the municipality of the Mariupol urban district” The object is included in the Register of ownerless real estate objects, which is confirmed by an Extract from the Register of ownerless real estate objects on the territory of the municipal urban district of Mariupol.
In accordance with Art. 10 of Law No. 66-RZ, after three months from the date of registration of the residential premises as ownerless by the territorial body of the federal executive body carrying out state cadastral registration, state registration of rights, maintenance of the Unified State Register of Real Estate, the authorized body applies to the court with an application for recognition of the right of municipal ownership of the municipality to this residential premises in the manner prescribed by the legislation of the Russian Federation.
Measures in accordance with clauses 2.9.1, 2.9.2, 2.9.3 of the standard procedure for identifying, recording and accepting into municipal ownership ownerless real estate and escheat property, approved by the Decree of the Head of the Donetsk People's Republic dated April 28, 2021. No. 116 by the administration of the city of Mariupol were completed in full
Based on the Decision of the Mariupol City Council of the Donetsk People's Republic dated November 30, 2023 No. 1-8/3 “On issues of legal succession in the municipality of the Mariupol urban district of the Donetsk People's Republic,” the successor to the administration of the city of Mariupol of the Donetsk People's Republic is the Administration of the Mariupol urban district of the Donetsk People's Republic.
Since from the day this object was registered as ownerless, within the period established by law, no one declared their rights to it, in order to claim it for registration in the manner established by the current legislation, the Administration of the Mariupol City District of the Donetsk People's Republic had the right to apply to the court for recognition of the right of municipal ownership to it. In this connection, the requirements of the application are subject to satisfaction.
