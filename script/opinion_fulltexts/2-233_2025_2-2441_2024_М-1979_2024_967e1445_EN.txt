At the court hearing Kravchenko E.V. representative by proxy of the city administration Mariupol did not appear, she was duly notified of the day, time and place of its holding, before it began she submitted a statement waiving the stated demands, and asked to consider the case on the merits in her absence. Taking into account the fact that the parties were properly notified of the day, time and place of the court hearing in the case, the court considers it possible to consider the case on the merits in their absence. In accordance with Art. 220 of the Civil Procedure Code of the Russian Federation, the court terminates the proceedings if the plaintiff renounces the claim and the refusal is accepted by the court /p. 4/. Taking into account the requirements of the current legislation, as well as the fact that the refusal of the city administration Mariupol from the demands made to her does not contradict the law and does not violate the rights and legitimate interests of third parties, the court considers it possible to accept this refusal and terminate the proceedings. Based on the above, guided by Articles 39, 173, 220, 224 – 225 of the Civil Procedure Code of the Russian Federation, the court
DEFINED:
Accept the refusal of the administration of the city district of Mariupol of the Donetsk People's Republic of the demands submitted to the Office of the Federal Service for State Registration, Cadastre and Cartography for the Donetsk People's Republic for recognition of the right of municipal ownership of an ownerless real estate property.
Terminate the proceedings on the application of the administration of the city district of Mariupol of the Donetsk People's Republic to the Office of the Federal Service for State Registration, Cadastre and Cartography for the Donetsk People's Republic for recognition of the right of municipal ownership of an ownerless real estate property
