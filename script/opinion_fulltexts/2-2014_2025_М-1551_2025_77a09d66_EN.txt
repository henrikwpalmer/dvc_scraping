The court, having examined the case materials and analyzed the evidence provided in its entirety, comes to the conclusion that the application must be granted on the following grounds.
According to the provisions of Article 3 of the Civil Procedure Code of the Russian Federation (hereinafter referred to as the Civil Procedure Code of the Russian Federation), an interested person has the right, in the manner established by the legislation on civil proceedings, to apply to the court for the protection of violated or disputed rights, freedoms or legitimate interests.
In accordance with Article 55 of the Code of Civil Procedure of the Russian Federation, evidence in the case is information obtained in the manner prescribed by law about the facts, on the basis of which the court establishes the presence or absence of circumstances justifying the demands and objections of the parties, as well as other circumstances that are important for the correct consideration and resolution of the case.
Evidence obtained in violation of the law has no legal force and cannot be used as the basis for a court decision.
Article 56 of the Code of Civil Procedure of the Russian Federation determines that each party must prove the circumstances to which it refers as the basis for its claims and objections, unless otherwise provided by law.
Each person participating in the case must disclose the evidence to which he refers as the basis of his claims and objections to other persons participating in the case, within the period established by the court, unless otherwise established by the said Code.
In accordance with Part 1 of Art. 225 of the Civil Code of the Russian Federation, ownerless is a thing that does not have an owner or the owner of which is unknown or, unless otherwise provided by law, the owner of which has renounced the right of ownership.
As follows from Part 3 of Art. 225 of the Civil Code of the Russian Federation, ownerless real estate is accepted for registration by the body that carries out state registration of rights to real estate, upon application from the local government body in whose territory they are located. After a year has passed from the date of registration of an ownerless immovable property, and in the case of registration of a linear object, after three months from the date of registration, the body authorized to manage municipal property may apply to the court with a request to recognize the right of municipal ownership of this thing.
Paragraph 3 of Article 218 of the Civil Code of the Russian Federation establishes that in cases and in the manner provided for by this Code, a person may acquire the right of ownership to property that does not have an owner, to property whose owner is unknown, or to property that the owner has abandoned or to which he has lost the right of ownership on other grounds provided by law.
According to Article 4 of the Federal Constitutional Law of the Russian Federation of October 4, 2022 No. 5-FKZ “On the admission into the Russian Federation of the Donetsk People's Republic and the formation within the Russian Federation of a new subject of the Donetsk People's Republic” (hereinafter referred to as Law No. 5-FKZ), legislative and other regulatory legal acts of the Russian Federation are in force on the territory of the Donetsk People's Republic from the date of admission of the Donetsk People's Republic to the Russian Federation and the formation of a new subject within the Russian Federation, unless otherwise provided by this Federal Constitutional Law.
Regulatory legal acts of the Donetsk People's Republic are valid on the territory of the Donetsk People's Republic until the end of the transition period or until the adoption of the corresponding regulatory legal act of the Russian Federation and (or) the regulatory legal act of the Donetsk People's Republic.
According to Part 7 of Article 12 of Law No. 5-FKZ, management of property located on the territory of the Donetsk People's Republic, which has signs of ownerlessness and is not classified as federal property, the property of a constituent entity of the Russian Federation or municipal property, is carried out by the authorities of the Donetsk People's Republic in the manner established by the Government of the Russian Federation, unless otherwise provided by this Federal Constitutional Law. The procedure for recognizing such property as ownerless, established by regulatory legal acts of the Donetsk People's Republic, applies until January 1, 2028 in agreement with the federal executive body exercising the functions of managing state property.
In accordance with Part 2 of Article 290 of the Code of Civil Procedure of the Russian Federation, an application for recognition of ownership of an ownerless immovable property is submitted to the court at its location by the body authorized to manage municipal property or property owned by the federal city of Moscow, St. Petersburg or Sevastopol.
Part 2 of Article 293 of the Code of Civil Procedure of the Russian Federation provides that the court, having recognized that the immovable thing does not have an owner or the owner of the immovable thing is unknown and it has been registered in the prescribed manner, makes a decision to recognize the right of municipal property or the property of a federal city of Moscow, St. Petersburg or Sevastopol to this thing.
According to clause 2.1 of Chapter Decree No. 140 of May 10, 2024, the executive body of the Donetsk People's Republic authorized to manage state property has the right to apply to the court with a demand for recognition of the right of state ownership of the objects defined in subclause 1.1 of this clause (with the exception of residential premises, outbuildings intended to satisfy citizens' household and other needs), after three months from the date of their registration as ownerless real estate by the body carrying out state registration of rights to real estate.
The Law of the Donetsk People's Republic dated March 21, 2024 No. 66-RZ defines the specifics of identifying, using and recognizing the right of municipal ownership of municipalities of the Donetsk People's Republic to residential premises that have signs of ownerless property located on the territory of the Donetsk People's Republic, according to which, after three months from the date of setting the residential premises as ownerless by the territorial body of the federal executive body exercising state cadastral registration, state registration of rights, maintenance of the Unified State Register of Real Estate, the authorized body applies to the court with an application to recognize the right of municipal property of a municipal entity to this residential premises in the manner prescribed by the legislation of the Russian Federation.
According to Part 1 of Art. 293 of the Code of Civil Procedure of the Russian Federation, the court, having recognized that the owner has renounced the right of ownership of the movable thing, makes a decision to recognize the movable thing as ownerless and transfer it to the ownership of the person who has taken possession of it.
In accordance with subparagraph 3.1 of paragraph 3 of the Decree of the Acting Head of the Donetsk People's Republic dated February 27, 2023 No. 64 “On some issues related to the management of property that has signs of being ownerless, located on the territory of the Donetsk People’s Republic, and the recognition of such property as ownerless” (hereinafter referred to as Decree No. 64), administrations of cities and districts are bodies for managing property that has signs of being ownerless. ownerless and not classified as federal property, located on the territory of the Donetsk People's Republic, in accordance with the Rules approved by the Decree of the Government of the Russian Federation of December 28, 2022 No. 2474 “On approval of the Rules for the management of bodies of the Donetsk People's Republic, Lugansk People's Republic, Zaporozhye region and Kherson region having signs of ownerlessness and not classified as federal property, property subject of the Russian Federation or municipal property with property located on the territories of the specified subjects of the Russian Federation.”
Subclause 3.2 of clause 3 of Decree No. 64 determines that until 01/01/2028, in relation to the property specified in subclause 3.1 of clause 3, the procedure for recognizing it as ownerless is applied, established by Decree of the Head of the Donetsk People's Republic of 04/28/2021 No. 116 “On identifying, recording and accepting ownerless property into municipal ownership.” real estate and escheat property" (hereinafter - Standard Procedure No. 116), taking into account the changes introduced by Decree No. 64.
In accordance with the decision of the Donetsk City Council of the Donetsk People's Republic dated November 30, 2023 No. 1/8-3 “On issues of legal succession in the municipal formation of the Donetsk City District of the Donetsk People's Republic”, when performing the functions of managing property that has signs of being ownerless, the administration of the Donetsk City District performs the functions of identifying, recording and accepting ownerless things into municipal ownership in accordance with the requirements of the Standard Procedure No. 116.
The procedure for identifying, registering, storing and accepting into municipal ownership of the territorial community of the city of Donetsk on the territory of the city of Donetsk is determined by the Procedure for identifying, recording and accepting ownerless real estate and escheat into municipal ownership of the territorial community of the city of Donetsk, approved by the order of the head of the administration of the city of Donetsk dated June 22, 2021 No. 1370 (hereinafter referred to as Procedure No. 1370).
Clause 1.1 of Procedure No. 1370, which was developed in accordance with the Civil Code of the Russian Federation and in pursuance of paragraph 2 of the Decree of the Head of the Donetsk People's Republic of April 28, 2021 No. 116 “On the identification, registration and acceptance into municipal ownership of ownerless real estate and escheat property”, defines the procedure for identifying, registering, storing and accepting into municipal ownership the territorial community of the city of Donetsk ownerless real estate and escheat property identified on the territory of the city of Donetsk.
According to clause 2.1 of section II of the Procedure for identifying, recording and accepting into the municipal ownership of the municipality of the Donetsk city district of the Donetsk People's Republic ownerless real estate of the housing stock, approved by the decision of the Donetsk city council of the Donetsk People's Republic dated 07/04/24 No. 1/32-5 (hereinafter referred to as Procedure No. 1/32-5), the administration of the Donetsk city district of the Donetsk People's Republic represented by The Department of Municipal Property of the Administration of the Donetsk Urban District (hereinafter referred to as the Department of Municipal Property) independently carries out actions to identify, record and accept ownerless real estate located on the territory of the Donetsk Urban District into the municipal property of the Donetsk Urban District. Information about residential premises that have signs of ownerlessness may be received by the municipal property department from individuals and legal entities, including public authorities, who have become aware of such property.
According to clause 2.2 of Procedure No. 1/32-5, the signs of ownerless real estate in relation to residential premises are: failure to pay for residential premises and utilities within one year before the day of identification of residential premises that have signs of ownerlessness; lack of information about the registered ownership of residential premises in the Unified State Register of Real Estate; non-use of residential premises, which, among other things, creates a threat to its safety (or an apartment building if the premises is an apartment, a room in an apartment building), safety for the life and health of other persons (including the rights holders of other premises in an apartment building if the premises is an apartment, a room in an apartment building).
As the court found, the Administration of the Donetsk Urban District followed the procedure for identifying, registering, storing and accepting into municipal ownership the above-mentioned real estate, which has signs of being ownerless, which is confirmed by evidence attached to the case materials, which were examined by the court (case sheet 7-37).
