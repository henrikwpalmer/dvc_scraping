The court, having examined the case materials, came to the conclusion that the demands of the Administration of the Makeyevka Urban District must be satisfied on the following grounds:
According to clause 6, part 1, art. 262 of the Code of Civil Procedure of the Russian Federation, by way of special proceedings, the court considers cases of recognition of the right of municipal ownership of an ownerless immovable property.
According to Part 1 of Art. 225 of the Civil Code of the Russian Federation, ownerless is a thing that does not have an owner or the owner of which is unknown, or, unless otherwise provided by law, the owner of which has renounced the right of ownership.
Clause 3 of Art. 218 of the Civil Code of the Russian Federation provides that a person can acquire ownership of property that does not have an owner, property whose owner is unknown, or property that the owner has abandoned or to which he has lost ownership on other grounds provided by law.

According to Article 4 of the Federal Constitutional Law of the Russian Federation of October 4, 2022 No. 5-FKZ, “On the admission into the Russian Federation of the Donetsk People’s Republic and the formation within the Russian Federation of a new subject - the Donetsk People’s Republic” (hereinafter referred to as the Law “5-FKZ”), legislative and other regulatory legal acts of the Russian Federation are valid on the territory of the Donetsk People’s Republic from the date of admission of the Donetsk People’s Republic to the Russian Federation of the People's Republic and the formation of a new subject within the Russian Federation, unless otherwise provided by this Federal Constitutional Law.
Regulatory legal acts of the Donetsk People's Republic are valid on the territory of the Donetsk People's Republic until the end of the transition period or until the adoption of the corresponding regulatory legal act of the Russian Federation and (or) the regulatory legal act of the Donetsk People's Republic.
According to Part 7 of Article 12 of Law No. 5-FKZ, management of property located on the territory of the Donetsk People's Republic, which has signs of ownerlessness and is not classified as federal property, the property of a constituent entity of the Russian Federation or municipal property, is carried out by the authorities of the Donetsk People's Republic in the manner established by the Government of the Russian Federation, unless otherwise provided by this Federal Constitutional Law. The procedure for recognizing such property as ownerless, established by the regulatory legal acts of the Donetsk People's Republic, is applied until January 1, 2028, in agreement with the federal executive body exercising the functions of managing state property.
According to clause 1.2 of the Decree of the Head of the Donetsk People's Republic of May 10, 2023 No. 140, the executive body of the Donetsk People's Republic authorized to manage state property has the right to apply to the court with a demand for recognition of the right of state ownership of the objects defined in subclause 1.1 of this clause (with the exception of residential premises, outbuildings intended to satisfy citizens' household and other needs), after three months from the date of their registration as ownerless real estate by the body carrying out state registration of rights to real estate.
By Decree of the Acting Head of the Donetsk People's Republic dated 02.27.2023 No. 64 “On some issues related to the management of property that has signs of ownerlessness, located on the territory of the Donetsk People’s Republic, and the recognition of such property as ownerless,” the Government of the Donetsk People’s Republic is vested with the authority to establish (adopt) a procedure for recognizing as ownerless property that has signs of ownerlessness and not classified as federal property, property of a constituent entity of the Russian Federation or municipal property located on the territory of the Donetsk People's Republic, with the exception of real estate in the form of residential premises, residential buildings, and outbuildings.
In accordance with paragraphs. 1.1. Clause 1 of the Decree of the Government of the Donetsk People's Republic "On the recognition of real estate located on the territory of the Donetsk People's Republic as ownerless with the exception of residential buildings, residential premises, garden houses, outbuildings intended to satisfy citizens' household and other needs" dated June 22, 2023 No. 45-5, the Ministry of Property and Land Relations of the Donetsk People's Republic is the executive body for management state real estate that has signs of ownerlessness in accordance with the Rules approved by Decree of the Government of the Russian Federation dated December 28, 2022 No. 2474 “On approval of the Rules for the management of bodies of the Donetsk People's Republic, Lugansk People's Republic, Zaporozhye region and Kherson region that have signs of ownerlessness and are not classified as federal property, property of a constituent entity of the Russian Federation or municipal property property located on the territories of the specified constituent entities of the Russian Federation"
pp. 2.1. Clause 2 of the Decree of the Government of the Donetsk People's Republic of June 22, 2023 No. 45-5 approved the procedure for recognizing real estate on the territory of the Donetsk People's Republic as ownerless, with the exception of residential buildings, residential premises, garden houses and outbuildings intended to satisfy citizens' household and other needs.
Thus, when carrying out the functions of managing real estate that has signs of ownerlessness, the Ministry of Property and Land Relations of the Donetsk People's Republic performed the functions of identifying, recording and accepting ownerless things into state ownership in accordance with the requirements of the Procedure.
In accordance with clause 3 of the Procedure, the Ministry of Property and Land Relations of the Donetsk People's Republic carries out actions to identify ownerless real estate in the state ownership of the administrative-territorial units of the Donetsk People's Republic, on the territory of which they are located.
In accordance with paragraphs. 5-12 of the Procedure, the Ministry of Property and Land Relations of the Donetsk People's Republic carried out all the prescribed measures to identify immovable property that has signs of being ownerless and falls under the Standard Procedure.
