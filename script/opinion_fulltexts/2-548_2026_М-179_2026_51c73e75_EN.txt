In accordance with Part 2 of Art. 4, part 7 art. 12 Federal Constitutional Law dated

- FKZ “On the admission of the Donetsk People's Republic to the Russian Federation and the formation of a new subject within the Russian Federation - the Donetsk People's Republic”, regulatory legal acts of the Donetsk People's Republic are valid on the territory of the Donetsk People's Republic until the end of the transition period or until the adoption of the relevant regulatory legal act of the Russian Federation and (or) a regulatory legal act of the Donetsk People's Republic.
Management of property located on the territory of the Donetsk People's Republic, which has signs of ownerlessness and is not classified as federal property, the property of a constituent entity of the Russian Federation or municipal property, is carried out by the authorities of the Donetsk People's Republic in the manner established by the Government of the Russian Federation, unless otherwise provided by 5-FKZ.
The procedure for recognizing such property as ownerless, established by regulatory legal acts of the Donetsk People's Republic, applies until
DD.MM.YYYY
, in agreement with the federal executive body performing the functions of managing state property.
In accordance with Part 1 of Art. 225 of the Civil Code of the Russian Federation, ownerless is a thing that does not have an owner or the owner of which is unknown or, unless otherwise provided by law, the right of ownership to which the owner has refused.
According to Part 3 of Art. 218 of the Civil Code of the Russian Federation, in cases and in the manner provided for by this Code, a person may acquire the right of ownership to property that does not have an owner, to property whose owner is unknown, or to property that the owner has abandoned or to which he has lost the right of ownership on other grounds provided by law.
P. 1 part 3 tbsp. 225 of the Civil Code of the Russian Federation stipulates that ownerless immovable things are accepted for registration by the body carrying out state registration of rights to real estate, at the request of a local government body, in the territory
which they are located.
In accordance with Art. 10 of the Law of the Donetsk People's Republic "On the specifics of identifying, using and recognizing the right of municipal property of municipalities of the Donetsk People's Republic to residential premises that have signs of ownerless property located on the territory of the Donetsk People's Republic" dated
DD.MM.YYYY

-RZ, after three months from the date of registration of the residential premises as ownerless by the territorial body of the federal executive body carrying out state cadastral registration, state registration of rights, maintenance of the Unified State Register of Real Estate, the authorized body applies to the court with an application for recognition of the right of municipal ownership of the municipal formation to this residential premises in the manner prescribed by the legislation of the Russian Federation.
In accordance with Art. 3 of Law 66-RZ, local government bodies of the Donetsk People's Republic, represented by bodies authorized by them, independently carry out actions to identify, record and accept residential premises that have signs of ownerless property into the municipal property of the municipalities of the Donetsk People's Republic, on the territory of which they are located, on the grounds provided for by law.
In accordance with Part 1 of Art. 4 of Law 66-RZ, information about residential premises that have signs of ownerlessness can come from individuals and legal entities, including public authorities, who have become aware of such property.
In accordance with Part 2 of Art. 5 of Law 66-RZ The Administration of the Makeyevka Urban District of the Donetsk People's Republic carried out all the prescribed measures to identify real estate that has signs of being ownerless.

checking the validity of the documents submitted by the applicant and the availability of the corresponding rights of the person or authority who prepared the document;

registration of ownerless real estate in the manner established by the regulatory body.
