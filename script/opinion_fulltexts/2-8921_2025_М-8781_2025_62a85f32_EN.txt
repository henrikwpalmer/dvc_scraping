Under such circumstances, taking into account the provisions of Part 1 of Article 167 of the Civil Procedure Code of the Russian Federation (hereinafter referred to as the Code of Civil Procedure of the Russian Federation), the court considers it possible to consider this case in the absence of the parties who failed to appear.
Having examined the case materials, the court considers the claims of the MIZO DPR to be satisfied on the following grounds.
The court found that in accordance with Article 4 of the Federal Constitutional Law of the Russian Federation dated October 4, 2022 No. 5-FKZ “On the admission into the Russian Federation of the Donetsk People’s Republic and the formation within the Russian Federation of a new subject - the Donetsk People’s Republic (hereinafter referred to as Law No. 5-FKZ”), legislative and other regulatory legal acts of the Russian Federation are valid on the territory of the Donetsk People’s Republic from the date of adoption in the Russian Federation of the Donetsk People's Republic and the formation of a new subject within the Russian Federation, unless otherwise provided by this Federal Constitutional Law.
Regulatory legal acts of the DPR are valid on the territory of the DPR until the end of the transition period or until the adoption of the corresponding regulatory legal act of the Russian Federation and (or) a regulatory act of the DPR.
According to Part 7 of Art. 12 of Law No. 5-FKZ, management of property located on the territory of the DPR, which has signs of ownerlessness and is not classified as federal property of a constituent entity of the Russian Federation or municipal property, is carried out by the bodies of the DPR in the manner established by the Government of the Russian Federation, unless otherwise provided by this FKZ. The procedure for recognizing such property as ownerless, established by the regulatory legal acts of the DPR, is applied until January 1, 2028, in agreement with the federal executive body exercising the functions of managing state property.
Decree of the Acting Head of the DPR dated May 10, 2023 No. 140 (hereinafter referred to as Decree No. 140 dated May 10, 2023) established the specifics of acquiring state and municipal property rights to ownerless real estate on the territory of the DPR.
According to clause 1.2 of Decree No. 14 dated 10.05.2023), the executive body of the DPR, authorized to manage state property, has the right to apply to the court with a demand for recognition of the right of state ownership of the objects defined in subclause 1.1 of this clause (with the exception of residential premises, outbuildings intended to satisfy citizens’ household and other needs), after three months from the date of their registration as ownerless real estate by the body carrying out state registration of rights to real estate.
From the norms of clause 1.2 of Decree No. 140 dated May 10, 2023, it follows that the executive body of the DPR, authorized to manage state property, has the right to go to court with a demand for recognition of the right of state ownership of ownerless buildings, structures (except for linear objects), premises, objects of unfinished construction that do not have an owner or the owner of which is unknown, or the ownership of which the owner has refused (with the exception of residential premises, utility buildings intended to satisfy citizens' household and other needs).
By Decree of the Acting Head of the DPR dated 02.27.2023 No. 64 “On some issues related to the management of property that has signs of ownerlessness, located on the territory of the Donetsk People’s Republic, and the recognition of such property as ownerless” (hereinafter referred to as Decree dated 02.27.2023 No. 64), the Government of the DPR is vested with the authority to establish (adoption) of the procedure for recognizing as ownerless property that has signs of ownerlessness and is not classified as federal property, the property of a constituent entity of the Russian Federation or municipal property located on the territory of the DPR, with the exception of real estate in the form of residential premises, residential buildings, and outbuildings.
By Decree of the Head of the DPR dated December 13, 2024 No. 695 (hereinafter referred to as Decree dated December 13, 2024 No. 695) “On the formation (establishment) of the Ministry of Property and Land Relations of the Donetsk People’s Republic,” the MIZO was formed and the Regulations on the Ministry of Property and Land Relations of the DPR were approved.
