From the analysis of the provisions of paragraph 1 of part 1 of article 262, part 3 of article 263, articles 264, 265 and 267 of the Civil Procedure Code of the Russian Federation, it follows that the courts can accept applications for establishing facts and consider them in a special proceeding if the applicant’s right is indisputable, but the lack of proper confirmation of circumstances significant for the exercise of this right prevents the exercise of a person’s rights, and if the establishment of a fact is not associated with the subsequent resolution of a dispute about the right, subordinate to the court.
