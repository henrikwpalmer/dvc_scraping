The court considers it possible to consider the case in the absence of the persons involved in the case who have been timely and properly notified of the date, time and place of the consideration of the case.
In accordance with Art. 39 of the Code of Civil Procedure of the Russian Federation, the plaintiff has the right to change the basis or subject of the claim, increase or decrease the amount of the claim, or abandon the claim, the defendant has the right to admit the claim, the parties can end the case with an amicable agreement.
According to Article 220 of the Code of Civil Procedure of the Russian Federation, the court terminates the proceedings if the plaintiff renounces the claim and the refusal is accepted by the court.
Having examined the case materials, the court comes to the conclusion that the refusal of the application does not contradict the current legislation and does not violate the rights of the parties and other persons.
Based on the above, guided by Art. 220, 221, 224, 225 Code of Civil Procedure of the Russian Federation court,
