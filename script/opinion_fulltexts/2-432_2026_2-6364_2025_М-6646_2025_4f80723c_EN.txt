According to clause 6, part 1, art. 262, part 2 art. 290 of the Code of Civil Procedure of the Russian Federation, an application for recognition of ownership of an ownerless immovable thing is considered by the court in a special proceeding.
Paragraph 3 of Article 218 of the Civil Code of the Russian Federation (hereinafter referred to as the Civil Code of the Russian Federation) establishes that in cases and in the manner provided for by the Civil Code of the Russian Federation, a person may acquire ownership rights to property that does not have an owner, to property whose owner is unknown, or to property that the owner has refused or to which he has lost ownership on other grounds provided by law.
In accordance with Article 225 of the Civil Code of the Russian Federation, an ownerless thing is a thing that does not have an owner or the owner of which is unknown or, unless otherwise provided by law, the right of ownership to which the owner has refused.
Ownerless real estate is accepted for registration by the body that carries out state registration of rights to real estate, upon application from the local government body in whose territory they are located. After a year has passed from the date of registration of an ownerless immovable property, the body authorized to manage municipal property may apply to the court with a request to recognize the right of municipal ownership of this thing.
In accordance with Part 2 of Article 4, Part 7 of Article 12 of the Federal Constitutional Law of October 4, 2022 No. 5-FKZ “On the admission of the Donetsk People's Republic to the Russian Federation and the formation of a new subject within the Russian Federation - the Donetsk People's Republic”, the regulatory legal acts of the Donetsk People's Republic are valid on the territory of the Donetsk People's Republic until the end of the transition period or until the adoption of the corresponding regulatory legal act of the Russian Federation and (or) normative legal act of the Donetsk People's Republic. Management of property located on the territory of the Donetsk People's Republic, which has signs of ownerlessness and is not classified as federal property, property of a constituent entity of the Russian Federation or municipal property, is carried out by the authorities of the Donetsk People's Republic in the following manner: becoming the Government of the Russian Federation, unless otherwise provided by this Federal Constitutional Law.

According to paragraph 7 of Section II of Order No. 1/32-4, the city district administration, represented by the municipal property department, independently carries out actions to identify, record and accept into municipal ownership ownerless real estate located on the territory of the Donetsk urban district. Based on Part 3 of Article 1 of the Federal Law of the Russian Federation dated
DD.MM.YYYY
No. 218-FZ “On State Registration of Real Estate” (hereinafter referred to as Law No. 218-FZ), state registration of rights to real estate is a legal act of recognition and confirmation of the emergence, change, transition, termination of the right of a certain person to real estate or restrictions on such a right and encumbrance of real estate (hereinafter referred to as state registration of rights).
In accordance with Part 3 of Article 3 of Law No. 218-FZ, the competence of the rights registration authority when carrying out state cadastral registration and state registration of rights includes, among other things:
checking the validity of the documents submitted by the applicant and the availability of the corresponding rights of the person or authority who prepared the document;
checking the availability of previously registered and previously declared rights;
registration of ownerless real estate in the manner established by the regulatory body.
Thus, by placing real estate at the address:
<address>
to be registered as ownerless, the territorial body of the federal executive body carrying out state cadastral registration, state registration of rights, maintaining the Unified State Register of Real Estate, confirms the fact that state registration as ownerless was carried out lawfully.
By virtue of Art. 210 of the Civil Code of the Russian Federation, the owner bears the burden of maintaining the property belonging to him, unless otherwise provided by law or contract. The Constitutional Court of the Russian Federation in paragraph 3.1 of the ruling dated November 2, 2006 No. 444-0 explained that this obligation includes, among other things, the incurrence of expenses for maintaining property in proper condition (major and current repairs, insurance, registration, security, etc.) and the fulfillment of other requirements emanating from state and local government bodies authorized by law.
According to Art. 12 of the Federal Law of December 28, 2010 N 390-FZ “On Security”, local government bodies, within their competence, ensure the implementation of the legislation of the Russian Federation in the field of security.
According to paragraph 5 of Section I of Order No. 1/32-4, the main goals and objectives of identifying ownerless real estate objects and registering municipal ownership rights to them are: involving unused property in free civil circulation; ensuring normal and safe technical operation of the property; improvement and proper maintenance of the territory of the Donetsk urban district.
Subparagraph 2 of paragraph 14 of Section II of Procedure No. 1/32-4 establishes, among other things, the need for personal appearance at the Department of a person (persons) who considers himself the owner of an immovable thing that has signs of being ownerless or who has rights to it with the following documents:
a passport of a citizen of the Russian Federation or other identification document;
title (title) documents for the real estate property.
After the expiration of the above period, from the date of registration of the ownerless immovable property, any individuals and legal entities have claims on the property at the address:
<address>
, which is the subject of this application, was not declared, that is, there is no dispute about the right.
Clause 1.1 of clause 1 of Decree No. 140 establishes, inter alia, that the local government body of a municipal formation in the Donetsk People's Republic, authorized to manage municipal property, has the right to apply to the court with a demand for recognition of the right of municipal ownership of ownerless buildings, structures (except for linear objects), premises, objects of unfinished construction that do not have an owner or the owner of which is unknown or from the ownership of which the owner refused, located on the territory of such a municipal entity, after three months from the date of their registration by the body carrying out state registration of rights to real estate.
Having assessed the factual circumstances and the evidence presented to the court, the court comes to the conclusion that there are legal grounds for satisfying the stated demands of the administration of the Donetsk urban district of the Donetsk People's Republic for recognition of the right of municipal ownership of an ownerless real estate property, since the owners of the above-mentioned property did not declare their rights, the conditions for recognition of the right of municipal ownership of an ownerless property, provided for in Article 225 of the Civil Code of the Russian Federation, were met by the applicant, the deadline from the date of statement registration of this object by the body carrying out state registration has expired, the legal status of the specified real estate object at the time of consideration of the case in court has not changed.
Under the above circumstances, the court comes to the conclusion that the demands made by the administration of the Donetsk urban district of the Donetsk People's Republic to recognize as ownerless a real estate property located at the address: Russian Federation, Donetsk People's Republic, Donetsk urban district,
<address>
