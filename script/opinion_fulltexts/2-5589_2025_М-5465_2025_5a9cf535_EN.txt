SOLUTION
IN THE NAME OF THE RUSSIAN FEDERATION
On September 23, 2025, the Voroshilovsky Interdistrict Court of Donetsk, composed of presiding judge Kozlova A.A., with secretary Gorelov A.A., having considered in open court in the courtroom a civil case at the request of the Administration of the Donetsk City District, the interested party - the Office of the Federal Service for State Registration, Cadastre and Cartography for the Donetsk People's Republic, on the recognition of the right of municipal property to ownerless immovable thing -
INSTALLED:
The administration of the Donetsk urban district applied to the Voroshilovsky interdistrict court of Donetsk with an application for recognition of the right of municipal ownership of an ownerless real estate property, in which it indicated that at the address:
<address>
, property has been identified that has signs of being ownerless. The state registration of the specified real estate was carried out as ownerless. Information about the identification of real estate that has signs of being ownerless has been officially published. To resolve the issue of further management of real estate, it is necessary to recognize the right of municipal ownership represented by the municipal formation of the Donetsk urban district of the Donetsk People's Republic to ownerless real estate -
<address>
.
The representative of the applicant, the Administration of the Donetsk City District, was duly notified of the time and place of the consideration of the case, did not appear at the court hearing, having filed a petition to consider the case without his participation, supported the stated demands in full, asked for the demands to be satisfied.
The representative of the Office of the Federal Service for State Registration, Cadastre and Cartography for the Donetsk People's Republic was duly notified of the time and place of the consideration of the case, but did not appear at the court hearing, filing a petition to consider the case in his absence.
Thus, the court considers it possible to consider the case in the absence of these persons, in accordance with the provisions of Art. 167 Code of Civil Procedure of the Russian Federation.
Having examined the case materials, the court found the following.
In accordance with Art. 55 of the Code of Civil Procedure of the Russian Federation, evidence in the case is information obtained in the manner prescribed by law about the facts, on the basis of which the court establishes the presence or absence of circumstances justifying the demands and objections of the parties, as well as other circumstances that are important for the correct consideration and resolution of the case. Evidence obtained in violation of the law has no legal force and cannot be used as the basis for a court decision.
Article 56 of the Code of Civil Procedure of the Russian Federation determines that each party must prove the circumstances to which it refers as the basis for its claims and objections, unless otherwise provided by federal law. Each person participating in the case must disclose the evidence to which he refers as the basis for his claims and objections to other persons participating in the case, within the period established by the court, unless otherwise established by this Code.
In accordance with Article 225 of the Civil Code of the Russian Federation (hereinafter referred to as the Civil Code of the Russian Federation), an ownerless thing is a thing that does not have an owner or the owner of which is unknown or, unless otherwise provided by law, the right of ownership to which the owner has refused.
Paragraph 3 of Article 218 of the Civil Code of the Russian Federation establishes that in cases and in the manner provided for by this Code, a person may acquire the right of ownership to property that does not have an owner, to property whose owner is unknown, or to property that the owner has abandoned or to which he has lost the right of ownership on other grounds provided by law.
In accordance with Part 2 of Article 4, Part 7 of Article 12 of the Federal Constitutional Law of October 4, 2022 No. 5-FKZ “On the admission of the Donetsk People's Republic to the Russian Federation and the formation of a new subject within the Russian Federation - the Donetsk People's Republic”, the regulatory legal acts of the Donetsk People's Republic are valid on the territory of the Donetsk People's Republic until the end of the transition period or until the adoption of the corresponding regulatory legal act of the Russian Federation and (or) normative legal act of the Donetsk People's Republic. Management of property located on the territory of the Donetsk People's Republic, which has signs of ownerlessness and is not classified as federal property, the property of a constituent entity of the Russian Federation or municipal property, is carried out by the authorities of the Donetsk People's Republic in the manner established by the Government of the Russian Federation, unless otherwise provided by this Federal Constitutional Law.
According to the Charter of the municipal formation of the Donetsk city district of the Donetsk People's Republic, adopted by the decision of the Donetsk city council of the Donetsk People's Republic dated November 09, 2023 No. 1/5-1, issues of local importance of the urban district include, among other things, the ownership, use and disposal of property in municipal ownership, the implementation of municipal housing control.
The procedure for recognizing such property as ownerless, established by the regulatory legal acts of the Donetsk People's Republic, applies until 01/01/2028, in agreement with the federal executive body performing the functions of managing state property.
In accordance with subparagraph 3.1 of paragraph 3 of the Decree of the Acting Head of the Donetsk People's Republic dated February 27, 2023 No. 64 “On some issues related to the management of property that has signs of ownerlessness, located on the territory of the DPR, and the recognition of such property as ownerless” (hereinafter referred to as Decree No. 64), city and district administrations are bodies for managing property that has signs of ownerlessness and not classified as Federal property, located on the territory of the DPR, in accordance with the Rules approved by the Decree of the Government of the Russian Federation dated December 28, 2022 No. 2474 “On approval of the Rules for the management of bodies of the Donetsk People's Republic, Lugansk People's Republic, Zaporozhye region and Kherson region, which have signs of ownerlessness and are not classified as federal property, the property of a constituent entity of the Russian Federation or municipal property property located on the territories of the specified constituent entities of the Russian Federation.”
Clause 1.2 of the decision of the Donetsk City Council of the Donetsk People's Republic dated November 30, 2023 No. 1/8-3 “On issues of legal succession in the municipal formation of the Donetsk City District of the Donetsk People's Republic” (hereinafter referred to as decision No. 1/8-3) determined that the legal successor of property rights and obligations of the bodies exercising on the territory of the Donetsk People's Republic on the day of the admission of Donetsk to the Russian Federation of the People's Republic and the formation of a new subject within the Russian Federation, the powers assigned by the legislation of the Russian Federation to the powers of local self-government bodies are exercised by the administration of the urban district of Donetsk of the Donetsk People's Republic (hereinafter referred to as the administration of the urban district) - in terms of the property necessary to ensure its activities, as well as other property that is in the municipal ownership of the municipal formation of the urban district of Donetsk of the Donetsk People's Republic (treasury, property transferred to operational management, economic management).
Clause 3.1.11 of the Regulations on the Department of Municipal Property of the Administration of the Donetsk City District (hereinafter referred to as the Department of Municipal Property), approved by the decision of the Donetsk City Council of the Donetsk People's Republic dated 01/06/2024 No. 1/14-5, determines that the Department of Municipal Property carries out work on the transfer of ownerless (escheated) real estate to the municipal property of the Donetsk City District.
The procedure for identifying, recording and accepting ownerless real estate into the municipal ownership of the municipality of the Donetsk City District of the Donetsk People's Republic is determined by the procedure for identifying, accounting and accepting ownerless real estate into the municipal ownership of the municipality of the Donetsk City District of the Donetsk People's Republic approved by the decision of the Donetsk City Council of the Donetsk People's Republic dated 07/04/2024 No. 1/32-4 (hereinafter referred to as - Procedure No. 1/32-4), which was developed in accordance with the Constitution of the Russian Federation, the Civil Code of the Russian Federation, Federal Law dated October 6, 2023 No. 131-FZ “On the general principles of organizing local self-government in the Russian Federation” (hereinafter referred to as Law No. 131-FZ), Resolution No. 2474, order of the Federal Service for State Registration, Cadastre and Cartography of the Russian Federation dated 03/15/2023 No. P/0086 “On establishing the Procedure for registering ownerless real estate” (hereinafter referred to as Order No. P/0086), Law of the Donetsk People’s Republic dated 08/14/2024 No. 468-PNS “On local self-government in the Donetsk People’s Republic” (hereinafter referred to as Law No. 468-PNS), Decree of the Acting Head of the Donetsk People's Republic dated May 10, 2023 No. 140 “On the peculiarities of acquiring the right of state and municipal ownership of ownerless real estate on the territory of the Donetsk People's Republic” (hereinafter referred to as Decree No. 140).
According to clause 2.1 of Section II of Order No. 1/32-5, the administration of the city district, represented by the department of municipal property, independently carries out actions to identify, record and accept into municipal ownership ownerless real estate located on the territory of the urban district of Donetsk. Information about real estate that has signs of ownerlessness may be received by the municipal property department from individuals and legal entities, including public authorities, who have become aware of such property.
Clause 2.2 of Section I of Procedure No. 1/32-5 stipulates that the Procedure applies to real estate that has the following characteristics of ownerless property: does not have an owner; whose owner is unknown; the owner renounced ownership.
In accordance with clause 25, part 1, art. 16 of the Federal Law of October 6, 2003 No. 131-FZ “On the General Principles of the Organization of Local Self-Government in the Russian Federation” (hereinafter referred to as Law No. 131-FZ), issues of local importance of the municipal district also include the approval of improvement rules. An ownerless real estate property may be a violation of the improvement rules of the relevant municipal district.
