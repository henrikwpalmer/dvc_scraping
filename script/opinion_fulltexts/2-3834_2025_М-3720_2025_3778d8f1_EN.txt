By virtue of Part 1 of Art. 225 of the Civil Code of the Russian Federation, ownerless is a thing that does not have an owner or the owner of which is unknown or, unless otherwise provided by law, the owner of which has renounced the right of ownership.
According to Part 1 of Art. 236 of the Civil Code of the Russian Federation, a citizen or legal entity may renounce the right of ownership of property belonging to him by declaring this or taking other actions that definitely indicate his removal from ownership, use and disposal of property without the intention of retaining any rights to this property.
Based on Art. 226 of the Civil Code of the Russian Federation, movable things abandoned by the owner or otherwise abandoned by him for the purpose of renouncing the right of ownership of them (abandoned things) may be turned into their property by other persons in the manner prescribed by paragraph 2 of this article. Other abandoned things become the property of the person who took possession of them if, at the request of this person, they are recognized by the court as ownerless.
Thus, since the owner of the above-mentioned vehicle renounced the rights to his property, which was confirmed in the case materials, neither he nor other interested parties applied to receive the vehicle during a long period of its presence in a specialized parking lot; neither the owner nor other interested parties applied to the traffic police authorities with applications to search for the vehicle, having actually renounced their rights to the vehicle, which is documented in the case materials; and the applicant actually took possession of the above-mentioned vehicle, the court considers it possible to recognize the above-mentioned vehicle as ownerless, transferring it into the ownership of the Russian Federation, since the written materials of the case and the evidence examined by the court during the consideration of the case, in the opinion of the court, reliably confirmed the refusal of the owner of the vehicle from the rights to movable property belonging to him.
Other persons did not declare their rights in relation to the disputed property.

EB (Ukraine), wanted, prohibited, not encumbered, not listed in the public registry regarding the pledge of movable property.
Requirements stated for a VAZ-2106 vehicle in a beige body, registration
