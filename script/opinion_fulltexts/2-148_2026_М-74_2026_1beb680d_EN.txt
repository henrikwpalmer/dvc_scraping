Part 2 of Article 290 of the Code of Civil Procedure of the Russian Federation stipulates that an application for recognition of ownership of an ownerless immovable property is submitted to the court at its location by the body authorized to manage municipal property.
According to Article 4 of the Federal Constitutional Law of the Russian Federation dated

Regulatory legal acts of the Donetsk People's Republic are valid on the territory of the Donetsk People's Republic until the end of the transition period or until the adoption of the corresponding regulatory legal act of the Russian Federation and (or) the regulatory legal act of the Donetsk People's Republic.

DD.MM.YYYY
