According to Article 4 of the Federal Constitutional Law of the Russian Federation of October 4, 2022 No. 5-FKZ, “On the admission into the Russian Federation of the Donetsk People's Republic and the formation within the Russian Federation of a new subject - the Donetsk People's Republic” (hereinafter referred to as Law No. 5-FKZ), legislative and other regulatory legal acts of the Russian Federation are valid on the territory of the Donetsk People's Republic from the date of admission of the Donetsk People's Republic to the Russian Federation of the People's Republic and the formation of a new subject within the Russian Federation, unless otherwise provided by this Federal Constitutional Law.
Regulatory legal acts of the Donetsk People's Republic are valid on the territory of the Donetsk People's Republic until the end of the transition period or until the adoption of the corresponding regulatory legal act of the Russian Federation and (or) the regulatory legal act of the Donetsk People's Republic.
According to Part 7 of Article 12 of Law No. 5-FKZ, management of property located on the territory of the Donetsk People's Republic, which has signs of ownerlessness and is not classified as federal property, the property of a constituent entity of the Russian Federation or municipal property, is carried out by the authorities of the Donetsk People's Republic in the manner established by the Government of the Russian Federation, unless otherwise provided by this Federal Constitutional Law. The procedure for recognizing such property as ownerless, established by the regulatory legal acts of the Donetsk People's Republic, is applied until January 1, 2028, in agreement with the federal executive body exercising the functions of managing state property.
According to Part 1 of Art. 225 of the Civil Code of the Russian Federation, an ownerless thing is a thing that does not have an owner or the owner of which is unknown, or a thing for which the owner has renounced the right of ownership.
The procedure for accepting ownerless real estate into municipal ownership is regulated by Resolution of the People's Council dated March 21, 2024 No. 66-RZ “On the peculiarities of identifying, using and recognizing the right of municipal property of municipalities of the Donetsk People's Republic to residential premises with signs of ownerless property located on the territory of the Donetsk People's Republic.”
As follows from Art. 2 of the Resolution, signs of ownerless property in relation to residential premises are: failure to pay rent for residential premises and utilities for one year, lack of information about registered ownership, non-use of residential premises.

According to paragraph 3 of Art. 225 of the Civil Code of the Russian Federation, ownerless immovable things are accepted for registration by the body carrying out state registration of rights
for real estate, at the request of the local government body in whose territory they are located.
As corresponds to paragraph 5 of Art. 60 of the Law of the Donetsk People's Republic of August 14, 2023 "On Local Self-Government" No. 468-IINS, local government bodies formed in accordance with the legislation of the Russian Federation are the legal successors of the bodies that exercised on the territory of the Donetsk People's Republic on the day of its admission to the Russian Federation and the formation of a new subject within the Russian Federation the powers attributed by the legislation of the Russian Federation to the powers of local bodies self-government in terms of: 1) property rights and obligations of such bodies; 2) contractual obligations, as well as other documents concluded by such bodies.
Guided by paragraph 1 of Article 10 of the People's Council Resolution No. 66-RZ of March 21, 2024, after three months from the date of registration of the residential premises as ownerless by the territorial body of the federal executive body carrying out state cadastral registration, state registration of rights, maintaining the Unified State Register of Real Estate, the authorized body applies to the court with an application for recognition of the right of municipal property of the municipality to this residential premises.
The body authorized to manage municipal property may apply to the court to recognize the right of ownership of ownerless real estate in a special proceeding, as defined in clause 6, part 1, art. 262 Code of Civil Procedure of the Russian Federation.
