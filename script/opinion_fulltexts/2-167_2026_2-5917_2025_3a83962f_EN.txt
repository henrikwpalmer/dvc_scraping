The court, having examined the case materials, came to the conclusion that the demands of the Administration of the Makeyevka Urban District must be satisfied on the following grounds:
According to clause 6, part 1, art. 262 of the Code of Civil Procedure of the Russian Federation, by way of special proceedings, the court considers cases of recognition of the right of municipal ownership of an ownerless immovable property.
According to Part 1 of Art. 225 of the Civil Code of the Russian Federation, ownerless is a thing that does not have an owner or the owner of which is unknown, or, unless otherwise provided by law, the ownership of which the owner has refused.
Part 3 of Art. 218 of the Civil Code of the Russian Federation provides that a person can acquire ownership of property that does not have an owner, property whose owner is unknown, or property that the owner has abandoned or to which he has lost ownership on other grounds provided by law.

According to Article 4 of the Federal Constitutional Law of the Russian Federation dated October 4, 2022 No. 5-FKZ, “On the admission into the Russian Federation of the Donetsk People's Republic and the formation within the Russian Federation of a new subject - the Donetsk People's Republic,” legislative and other regulatory legal acts of the Russian Federation are valid on the territory of the Donetsk People's Republic from the date of admission to the Russian Federation of the Donetsk People's Republic and the formation of a new entity within the Russian Federation subject, unless otherwise provided by this Federal Constitutional Law.
Regulatory legal acts of the Donetsk People's Republic are valid on the territory of the Donetsk People's Republic until the end of the transition period or until the adoption of the corresponding regulatory legal act of the Russian Federation and (or) the regulatory legal act of the Donetsk People's Republic.
According to Part 7 of Article 12 of Law No. 5-FKZ, management of property located on the territory of the Donetsk People's Republic, which has signs of ownerlessness and is not classified as federal property, the property of a constituent entity of the Russian Federation or municipal property, is carried out by the authorities of the Donetsk People's Republic in the manner established by the Government of the Russian Federation, unless otherwise provided by this Federal Constitutional Law. The procedure for recognizing such property as ownerless, established by the regulatory legal acts of the Donetsk People's Republic, is applied until January 1, 2028, in agreement with the federal executive body exercising the functions of managing state property.
In accordance with Art. 10 of the Law of the Donetsk People's Republic "On the peculiarities of identifying, using and recognizing the right of municipal property of municipalities of the Donetsk People's Republic to residential premises with signs of ownerless property located on the territory of the Donetsk People's Republic" dated 03.21.2024 No. 66-RZ after three months from the date of designation of the residential premises as ownerless by the territorial body of the federal executive body carrying out state cadastral registration, state registration of rights, maintenance of the Unified State Register of Real Estate, the authorized body applies to the court with an application for recognition of the right of municipal property of a municipal entity to this residential premises in the manner prescribed by the legislation of the Russian Federation.
In accordance with Art. 3 of Law 66-RZ, local self-government bodies of the Donetsk People's Republic, represented by bodies authorized by them (hereinafter referred to as authorized bodies), independently carry out actions to identify, record and accept residential premises that have signs of ownerless property into the municipal property of the municipalities of the Donetsk People's Republic, on the territory of which they are located, on the grounds provided for by law.
In accordance with Part 1 of Art. 4 of Law 66-RZ, information about residential premises that have signs of ownerlessness can come from individuals and legal entities, including public authorities, who have become aware of such property.
Thus, when carrying out the functions of managing property that has signs of ownerlessness, the District Administration performed the functions of identifying, recording and accepting ownerless things into municipal ownership in accordance with the requirements of Procedure 116.
In accordance with clause 2.2 of Order 116, the administrations of districts and cities of the Donetsk People's Republic independently carry out actions to identify, record and accept ownerless real estate into the municipal ownership of the administrative-territorial units of the Donetsk People's Republic on the territory of which they are located.
In accordance with clause 2.3 of Procedure 116, information about immovable things that have signs of being ownerless can be received by the City Administration from legal entities and individuals who have become aware of property located on the territory of the city of Makeyevka, which does not have an owner or the owner of which is unknown.
In accordance with paragraphs. 2.9.3. And. 2.9. Order 116 The city administration carried out all the prescribed measures to identify immovable property that has signs of being ownerless and falls under the Standard Procedure.
