According to Part 3 of Art. 263 of the Code of Civil Procedure of the Russian Federation, if, when filing an application or considering a case in a special proceeding, it is established that there is a dispute about the right within the jurisdiction of the court, the court issues a ruling to leave the application without consideration, in which it explains to the applicant and other interested parties their right to resolve the dispute in the manner of claim proceedings.
Within the meaning of this norm, the absence of a dispute about the law is a prerequisite for the application of procedural rules of special proceedings. If, when filing an application or during the consideration of a case, the presence of a substantive legal dispute is revealed, then its consideration should take place according to other procedural rules - the rules of claim proceedings.
The court found that the establishment of the above fact entails for the applicant the recognition of ownership of non-real estate.
In this situation, taking into account the nature of the stated requirements, objections to satisfying the requirements of the interested party
Full name1
, the court comes to the conclusion that it has left without consideration the application of the administration of the Donetsk urban district to recognize the right of municipal ownership of ownerless real estate.
The court also considers it necessary to explain to the applicant that in order to resolve this issue, one should apply to the court with a statement of claim according to the rules of jurisdiction provided for in Chapter 3 of the Code of Civil Procedure of the Russian Federation.
