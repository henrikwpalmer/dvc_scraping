According to Article 4 of the Federal Constitutional Law of the Russian Federation of October 4, 2022 No. 5-FKZ “On the admission into the Russian Federation of the Donetsk People's Republic and the formation within the Russian Federation of a new subject - the Donetsk People's Republic” (hereinafter referred to as Law No. 5-FKZ), legislative and other regulatory legal acts of the Russian Federation are valid on the territory of the Donetsk People's Republic from the date of admission of the Donetsk People's Republic to the Russian Federation Republic and the formation of a new subject within the Russian Federation, unless otherwise provided by this Federal Constitutional Law, that is, from September 30, 2022.
Regulatory legal acts of the Donetsk People's Republic are valid on the territory of the Donetsk People's Republic until the end of the transition period or until the adoption of the corresponding regulatory legal act of the Russian Federation and (or) the regulatory legal act of the Donetsk People's Republic.
In accordance with Part 6 of Art. 11 of Law No. 5-FKZ in the territories of newly formed municipalities until the completion of the formation of local self-government bodies, the powers of local government bodies and local government officials are exercised by the bodies and officials who exercised in these territories on the day of the admission of the Donetsk People's Republic to the Russian Federation and the formation of a new subject within the Russian Federation the powers classified by the legislation of the Russian Federation as the powers of local self-government bodies.
According to Part 7 of Article 12 of Law No. 5-FKZ, management of property located on the territory of the Donetsk People's Republic, which has signs of ownerlessness and is not classified as federal property, the property of a constituent entity of the Russian Federation or municipal property, is carried out by the authorities of the Donetsk People's Republic in the manner established by the Government of the Russian Federation, unless otherwise provided by this Federal Constitutional Law. The procedure for recognizing such property as ownerless, established by the regulatory legal acts of the Donetsk People's Republic, is applied until January 1, 2028, in agreement with the federal executive body exercising the functions of managing state property.
In accordance with paragraph 1 of Article 225 of the Civil Code (hereinafter referred to as the Civil Code) of the Russian Federation, an ownerless thing is a thing that does not have an owner or the owner of which is unknown or, unless otherwise provided by law, the right of ownership to which the owner has refused.
According to clause 3 of Art. 225 of the Civil Code of the Russian Federation, ownerless real estate is accepted for registration by the body that carries out state registration of rights to real estate, upon application from the local government body in whose territory they are located.
Paragraph 3 of Article 218 of the Civil Code of the Russian Federation establishes that in cases and in the manner provided for by this Code, a person may acquire ownership of property that does not have an owner, of property whose owner is unknown, or of property that the owner has abandoned or to which he has lost ownership on other grounds provided by law.
By virtue of the provisions of paragraphs 1 and 2 of Art. 130 of the Civil Code of the Russian Federation, immovable things (real estate, real estate) include land plots, subsoil plots and everything that is firmly connected to the land, that is, objects whose movement without disproportionate damage to their purpose is impossible, including buildings, structures, unfinished construction objects. Items not related to real estate, including money and securities, are recognized as movable property.
According to the requirements of Part 2 of Art. 290 of the Code of Civil Procedure of the Russian Federation, an application for recognition of ownership of an ownerless immovable property is submitted to the court at its location by the body authorized to manage municipal property or property owned by the federal city of Moscow, St. Petersburg or Sevastopol.
If the body authorized to manage the relevant property applies to the court before the expiration of a year from the date of registration of the immovable thing by the body carrying out state registration of rights to real estate, the judge refuses to accept the application and the court terminates the proceedings.
In accordance with Part 2 of Art. 293 of the Code of Civil Procedure of the Russian Federation, the court, having recognized that the immovable thing does not have an owner or the owner of the immovable thing is unknown, and it was registered in the prescribed manner, makes a decision to recognize the right of municipal property or the property of the federal city of Moscow, St. Petersburg or Sevastopol to this thing.
According to the legal position set out in paragraph 59 of the resolution of the Plenum of the Supreme Court of the Russian Federation No. 10, the Plenum of the Supreme Arbitration Court of the Russian Federation No. 22 of April 29, 2010 “On some issues arising in judicial practice when resolving disputes related to the protection of property rights and other property rights,” unless otherwise provided by law, a claim for recognition of a right is subject to satisfaction if the plaintiff provides evidence of the emergence of the corresponding right.
Decree of the Acting Head of the Donetsk People's Republic dated May 10, 2023 No. 140 (hereinafter referred to as Decree No. 140 dated 05.10.2023) established the specifics of acquiring state and municipal property rights to ownerless real estate on the territory of the Donetsk People's Republic.
According to clause 1.1 of Decree No. 140 dated 10.05.2023, the body and officials of the Donetsk People's Republic exercising the powers classified by the legislation of the Russian Federation as the powers of local government bodies, before the completion of the formation of local government bodies, the local government body of the municipality in the Donetsk People's Republic, authorized to manage municipal property, has the right to apply to the court with a demand for recognition of the right municipal ownership of ownerless buildings, structures (except for linear objects), premises, objects of unfinished construction that do not have an owner or the owner of which is unknown or the owner of which has refused ownership, located on the territory of such a municipal entity, after three months from the date of their registration as ownerless real estate by the body carrying out state registration of rights to real estate, unless otherwise provided by this Decree.
The procedure for accepting ownerless real estate into municipal ownership is regulated by Resolution of the People's Council dated March 21, 2024 No. 66-RZ “On the peculiarities of identifying, using and recognizing the right of municipal property of municipalities of the Donetsk People's Republic to residential premises with signs of ownerless property located on the territory of the Donetsk People's Republic.”
As follows from Art. 2 of the Resolution, the signs of ownerless property in relation to residential premises are: 1) failure to pay for residential premises and utilities within one year before the date of identification of residential premises that have signs of ownerlessness; 2) lack of information about the registered ownership of residential premises in the Unified State Register of Real Estate; 3) non-use of residential premises, which, among other things, creates a threat to its safety (or an apartment building if the premises is an apartment, a room in an apartment building), safety for the life and health of other persons (including the rights holders of other premises in an apartment building if the premises is an apartment, a room in an apartment building).
In accordance with Art. 10 of the Resolution, after three months from the date of registration of the residential premises as ownerless by the territorial body of the federal executive body carrying out state cadastral registration, state registration of rights, maintenance of the Unified State Register of Real Estate, the authorized body applies to the court with an application for recognition of the right of municipal ownership of the municipal formation to this residential premises in the manner prescribed by the legislation of the Russian Federation. Recognition of ownership of ownerless movable things located in residential premises is carried out in the manner prescribed by the Law of the Donetsk People's Republic of February 13, 2024 No. 52-RZ “On the peculiarities of regulation of property rights in relation to ownerless movable things located on real estate objects located on the territory of the Donetsk People's Republic.”

By Decree of the Head of the Donetsk People's Republic No. 116 of April 28, 2021. The Standard Procedure for identifying, recording and accepting ownerless real estate and escheatable property into municipal ownership (hereinafter referred to as the Standard Procedure) has been approved, according to clause 3.1 of which, after three months from the date of registration of the ownerless real estate, the administration is obliged to file a lawsuit to recognize the right of municipal ownership of this thing.
According to clause 3.2 of the Standard Procedure, if before the acceptance of an ownerless immovable property into municipal ownership, a person (persons) who considers themselves to be its owner appears, it is the responsibility of such person to prove ownership in court.
During the court hearing, the person who considered himself the owner of the real estate specified in the application did not appear.
The court considers that the above-mentioned real estate was legally registered as ownerless, the Administration of the city of Snezhnoye of the Donetsk People's Republic met the conditions stipulated by the current legislation for identifying and recording ownerless real estate, and after three months from the date of registration of the ownerless real estate, it went to court with this application.
Having assessed the evidence collected in the case according to the rules of Art. 56 of the Code of Civil Procedure of the Russian Federation, the court comes to the conclusion that the stated requirements for recognition of the right of municipal ownership of an ownerless real estate property are justified, since the application was submitted by the body authorized to manage municipal property, the specified real estate object does not have an owner, was registered as an ownerless real estate object in the prescribed manner, was registered with the body carrying out state registration of the right to real estate property as ownerless for more than three months, and during this time, as well as during the trial, the owner’s right to this object was not declared by anyone.
Taking into account the above, the court comes to the conclusion that the application for recognition of the right of municipal ownership of an ownerless immovable property is satisfied, on the grounds stated above.
