According to Part 7 of Article 12 of Law No. 5-FKZ, management of property located on the territory of the Donetsk People's Republic, which has signs of ownerlessness and is not classified as federal property, the property of a constituent entity of the Russian Federation or municipal property, is carried out by the authorities of the Donetsk People's Republic in the manner established by the Government of the Russian Federation, unless otherwise provided by this Federal Constitutional Law. According to Part 1 of Art. 225 of the Civil Code of the Russian Federation, an ownerless thing is a thing that does not have an owner or the owner of which is unknown, or a thing for which the owner has renounced the right of ownership.
The procedure for accepting ownerless real estate into municipal ownership is regulated by Resolution of the People's Council dated March 21, 2024 No. 66-RZ “On the peculiarities of identifying, using and recognizing the right of municipal property of municipalities of the Donetsk People's Republic to residential premises with signs of ownerless property located on the territory of the Donetsk People's Republic.”
As follows from Art. 2 of the Resolution, signs of ownerless property in relation to residential premises are: failure to pay rent for residential premises and utilities for one year, lack of information about registered ownership, non-use of residential premises.

Based on what was presented, in the opinion of the court, signs of ownerless property, lack of actual use and rights to the apartment were established.
According to paragraph 3 of Art. 225 of the Civil Code of the Russian Federation, ownerless real estate is accepted for registration by the body that carries out state registration of rights to real estate, upon application from the local government body in whose territory they are located.
As corresponds to paragraph 5 of Art. 60 of the Law of the Donetsk People's Republic dated
DD.MM.YYYY
“On Local Self-Government” No. 468-IINS, local self-government bodies formed in accordance with the legislation of the Russian Federation are the legal successors of the bodies that exercised on the territory of the Donetsk People's Republic on the day of its admission to the Russian Federation and the formation of a new subject within the Russian Federation the powers classified by the legislation of the Russian Federation as the powers of local self-government bodies in terms of: 1) property rights and obligations of such bodies; 2) contractual obligations, as well as other documents concluded by such bodies.
Guided by paragraph 1 of article 10 of the Resolution of the People's Council of
DD.MM.YYYY
No. 66-RZ, after three months from the date of registration of the residential premises as ownerless by the territorial body of the federal executive body carrying out state cadastral registration, state registration of rights, maintenance of the Unified State Register of Real Estate, the authorized body applies to the court with an application for recognition of the right of municipal ownership of the municipality to this residential premises.
The body authorized to manage municipal property may apply to the court to recognize the right of ownership of ownerless real estate in a special proceeding, as defined in clause 6, part 1, art. 262 Code of Civil Procedure of the Russian Federation.
Taking into account the circumstances established by the court and the evidence examined, assessed in their totality in accordance with the requirements of Art. 67 of the Code of Civil Procedure of the Russian Federation, taking into account that the local government body has the right to submit an application for registration of this property as ownerless, and subsequently for recognition of the right of municipal property to it, taking into account that the legal owners of real estate have not been established by the court, the property is registered as ownerless
DD.MM.YYYY
, for the period that has elapsed since registration, no rights have been claimed to it, the court comes to the conclusion that the stated requirements have been satisfied.
