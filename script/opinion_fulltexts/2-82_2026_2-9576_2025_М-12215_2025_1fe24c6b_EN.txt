According to Part 3 of Article 218 of the Civil Code of the Russian Federation, in cases and in the manner provided for by this Code, a person may acquire ownership of property that does not have an owner, property whose owner is unknown, or property that the owner has abandoned or to which he has lost ownership on other grounds provided by law.
In accordance with Article 225 of the Civil Code of the Russian Federation, an ownerless thing is a thing that does not have an owner or the owner of which is unknown, or, unless otherwise provided by law, a property right to which the owner has refused.
In accordance with Part 3 of Article 225 of the Civil Code of the Russian Federation, ownerless real estate is accepted for registration by the body that carries out state registration of rights to real estate, upon application from the local government body in whose territory they are located.

A standard procedure has been established for identifying, recording and accepting ownerless real estate and escheatable property into municipal ownership (hereinafter referred to as the Standard Procedure).
According to clause 2.2. According to the standard procedure, the administrations of districts and cities of the Donetsk People's Republic independently carry out actions to identify, record and accept ownerless real estate into the municipal ownership of the administrative-territorial units of the Donetsk People's Republic on the territory of which they are located.
According to clause 2.9 of the Standard Procedure, within 10 calendar days from the date of discovery of an object of real estate that has signs of ownerlessness, an announcement is placed on the official website of the administration on the Internet about the identification of a real estate item in the relevant administrative-territorial unit of the Donetsk People's Republic that has signs of ownerlessness, and about the need for the appearance of a person (persons) who considers themselves its owner or has (have) ownership of it rights, contact the administration with documents excluding the possibility of considering the specified real estate object ownerless. If the summoned person(s) fail to appear, the specified immovable thing will, at the request of the administration, be registered with the body that carries out state registration of real rights to real estate and their encumbrances, as ownerless. Sends requests to confirm information about an identified immovable thing that has signs of ownerlessness and is subject to this Procedure to the executive body that carries out state registration of real rights to real estate and their restrictions (encumbrances), - about the existence of registered rights to immovable things that have signs of ownerlessness, and their restrictions (encumbrances), to the executive body that implements state policy and carries out functions according to the normative legal regulation, control and supervision in the field of land relations, implementation of land management, state registration and maintenance of the State Land Cadastre, land valuation, geodesy and cartography, - on the existence of registered rights to a land plot, if the immovable thing is integrally connected with the land plot; to the executive body implementing state policy in the field of taxation and customs - on the availability in the Unified State Register of Legal Entities and Individual Entrepreneurs of information about a legal entity or individual entrepreneur who is a possible owner of the property, as well as the Within 30 calendar days from the date of publication, organizes a commission inspection of an item that has signs of being ownerless (with an on-site visit), with the involvement of the necessary representatives of government bodies, legal entities (as agreed) and with the preparation of an inspection report of the object, which indicates the location, technical and other condition of the property, as well as other information. Photographs of the property may be attached to the inspection report of an item that has signs of being ownerless. information about payment for land at the established address (if the immovable thing is integrally connected with the land plot); to the Migration Service of the Ministry of Internal Affairs of the Donetsk People's Republic of an individual who has registered ownership of an immovable thing that has signs of being ownerless; to the State Property Fund of the Donetsk People's Republic - about the presence of this object in the Unified Register of State Property Objects and objects in respect of which temporary administration (operational management) has been introduced, as well as objects of other forms of ownership under state control; to the relevant department of the administration - about the presence of an object in municipal ownership; to state and municipal enterprises that provide services to legal entities and individuals in the field of housing and communal services - on fulfilling the burden of maintaining immovable property that has signs of being ownerless.
According to clause 2.11 of the Standard Procedure, after 30 calendar days from the date of publication of the announcement, in the event of the absence of a person (persons) who considers themselves the owner of an immovable thing, and after receiving from the authorities specified in subclause 2.9.3 of clause 2.9 of this Procedure, responses indicating the absence of the owner of an immovable thing that has signs of being ownerless, the administration: within 5 working days draws up an act on impossibility of identifying the owner of this property. Organizes, if necessary, in the prescribed manner, work on conducting a technical inventory of real estate that has signs of being ownerless, preparing technical documentation for this object, as well as assigning a postal address to it. If necessary, collects other documents confirming the presence of signs of ownerless things, including documents indicating the absence of residents in ownerless residential premises (inspection reports, extracts from house books, personal accounts, etc.). To register an immovable thing as ownerless, he issues an appropriate order and applies to the body that carries out state registration of real rights to real estate and their restrictions (encumbrances) with an application to register the immovable thing as ownerless.
According to Article 290 of the Code of Civil Procedure of the Russian Federation, an application to recognize a movable thing as ownerless is submitted to the court by the person who took possession of it at the place of residence or location of the applicant. An application for recognition of ownership of an ownerless immovable property is submitted to the court at its location by the body authorized to manage municipal property or property owned by the federal city of Moscow, St. Petersburg or Sevastopol.
According to Part 1 of Article 291 of the Code of Civil Procedure of the Russian Federation, the application for recognition of a movable thing as ownerless must indicate which movable thing is subject to recognition as ownerless, its main features must be described, as well as evidence indicating the owner’s renunciation of ownership of it, and evidence indicating that the applicant has taken possession of this thing.

According to Part 2 of Article 291 of the Code of Civil Procedure of the Russian Federation, the application of the body authorized to manage municipal property or property owned by the federal city of Moscow, St. Petersburg or Sevastopol for recognition of ownership of an ownerless immovable thing must indicate by whom, when the immovable thing was registered, and must also provide evidence indicating the absence of its owner.
