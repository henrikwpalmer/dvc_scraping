When studying the materials of the inheritance case, it was established that Bylych D.I. turned to the notary of the Yenakievo city notary district on the issue of registration of inheritance rights after the death of Bylych I.I., deceased
DD.MM.YYYY
, having provided title documents for the inherited property and documents confirming family relationships, however, it was explained to him that the submitted documents do not confirm the acceptance of the inheritance in the manner and on time established by law (case sheet 17, 32-49).
Thus, the stated circumstances indicate that there are no heirs who accepted the inheritance.
The provisions of Art. 1240 of the Civil Code of the Donetsk People's Republic provides that inheritance is carried out by will, by inheritance agreement and by law. Inheritance by law takes place when and insofar as it is not changed by a will, as well as in other cases established by this Code.
In accordance with Part 1 of Art. 1241 of the Civil Code of the Donetsk People's Republic, the inheritance includes things and other property that belonged to the testator on the day the inheritance was opened, including property rights and obligations.
According to Part 1 of Art. 1272 of the Civil Code of the Donetsk People's Republic, the heirs of the first priority according to the law are the children, spouse and parents of the testator.
In accordance with Art. 1272 Civil Code of the Donetsk People's Republic Bylich D.I. is the heir of the first priority by law.
In accordance with Part 1 of Art. 1282 of the Civil Code of the Donetsk People's Republic, in order to acquire an inheritance, the heir must accept it.
In accordance with Part 2 of Art. 1283 of the Civil Code of the Donetsk People's Republic is recognized, until otherwise proven, that the heir accepted the inheritance if he performed actions indicating the actual acceptance of the inheritance, in particular, if the heir: took possession or management of the inherited property; took measures to preserve the inherited property, protect it from encroachments or claims of third parties; made expenses at his own expense for the maintenance of the inherited property; paid the testator's debts at his own expense or received funds due to the testator from third parties.
Such actions, in particular, may include: moving the heir into the residential premises belonging to the testator or living in it on the day of opening of the inheritance, processing by the heir of the land plot, paying for utilities, reimbursement from the inherited property of expenses associated with the death of the testator and expenses for the protection and management of the inheritance, other actions for the ownership, use and disposal of inherited property.
These actions must be completed within the period for accepting the inheritance established by Art. 1284 of the Civil Code of the Donetsk People's Republic, that is, within six months from the date of opening of the inheritance.
The plaintiff does not have the opportunity to exercise his inheritance rights out of court, and therefore, the establishment of this fact has legal significance for the plaintiff, the emergence, change, termination of personal or property rights, in particular, the right to exercise inheritance rights, depends on it.
The norms of the Civil Code of the Donetsk People's Republic provide for certain actions that indicate the acceptance of an inheritance, including the acceptance of an inheritance by taking possession and managing inherited property (actual acceptance of an inheritance).
Analyzing the circumstances of the case and other evidence provided, the court comes to the conclusion that Bylich D.I. within six months from the date of opening of the inheritance, took possession and management of the inherited property, took measures to preserve the inherited property, protect it from encroachments or claims of third parties, made expenses at his own expense for the maintenance of the inherited property, i.e. performed actions indicating the actual acceptance of the inheritance, in accordance with the provisions of Part 2 of Art. 1283 of the Civil Code of the Donetsk People's Republic, there are no other heirs who accepted the inheritance, taking into account also the position of the defendant, who did not object to the satisfaction of the claims, believes that the demands of Bylich D.I. on establishing the fact of acceptance of the inheritance are justified and subject to satisfaction.
