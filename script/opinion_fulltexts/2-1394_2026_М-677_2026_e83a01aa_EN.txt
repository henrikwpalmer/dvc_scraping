Having studied the case materials, the court comes to the conclusion that the proceedings in the case are terminated due to the applicant’s refusal of the stated demands due to the following.
According to the provisions of Art. 39 of the Code of Civil Procedure of the Russian Federation, the plaintiff has the right to abandon the claim.
In accordance with Article 220 of the Code of Civil Procedure of the Russian Federation, the court terminates the proceedings if the plaintiff has abandoned the claim and the refusal is accepted by the court.
The court considers it possible to accept the applicant’s refusal of the claim, since the refusal of the claim does not contradict the law and does not violate the rights and legitimate interests of other persons.
Based on the above and guided by Art. 220, 221, 224, 225, 227 Code of Civil Procedure of the Russian Federation, court
