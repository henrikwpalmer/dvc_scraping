Having studied the arguments of the application and examined the written materials of the case in their entirety, the court comes to the following conclusions.
According to Part. 1 and 3 tbsp. 35 of the Constitution of the Russian Federation, the right to private property is protected by law; no one can be deprived of their property except by a court decision.
From paragraph 1 of Art. 1 of the Civil Code of the Russian Federation (hereinafter referred to as the Civil Code of the Russian Federation) it follows that civil legislation is based on the recognition of the equality of participants in the relations regulated by it, the inviolability of property, freedom of contract, the inadmissibility of arbitrary interference by anyone in private affairs, the need for the unhindered exercise of civil rights, ensuring the restoration of violated rights, and their judicial protection.
By virtue of paragraph 2 of Art. 1 of the Civil Code of the Russian Federation, citizens (individuals) and legal entities acquire and exercise their civil rights of their own will and in their own interest. They are free to establish their rights and obligations on the basis of the contract and to determine any terms of the contract that do not contradict the law.
In accordance with paragraph 1 of Art. 209 of the Civil Code of the Russian Federation, the owner has the rights to own, use and dispose of his property.
In Art. 210 of the Civil Code of the Russian Federation states that the owner bears the burden of maintaining the property he owns, unless otherwise provided by law or contract.
According to paragraph 3 of Art. 218 of the Civil Code of the Russian Federation, in cases and in the manner provided for by this Code, a person may acquire the right of ownership to property that does not have an owner, to property whose owner is unknown, or to property that the owner has abandoned or to which he has lost the right of ownership on other grounds provided by law.
An ownerless thing is a thing that does not have an owner or the owner of which is unknown or, unless otherwise provided by law, the owner of which has renounced the right of ownership, as provided for in Part 1 of Art. 225 Civil Code of the Russian Federation
As follows from Part 3 of Art. 225 of the Civil Code of the Russian Federation, ownerless real estate is accepted for registration by the body that carries out state registration of rights to real estate, upon application from the local government body in whose territory they are located. After a year has passed from the date of registration of an ownerless immovable property, and in the case of registration of a linear object, after three months from the date of registration, the body authorized to manage municipal property may apply to the court with a request to recognize the right of municipal ownership of this thing.
By virtue of the provisions of Art. 215 of the Civil Code of the Russian Federation, property owned by right of ownership to urban and rural settlements, as well as other municipal entities, is municipal property. On behalf of the municipality, the rights of the owner are exercised by local government bodies and the persons specified in Art. 125 of the Civil Code of the Russian Federation.
According to Part 1 of Art. 130 of the Civil Code of the Russian Federation, immovable things (real estate, real estate) include land plots, subsoil plots and everything that is firmly connected to the land, that is, objects whose movement without disproportionate damage to their purpose is impossible, including buildings, structures, unfinished construction objects. Real estate includes residential and non-residential premises, as well as parts of buildings or structures intended to accommodate vehicles (car spaces), if the boundaries of such premises, parts of buildings or structures are described in the manner established by the legislation on state cadastral registration.
From Part 3 of Art. 293 of the Civil Procedure Code of the Russian Federation (hereinafter - the Code of Civil Procedure of the Russian Federation) it follows that the court, having recognized that the immovable thing does not have an owner or the owner of the immovable thing is unknown and it has been registered in the prescribed manner, makes a decision to recognize the right of municipal ownership of this thing.
Thus, within the meaning of the stated legal norms, the registration of an object of real estate as ownerless, as well as the subsequent recognition of the body authorized to manage municipal property, the right of ownership of an ownerless real estate in court on the basis of Art. 225 of the Civil Code of the Russian Federation is possible only in the absence of legal claims of other persons to such property. At the same time, the absence of state registration of ownership of a disputed object cannot in itself be a basis for recognizing it as ownerless.
At the same time, in accordance with Part 7 of Article 12 of the Federal Constitutional Law of October 4, 2022 N 5-FKZ (as amended on December 25, 2023) “On the admission of the Donetsk People’s Republic into the Russian Federation and the formation of a new subject within the Russian Federation - the Donetsk People’s Republic,” management of property located on the territory of the Donetsk People’s Republic that has signs of being ownerless and not assigned to federal property, property of a constituent entity of the Russian Federation or municipal property, is carried out by the bodies of the Donetsk People's Republic in the manner established by the Government of the Russian Federation, unless otherwise provided by this Federal Constitutional Law. The procedure for recognizing such property as ownerless, established by the regulatory legal acts of the Donetsk People's Republic, is applied until January 1, 2028, in agreement with the federal executive body exercising the functions of managing state property.
In accordance with clause 1.1 of Decree of the Acting Head of the Donetsk People's Republic No. 140 dated 05/10/2023 “On the specifics of acquiring the right of state and municipal ownership of ownerless real estate on the territory of the Donetsk People's Republic” /hereinafter - Decree No. 140 dated 05/10/2023/ the body and officials of the Donetsk People's Republic exercising the powers assigned legislation of the Russian Federation to the powers of local self-government bodies, before the completion of the formation of local self-government bodies, the local government body of a municipal formation in the Donetsk People's Republic, authorized to manage municipal property, has the right to apply to the court with a demand for recognition of the right of municipal ownership of ownerless buildings, structures (except for linear objects), premises, objects of unfinished construction that do not have an owner or the owner of which is unknown or from the ownership of which the owner refused, located on the territory of such a municipal entity, after three months from the date of their registration as ownerless real estate by the body carrying out state registration of rights to real estate, unless otherwise provided by this Decree.
By Decree of the Acting Head of the Donetsk People's Republic of February 27, 2023 No. 64 “On some issues related to the management of property that has signs of ownerlessness, located on the territory of the Donetsk People's Republic, and the recognition of such property as ownerless” (hereinafter referred to as Decree No. 64), the Government of the Donetsk People's Republic is vested with the authority to establish (adopt) a procedure for recognizing property as ownerless, having signs of being ownerless and not classified as federal property, property of a constituent entity of the Russian Federation or municipal property located on the territory of the Donetsk People's Republic, with the exception of real estate in the form of residential premises, residential buildings, and outbuildings.
As part of the implementation of Decree No. 140, as well as Decree No. 64, by Decree of the Government of the Donetsk People's Republic of June 22, 2023 No. 45-5, the executive body of the Donetsk People's Republic authorized to manage state real estate established the State Property Fund of the Donetsk People's Republic and approved the Procedure for recognizing real estate located on the territory of the Donetsk People's Republic as ownerless, with the exception of residential houses, residential premises, garden houses and outbuildings intended to satisfy citizens' household and other needs (hereinafter referred to as the Procedure), which regulates legal relations and establishes the procedure for recognizing property on the territory of the Donetsk People's Republic as ownerless, as well as recognizing the right of state ownership of the Donetsk People's Republic to ownerless real estate.
Clause 7 of the Procedure establishes that no later than the next working day after receiving an application for the identification of an ownerless immovable property from the body that identified the object, the State Property Fund of the Donetsk People's Republic sends to the executive body of the Donetsk People's Republic implementing state policy in the field of mass communications information about the identified ownerless immovable property, with the aim of posting this information on the official website of the Donetsk People's Republic in the information - telecommunications network "Internet" to identify and inform property owners.
From the content of these norms it follows that the current legislation provides for the possibility of terminating the right of ownership of a particular property by the owner performing actions indicating his renunciation of his property right.
According to Art. 290 of the Code of Civil Procedure of the Russian Federation, an application to recognize a movable thing as ownerless is submitted to the court by the person who took possession of it at the place of residence or location of the applicant. An application for recognition of ownership of an ownerless immovable property is submitted to the court at its location by the body authorized to manage municipal property or property owned by the federal city of Moscow, St. Petersburg or Sevastopol.
According to Part 1 of Article 291 of the Code of Civil Procedure of the Russian Federation, an application to recognize a movable thing as ownerless must indicate which movable thing is subject to recognition as ownerless, its main features must be described, as well as provide evidence indicating that the owner has renounced ownership of it, and evidence indicating that the applicant has taken possession of this thing.
According to Part 4 of Art. 1 of Federal Law No. 218-FZ of July 13, 2015 “On State Registration of Real Estate”, state registration of rights is carried out by making a record in the Unified State Register of Real Estate of the right to real estate, information about which is included in the Unified State Register of Real Estate. State registration of the transfer of the right to a real estate object, restriction of the right to a real estate object, encumbrance of a real estate object or a transaction with a real estate object is carried out subject to the existence of state registration of the right to this object in the Unified State Register of Real Estate, unless otherwise established by federal law.
The grounds for the implementation of state cadastral registration and (or) state registration of rights are, in particular, acts issued by state authorities or local governments within the framework of their competence and in the manner established by the legislation in force at the place of publication of such acts at the time of their publication, and establishing the existence, emergence, transfer, termination of a right or restriction of a right and encumbrance of a property (clause 1, part 2, article 14 of Law 218-FZ dated July 13, 2015).
According to the Procedure for registering ownerless real estate, established by order of Rosreestr No. P/0086 dated March 15, 2023, registration of ownerless real estate is carried out by the federal executive body, which carries out the functions of developing state policy and legal regulation in the field of state cadastral registration of real estate and state registration of rights to real estate and transactions with it, its territorial bodies, executive authorities of the Republic of Crimea and the federal city of Sevastopol when transferring to them the powers of the rights registration body by resolutions of the Government of the Russian Federation in accordance with Part 8 of Art. 72 of Federal Law No. 218-FZ (hereinafter referred to as the rights registration authority). Buildings (structures), structures, premises, parking spaces, objects of unfinished construction (hereinafter referred to as real estate objects), information about which is included in the Unified State Register of Real Estate (hereinafter referred to as the Unified State Register of Real Estate) and which do not have owners or whose owners are unknown, or, unless otherwise provided by law, the owners of which have renounced their ownership rights, are accepted for registration. The decision to register a real estate property as ownerless is made no later than fifteen working days from the date of receipt of the application and necessary documents by the rights registration authority. Registration of a real estate property as ownerless is carried out by entering the relevant information into the Unified State Register of Real Estate in accordance with the procedure for maintaining the Unified State Register of Real Estate, approved in accordance with Part 7 of Art. 7 of Federal Law No. 218-FZ (clauses 2, 3, 12, 13).
Thus, the evidence examined by the court shows with sufficient completeness that the property located at the address: Russian Federation, Donetsk People's Republic, Yenakievo urban district, Krasnodarskaya street, house No. 10zh, NP lit R-2, has all the signs of ownerless property, since no contracts for utility services have been concluded, the premises have not been used for a long time, which indicates the actual refusal of the owner to own and use the above property.
Also, the evidence examined at the court hearing indicates that the applicant fulfilled all the necessary conditions for identifying and accounting for the said ownerless real estate in order to be able to recognize the right of municipal ownership of this property.
In such circumstances, taking into account that the specified real estate is ownerless, which was registered by the body carrying out state registration of rights to real estate, and the period prescribed by law from the date of its registration has expired, the court comes to the conclusion that the stated requirements of the Administration of the Yenakievo City District of the Donetsk People's Republic have been satisfied and the municipality of the Yenakievo City District of the Donetsk People's Republic has been recognized as having the right of municipal ownership of ownerless real estate property, built-in non-residential premises, located at:
<address>
