Currently, the city administration Mariupol DPR asks the court to recognize the right of municipal ownership of the above-mentioned ownerless real estate property and the court finds these claims valid on the following grounds:
According to Part 2 of Art. 290 of the Civil Procedure Code of the Russian Federation, an application for recognition of ownership of an ownerless immovable property is submitted to the court at its location by the body authorized to manage municipal property or property owned by the federal city of Moscow, St. Petersburg or Sevastopol.
According to Article 4 of the Federal Constitutional Law of the Russian Federation dated October 4, 2022 No. 5-FKZ “On the admission of the Donetsk People's Republic into the Russian Federation and the formation within the Russian Federation of a new subject - the Donetsk People's Republic” (hereinafter referred to as Law No. 5-FKZ), legislative and other regulatory legal acts of the Russian Federation are valid on the territory of the Donetsk People's Republic from the date of admission of the Donetsk People's Republic to the Russian Federation of the People's Republic and the formation of a new subject within the Russian Federation, unless otherwise provided by this Federal Constitutional Law.
Regulatory legal acts of the Donetsk People's Republic are valid on the territory of the Donetsk People's Republic until the end of the transition period or until the adoption of the corresponding regulatory legal act of the Russian Federation and (or) the regulatory legal act of the Donetsk People's Republic. According to Part 7 of Article 12 of Law No. 5-FKZ, management of property located on the territory of the Donetsk People's Republic, which has signs of ownerlessness and is not classified as federal property, the property of a constituent entity of the Russian Federation or municipal property, is carried out by the authorities of the Donetsk People's Republic in the manner established by the Government of the Russian Federation, unless otherwise provided by this Federal Constitutional Law; the procedure for recognizing such property as ownerless, established by the regulatory legal acts of the Donetsk People's Republic, is applied until January 1, 2028, in agreement with the federal executive body exercising the functions of managing state property.
Decree of the Acting Head of the Donetsk People's Republic dated 05/10/2023 No. 140 (hereinafter referred to as Decree No. 140 dated 05/10/2023) established the specifics of acquiring state and municipal property rights to ownerless real estate on the territory of the Donetsk People's Republic. According to clause 1.1 of Decree No. 140 dated May 10, 2023, the local government body of a municipal formation in the Donetsk People's Republic, authorized to manage municipal property, has the right to apply to the court with a demand for recognition of the right of municipal ownership of ownerless buildings, structures (except for linear objects), premises, objects of unfinished construction that do not have an owner or the owner of which is unknown or from the ownership of which the owner refused, located on the territory of such a municipal entity, after three months from the date of their registration as ownerless real estate by the body carrying out state registration of rights to real estate. By Decree of the Acting Head of the Donetsk People's Republic dated 02.27.2023 No. 64 “On some issues related to the management of property that has signs of ownerlessness, located on the territory of the Donetsk People's Republic, and the recognition of such property as ownerless” (hereinafter referred to as Decree dated 02.27.2023 No. 64), the Government of the Donetsk People's Republic is vested with the authority to establish (adoption) of the procedure for recognizing as ownerless property that has signs of ownerlessness and is not classified as federal property, the property of a constituent entity of the Russian Federation or municipal property located on the territory of the Donetsk People's Republic, with the exception of real estate objects in the form of residential premises, residential buildings, and outbuildings.

At the same time, the court considers it necessary to note its critical attitude to the consistency of B.A. Yakubenko’s arguments. representative by proxy Dudnik I.V. about the existence of a dispute about the right in the above stated by the administration of the city. Mariupol DPR requirements for recognition of municipal property rights on the following grounds:
According to the provisions of Art. 8 of the Civil Code of the Russian Federation, civil rights and obligations arise, inter alia, as a result of events with which the law or other legal act associates the onset of civil consequences.
By the Resolution of the People's Council of the Donetsk People's Republic dated March 21, 2024, in accordance with the Federal Law of the Russian Federation dated October 4, 2022 No. 5-FKZ, Law No. 66-RZ was adopted “On the specifics of identifying, using and recognizing the right of municipal property of municipalities of the Donetsk People’s Republic to residential premises with signs of ownerless property located on the territory of the Donetsk People’s Republic”, which the specifics of the procedure for identifying, recording and using residential premises with signs of ownerless property located on the territory of the Donetsk People's Republic, recognizing the right of municipal ownership of municipal formations of the Donetsk People's Republic to this property, as well as the procedure for disposing of this property have been established; the effect of this Law on the basis of the provisions of Part 2 of Art. 1 applies to residential premises in the form of residential buildings, apartments, rooms.
Based on the provisions of Art. 9 of the Law of the Donetsk People's Republic No. 66-RZ “On the specifics of identifying, using and recognizing the right of municipal ownership of municipal formations of the Donetsk People's Republic to residential premises with signs of ownerless property located on the territory of the Donetsk People's Republic”, the procedure for recognizing a residential premises as ownerless is terminated upon personal announcement of the owner of such premises and confirmation of ownership of such premises on the basis of the provisions provided for in clauses. “a” “b” clause 3 of Art. 5 of this Law of documents (Part 1); within 5 days from the date of confirmation of ownership of the residential premises, the authorized body makes a decision to terminate the procedure for recognizing the residential premises as municipal property and to deregister the residential premises from the register of identified ownerless real estate on the territory of the municipality (Part 2).
Thus, from the systematic interpretation of the above provisions of Art. 8 of the Civil Code of the Russian Federation and Art. 9 of the Law of the Donetsk People's Republic No. 66-RZ it follows that the possibility of terminating the procedure for turning an ownerless residential premises into municipal property on the territory of the Donetsk People's Republic can only be realized upon the personal appearance of the owner of this residential premises at the relevant local government body and after this owner presents the original title documents for this premises and a passport of a citizen of the Russian Federation; refusal to carry out the above-mentioned actions provided for by law to terminate the procedure for turning real estate into municipal property is evidence of the refusal of the owner of the disputed residential premises from the right of ownership to it within the meaning of the above provisions of Art. 8 of the Civil Code of the Russian Federation.
The court was not presented with admissible and relevant evidence of the execution of I.V. Dudnik. the above-mentioned legal requirements to terminate the procedure for applying for an apartment
<address>

Taking into account the above, the court does not see the existence of a dispute about the right in the city regulations declared by the administration. Mariupol DPR demands on the above grounds and does not see any grounds for satisfying the petition of B.A. Yakubenko. representative by proxy Dudnik I.V. about leaving a statement from the city administration Mariupol DPR without consideration for the specified reason.
