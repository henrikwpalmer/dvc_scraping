Having studied the case materials, the court comes to the following conclusion.
In accordance with paragraph 1 of Art. 225 of the Civil Code of the Russian Federation, ownerless is a thing that does not have an owner or the owner of which is unknown or, unless otherwise provided by law, the owner of which has renounced the right of ownership.
According to paragraph 1, clause 3, art. 225 of the Civil Code of the Russian Federation, ownerless real estate is accepted for registration by the body that carries out state registration of rights to real estate, upon application from the local government body in whose territory they are located.
Paragraph 2, paragraph 3, art. 225 of the Civil Code of the Russian Federation provides that after a year from the date of registration of an ownerless immovable property, and in the case of registration of a linear object, after three months from the date of registration, the body authorized to manage municipal property may apply to the court with a demand for recognition of the right of municipal ownership of this thing.
In accordance with paragraph 5 of part 2 of article 14 of the Federal Law of
DD.MM.YYYY
N 218-FZ “On State Registration of Real Estate” (hereinafter referred to as the Law on Registration), the grounds for state registration of rights are, among other things, judicial acts that have entered into legal force. Rights to real estate established by a court decision are subject to state registration in accordance with this Federal Law, that is, when the applicant applies to the rights registration authority, with an application for state registration of the right and the submission of other documents necessary for its implementation (Article 18 of the Registration Law).

In accordance with Article 10 of the Law of the Donetsk People's Republic "On the specifics of identifying, using and recognizing the right of municipal property of municipalities of the Donetsk People's Republic to residential premises with signs of ownerless property located on the territory of the Donetsk People's Republic" dated 03.21.2024 No. 66-RZ after three months from the date of designation of the residential premises as ownerless by the territorial body of the federal body executive power, which carries out state cadastral registration, state registration of rights, maintenance of the Unified State Register of Real Estate, the authorized body applies to the court with an application for recognition of the right of municipal property of a municipal entity to this residential premises in the manner prescribed by the legislation of the Russian Federation.
In accordance with subclause 3.1. paragraph 3 of the Decree of the Acting Head of the Donetsk People's Republic "On some issues related to the management of property that has signs of ownerlessness, located on the territory of the Donetsk People's Republic, and the recognition of such property as ownerless" dated 02.27.2023 No. 64 (hereinafter referred to as Decree 64), the administration of the city of Shakhtersk was the body for managing property that has signs of ownerlessness in accordance with the Rules, approved by Decree of the Government of the Russian Federation dated December 28, 2022 No. 2474 “On approval of the Rules for the management of bodies of the Donetsk People's Republic, Lugansk People's Republic, Zaporozhye region and Kherson region having signs of ownerlessness and not classified as federal property, property of a constituent entity of the Russian Federation or municipal property located on the territories of these constituent entities of the Russian Federation.”
Subclause 3.2. Clause 3 of Decree 64 determines that until 01/01/2028, in relation to the property specified in subclause 3.1 of this clause, the procedure for recognizing it as ownerless, established by Decree of the Head of the Donetsk People's Republic of 04/28/2021 No. 116 “On the identification, accounting and acceptance into municipal ownership of ownerless real estate and escheated property”, is applied. (hereinafter referred to as Procedure 116) taking into account the changes made.
Thus, when carrying out the functions of managing property that has signs of ownerlessness, the administration of the city of Shakhtersk performed the functions of identifying, recording and accepting ownerless things into municipal ownership in accordance with the requirements of Procedure 116.
In accordance with paragraph 2.2 of Order 116, the administrations of districts and cities of the Donetsk People's Republic independently carry out actions to identify, count and accept ownerless real estate into the municipal ownership of the administrative-territorial units of the Donetsk People's Republic on the territory of which they are located.
In accordance with paragraph 2.3 of Procedure 116, information about real estate that has signs of being ownerless can be received by the city administration from legal entities and individuals who have become aware of property located on the territory of the Shakhtarsky Municipal District, which does not have an owner or whose owner is unknown.
