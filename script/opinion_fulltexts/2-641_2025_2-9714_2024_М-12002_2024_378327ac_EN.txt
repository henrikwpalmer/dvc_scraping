Having examined the case materials, the court comes to the following conclusion.
According to Art. 4 of the Federal Constitutional Law of the Russian Federation of October 4, 2022 No. 5-FKZ, “On the admission into the Russian Federation of the Donetsk People’s Republic and the formation within the Russian Federation of a new subject - the Donetsk People’s Republic” (hereinafter referred to as Law No. 5-FKZ), legislative and other regulatory legal acts of the Russian Federation are valid on the territory of the Donetsk People’s Republic from the date of admission of the Donetsk People’s Republic to the Russian Federation and the formation of a new subject within the Russian Federation, unless otherwise provided by this Federal Constitutional Law.
Regulatory legal acts of the Donetsk People's Republic are valid on the territory of the Donetsk People's Republic until the end of the transition period or until the adoption of the corresponding regulatory legal act of the Russian Federation and (or) the regulatory legal act of the Donetsk People's Republic.
According to Part 7 of Art. 12 of Law No. 5-FKZ, management of property located on the territory of the Donetsk People's Republic, which has signs of ownerlessness and is not classified as federal property, the property of a constituent entity of the Russian Federation or municipal property, is carried out by the authorities of the Donetsk People's Republic in the manner established by the Government of the Russian Federation, unless otherwise provided by this Federal Constitutional Law. The procedure for recognizing such property as ownerless, established by the regulatory legal acts of the Donetsk People's Republic, is applied until January 1, 2028, in agreement with the federal executive body exercising the functions of managing state property.
According to Part 1 of Art. 225 of the Civil Code of the Russian Federation, an ownerless thing is a thing that does not have an owner or the owner of which is unknown, or a thing for which the owner has renounced the right of ownership.
The procedure for accepting ownerless real estate into municipal ownership is regulated by the Law of the Donetsk People's Republic dated March 21, 2024 No. 66-RZ "On the specifics of identifying, using and recognizing the right of municipal property of municipalities of the Donetsk People's Republic to residential premises that have signs of ownerless property located on the territory of the Donetsk People's Republic" (hereinafter referred to as the DPR Law dated 03/21/2024 No. 66-RZ).
As follows from Art. 2 Law of the DPR dated March 21, 2024 No. 66-RZ, signs of ownerless property in relation to residential premises are: failure to pay for residential premises and utilities within one year before the day of identification of residential premises that have signs of ownerlessness, lack of information about the registered ownership in the Unified State Register of Real Estate, non-use of residential premises.

The materials examined during the court hearing confirm, in the opinion of the court, the signs of ownerless property - until the day the residential premises were identified as having signs of ownerlessness, the lack of information about the registered ownership in the Unified State Register of Real Estate, the non-use of the residential premises.
According to paragraph 3 of Art. 225 of the Civil Code of the Russian Federation, ownerless immovable things are accepted for registration by the body carrying out state registration of rights to real estate, upon application from the local government body in whose territory they are located, in the manner prescribed by Rosreestr Order No. P/0086 dated March 15, 2023 “On establishing the Procedure for registering ownerless real estate.”
As established by paragraph 1 of Article 10 of the Law of the DPR dated March 21, 2024 No. 66-RZ), after three months from the date of registration of the residential premises as ownerless by the territorial body of the federal executive body carrying out state cadastral registration, state registration of rights, maintaining the Unified State Register of Real Estate, the authorized body applies to the court with an application for recognition of the right of municipal ownership of the municipality to this residential premises.
According to paragraph 5 of Art. 60 of the Law of the Donetsk People's Republic of August 14, 2023 "On Local Self-Government" No. 468-IINS, local government bodies formed in accordance with the legislation of the Russian Federation are the legal successors of the bodies that exercised on the territory of the Donetsk People's Republic on the day of its admission to the Russian Federation and the formation of a new subject within the Russian Federation the powers attributed by the legislation of the Russian Federation to the powers of local bodies self-government in terms of: 1) property rights and obligations of such bodies; 2) contractual obligations, as well as other documents concluded by such bodies.
By decision of the Torez City Council dated November 30, 2023 No. 39 “On issues of succession in the municipal formation of the Torez city district of the Donetsk People's Republic” it was determined that the successor to the administration of the city of Torez is the administration of the Torez city district of the Donetsk People's Republic.
The body authorized to manage municipal property may apply to the court to recognize the right of ownership of ownerless real estate in a special proceeding, as defined in clause 6, part 1, art. 262 Code of Civil Procedure of the Russian Federation.
Taking into account the circumstances established by the court and the evidence examined, assessed in their totality in accordance with the requirements of Art. 67 of the Code of Civil Procedure of the Russian Federation, taking into account that in relation to the real estate property on September 18, 2024, an entry was made in the Unified State Register of Registering it as ownerless, for the period that has elapsed from the moment of registration no rights to it have been declared, the court, taking into account the provisions of paragraph 1 of Article 10 of the DPR Law of March 21, 2024 No. 66-RZ, comes to the conclusion on satisfaction of the stated requirements and recognition of the right of municipal ownership of the municipality to this residential premises.
