According to the Federal Constitutional Law of 04.10.2022 No. 5-FKZ “On the admission of the Donetsk People’s Republic into the Russian Federation and the formation of a new entity within the Russian Federation - the Donetsk People’s Republic” (hereinafter referred to as FKZ No. 5), the Agreement between the Russian Federation and the Donetsk People’s Republic on the admission of the Donetsk People’s Republic to the Russian Federation and the formation of a new entity within the Russian Federation, The Donetsk People's Republic is considered admitted to the Russian Federation on September 30, 2022.
In accordance with Part 7 of Art. 12 FKZ No. 5, management of property located on the territory of the Donetsk People's Republic, which has signs of ownerlessness and is not classified as federal property, the property of a constituent entity of the Russian Federation or municipal property, is carried out by the authorities of the Donetsk People's Republic in the manner established by the Government of the Russian Federation, unless otherwise provided by this Federal Constitutional Law. The procedure for recognizing such property as ownerless, established by the regulatory legal acts of the Donetsk People's Republic, is applied until 01/01/2028 in agreement with the federal executive body performing the functions of managing state property.
In order to implement Part 7 of Art. 12 FKZ No. 5 adopted Decree of the Head of the Donetsk People's Republic dated May 10, 2023 No. 140 “On the specifics of acquiring the right of state and municipal property to ownerless real estate on the territory of the Donetsk People’s Republic”, which established that the body and officials of the Donetsk People’s Republic exercising the powers classified by the legislation of the Russian Federation as the powers of local self-government bodies, until the completion of the formation of local authorities self-government, a local government body of a municipal formation in the Donetsk People's Republic, authorized to manage municipal property, has the right to go to court with a demand for recognition of the right of municipal ownership of ownerless buildings, structures (except for linear objects), premises, objects of unfinished construction that do not have an owner or the owner of which is unknown or the owner of which has refused ownership, located on the territory of such a municipal formation, after three months from the date their registration as ownerless real estate by the body carrying out state registration of rights to real estate, unless otherwise provided by this Decree (clause 1.1 of Decree No. 140).
By Decree of the Acting Head of the Donetsk People's Republic of February 27, 2023 No. 64 “On some issues related to the management of property that has signs of ownerlessness, located on the territory of the Donetsk People’s Republic, and the recognition of such property as ownerless” (hereinafter referred to as Decree No. 64), the Government of the Donetsk People’s Republic is vested with the authority to establish (adopt) the procedure for recognition as ownerless property that has signs of being ownerless and not classified as federal property, property of a constituent entity of the Russian Federation or municipal property located on the territory of the Donetsk People's Republic.
According to sub. 3.1 and 3.2 of Decree No. 64, administrations of districts and cities of the Donetsk People's Republic are bodies for managing property that has signs of ownerlessness and is not classified as federal property, property of the Donetsk People's Republic or municipal property located on the territory of the Donetsk People's Republic, in accordance with the Rules regarding real estate in the form of residential premises, garden houses, outbuildings intended for the satisfaction of citizens of household and other needs.
Until 01/01/2028 in relation to the property specified in subparagraph. 3.1 of this paragraph, the procedure for recognizing it as ownerless is applied, established by Decree of the Head of the Donetsk People's Republic of April 28, 2021 No. 116 “On identifying, recording and accepting ownerless real estate and escheatable property into municipal ownership” (hereinafter referred to as Decree No. 116).
As part of the implementation of Decree No. 140, as well as Decree No. 64, Decree No. 116 approved the Standard Procedure for identifying, recording and accepting ownerless real estate and escheatable property into municipal ownership (hereinafter referred to as the Procedure), which regulates legal relations and establishes the procedure for recognizing property as ownerless on the territory of the Donetsk People's Republic, as well as recognizing the right of municipal ownership of ownerless real estate.
In accordance with clause 2.2 of the Procedure, the administrations of districts and cities of the Donetsk People's Republic (hereinafter referred to as the administrations) independently carry out actions to identify, record and accept ownerless real estate into the municipal ownership of the administrative-territorial units of the Donetsk People's Republic on the territory of which they are located.
Within 10 calendar days from the date of discovery of an object of real estate that has signs of ownerlessness, the administration places on the official website on the Internet an announcement about the identification of an immovable property in the relevant administrative-territorial unit of the Donetsk People's Republic that has signs of ownerlessness, and about the need for the appearance of a person (persons) who considers themselves its owner or has (have) rights to it, to contact the administration with documents excluding the possibility of considering the specified real estate object ownerless. If the summoned person(s) fail to appear, the specified immovable thing will, at the request of the administration, be registered with the body that carries out state registration of real rights to real estate and their encumbrances as ownerless (clause 2.9.1 of the Procedure).
Clause 2.9.3 of the Procedure provides that the administration sends requests to confirm information about an identified immovable property that has signs of being ownerless and is subject to this Procedure:
to the territorial body of the federal executive body carrying out state cadastral registration, state registration of rights, maintaining the Unified State Register of Real Estate - about the existence of registered rights to an immovable thing that has signs of being ownerless, and their restrictions (encumbrances), as well as about the presence of registered rights to a land plot, if the immovable thing is integrally connected with the land plot;
to the territorial body of the federal executive body exercising the functions of control and supervision over compliance with the legislation on taxes and fees, for the correctness of calculation, completeness and timely payment of taxes, fees and insurance contributions to the budgetary system of the Russian Federation - regarding information about the payment of land tax at the established address (if the immovable thing is integrally connected with the land plot);
to the territorial body of the federal executive body exercising the functions of developing and implementing state policy and legal regulation in the field of migration - regarding information about the place of residence of an individual who has registered ownership of an immovable thing that has signs of being ownerless;
to the territorial body of the federal executive body that carries out the functions of managing federal property, and to the executive body of the Donetsk People's Republic that ensures accounting of state property of the Donetsk People's Republic - about the presence or absence of an immovable property that has signs of being ownerless in the registers of federal property, state property of the Donetsk People's Republic (information about filing a declaration on property subject to registration in the register of federal property, or proposals for classifying an object as state property of the Donetsk People's Republic or municipal property or transferring such an object into state property of the Donetsk People's Republic or municipal property);
to the Ministry of Construction and Housing and Communal Services of the Russian Federation - about the availability of information regarding an immovable property that has signs of being ownerless, and (or) the land plot on which such a property is located, within the scope of competence in the established area;
to the relevant department of the administration - about the presence of an immovable property that has signs of being ownerless in the register of municipal property;
to state and municipal enterprises that provide services to legal entities and individuals in the field of housing and communal services - on fulfilling the burden of maintaining immovable property that has signs of being ownerless;
to the territorial body of the federal executive body exercising powers in the field of border activities - regarding information about the stay outside the territory of the Russian Federation during the last six months of an individual who has registered ownership of an immovable thing that has signs of being ownerless;
to the official website of the Federal Tax Service of the Russian Federation - about the availability in the Unified State Register of Legal Entities of information about a legal entity that is a possible owner of property (if it is necessary to establish the status of a legal entity).
According to clause 2.11 of the Procedure, after 30 calendar days from the date of publication of the announcement, in the event of the absence of a person (persons) who considers themselves the owner of an immovable thing, and after receiving responses from authorized bodies indicating the absence of the owner of an immovable thing that has signs of being ownerless, or that he is located outside the territory of the Russian Federation, the administration, within 5 working days, draws up a report on the impossibility of identifying the owner of this object real estate or its location outside the territory of the Russian Federation and failure to ensure the maintenance of such immovable property (subclause 2.11.1); organizes a commission inspection of an item that has signs of being ownerless (with an on-site visit), with the involvement of the necessary representatives of the executive bodies of the Donetsk People's Republic and territorial bodies of the federal executive authorities, organizations (as agreed) and with the drawing up of an appropriate act, which indicates the location, technical and other condition of the property, as well as other information. If free access to the interior of the real estate object being inspected is possible, the act reflects information about the internal state of such an object (subclause 2.11.4).
To register an immovable property as ownerless, the administration issues an appropriate order and applies to the territorial body of the federal executive body that carries out state cadastral registration, state registration of rights, maintaining the Unified State Register of Real Estate, with an application to register the immovable property as ownerless and other documents provided for by the current legislation (clause 2.12 of the Procedure).
In accordance with clause 3.1 of the Procedure, after three months from the date of registration of an ownerless real estate property, the administration is obliged to apply to the court with a claim for recognition of the right of municipal ownership of this thing.
Clause 3.2 of the Procedure establishes that if, before the acceptance of an ownerless immovable property into municipal ownership, a person (persons) who considers themselves to be its owner appears, it is the responsibility of such person to prove ownership in court.
In addition, on March 21, 2024, the People's Council of the Donetsk People's Republic adopted the Law of the Donetsk People's Republic No. 66-RZ "On the specifics of identifying, using and recognizing the right of municipal property of municipalities of the Donetsk People's Republic to residential premises that have signs of ownerless property located on the territory of the Donetsk People's Republic" (hereinafter referred to as DPR Law No. 66-RZ), which also established the specifics of the procedure for identifying, recording and the use of residential premises that have signs of ownerless property located on the territory of the Donetsk People's Republic, recognition of the right of municipal ownership of municipal formations of the Donetsk People's Republic to this property, as well as the procedure for disposing of this property.
In accordance with Art. 2 of DPR Law No. 66-RZ, signs of ownerless property in relation to residential premises are:
1) failure to pay for residential premises and utilities within one year before the day of identification of residential premises that have signs of ownerlessness;
2) lack of information about the registered ownership of residential premises in the Unified State Register of Real Estate;
3) non-use of residential premises, which, among other things, creates a threat to its safety (or an apartment building if the premises is an apartment, a room in an apartment building), safety for the life and health of other persons (including the rights holders of other premises in an apartment building if the premises is an apartment, a room in an apartment building).
In case of receiving information about residential premises that have signs of ownerless property, the authorized body in accordance with Art. 5 of DPR Law No. 66-RZ performs the following actions:
1) requests information on the official website of the Federal Tax Service of the Russian Federation (https://www.nalog.gov.ru/) - about the availability in the Unified State Register of Legal Entities of information about a legal entity that is a possible owner of residential premises (if it is necessary to establish the status of a legal entity);
2) sends requests to confirm information about identified residential premises that have signs of ownerlessness:
a) to the territorial body of the federal executive body carrying out state cadastral registration, state registration of rights, maintenance of the Unified State Register of Real Estate - about the existence of registered rights to residential premises that have signs of ownerlessness, and their restrictions (encumbrances), as well as about the existence of registered rights to a land plot, if the residential premises are integrally connected with the land plot;
b) to state and municipal enterprises that provide services to legal entities and individuals in the field of housing and communal services - on the fulfillment of the burden of maintaining residential premises that have signs of ownerlessness;
c) to the relevant bodies for accounting of state and municipal property, which maintain registers of federal property, state property of the Donetsk People's Republic and municipal property;
d) if necessary, to the civil registry authorities about the availability of an act record of the death of the last owner of a residential premises that has signs of ownerlessness, to other institutions, organizations, enterprises;
3) within 10 calendar days from the date of discovery of a residential premises that has signs of ownerlessness, places on the official website of the local government body on the Internet, as well as in accessible places at the entrance to the residential premises, an announcement about the identification of a residential premises that has signs of ownerlessness, and about the need for personal appearance before the authorized body of the person (persons) who considers themselves its owner or has (have) rights to it, within 30 calendar days from the date of placement of such an advertisement with the following documents:
a) a passport of a citizen of the Russian Federation or other identification document;
b) title documents for residential premises;
4) after 30 calendar days from the date of publication of the announcement, in the event of the absence of a person (persons) who considers themselves the owner of the residential premises, and after receiving information from the website (if necessary, obtaining such information) specified in paragraph 1 of this article, responses from the authorities specified in paragraph 2 of this article, indicating that the residential premises, which has signs of ownerless property, do not have an owner:
a) within 5 working days, draws up a report on the impossibility of identifying the owner of this residential premises;
b) organizes, if necessary, in the prescribed manner, the carrying out of cadastral work in relation to residential premises that have signs of being ownerless, as well as assigning a postal address to it;
c) if necessary, collects other documents confirming the presence of signs of ownerless property in the residential premises, including documents indicating the absence of residents in such residential premises (inspection reports, extracts from house books, personal accounts, etc.);
d) organizes a commission inspection of a residential premises that has signs of ownerlessness (with an on-site visit), with the involvement of the necessary representatives of the executive bodies of the Donetsk People's Republic and territorial bodies of the federal executive authorities, organizations (as agreed) and with the drawing up of an appropriate act, which indicates the details of the act, the date of the inspection, information about the persons involved, the location, technical and other condition of the residential premises, an inventory of furniture, household appliances and other movable property located in the residential premises, as well as other information indicating the presence of signs of ownerlessness of the residential premises.
By virtue of Art. 9 Law of the DPR No. 66-RZ, the procedure for recognizing ownerless residential premises as municipal property is terminated upon personal announcement of the owner of such premises and confirmation of ownership of such premises on the basis of the documents provided for in subsection. “a”, “b” clause 3 of Art. 5 of this Law.
Article 10 of DPR Law No. 66-RZ provides that after 3 months from the date of registration of a residential premises as ownerless by the territorial body of the federal executive body carrying out state cadastral registration, state registration of rights, maintenance of the Unified State Register of Real Estate, the authorized body applies to the court with an application for recognition of the right of municipal ownership of the municipality to this residential premises in the manner prescribed by the legislation of the Russian Federation.
By-laws of the Donetsk People's Republic regulating the issues of identifying, staging, accounting for ownerless property are aimed at resolving issues regarding the introduction of state/municipal management of ownerless real estate, ensuring their safety and determining the optimal way to manage them in accordance with established standards before the owner is declared or before the form of ownership of the said property is established in court.
In addition, by virtue of Part 2 of Art. 21.1 of the Federal Constitutional Law of October 4, 2022 No. 5-FKZ “On the admission of the Donetsk People’s Republic to the Russian Federation and the formation of a new subject within the Russian Federation - the Donetsk People’s Republic” (as amended by the Federal Constitutional Law of December 15, 2025 No. 4-FKZ), the ownership right of the Donetsk People’s Republic or the ownership right of its municipalities for residential premises, having signs of ownerless property, arises by force of law from the date of inclusion of such residential premises in the property register of a constituent entity of the Russian Federation or in the register of municipal property, but no later than 01/01/2030 and is subject to state registration in accordance with federal law.

According to Art. 12 of the Civil Code of the Russian Federation (hereinafter referred to as the Civil Code of the Russian Federation), the protection of civil rights is carried out through the recognition of rights.
As provided in paragraph 3 of Art. 218 of the Civil Code of the Russian Federation, in cases and in the manner provided for by this Code, a person may acquire the right of ownership to property that does not have an owner, to property whose owner is unknown, or to property that the owner has abandoned or to which he has lost the right of ownership on other grounds provided by law.
The provisions of Art. 225 of the Civil Code of the Russian Federation establishes that the ownerlessness of real estate presupposes the absence or unknownness of its owner, and also the condition for considering an application to recognize the property as ownerless is the absence of a dispute about the right.
In accordance with paragraph 1 of Art. 225 of the Civil Code of the Russian Federation, ownerless is a thing that does not have an owner or the owner of which is unknown or, unless otherwise provided by law, the right of ownership to which the owner has refused.
Based on the content of Article 225 of the Civil Code of the Russian Federation, ownerlessness of real estate presupposes the absence or unknownness of its owner, and the condition for considering an application to recognize property as ownerless is the absence of a dispute about the right. It is these facts that are included in the subject of proof in this category of cases and must be established in court.
Based on Part 2 of Art. 290 of the Code of Civil Procedure of the Russian Federation, an application for recognition of the right of municipal ownership of an ownerless immovable property is submitted to the court at its location by the body authorized to manage municipal property.
According to the legal position set out in paragraph 59 of the resolution of the Plenum of the Supreme Court of the Russian Federation No. 10, Plenum of the Supreme Arbitration Court of the Russian Federation No. 22 of April 29, 2010 “On some issues arising in judicial practice when resolving disputes related to the protection of property rights and other real rights,” unless otherwise provided by law, a claim for recognition of a right is subject to satisfaction if the plaintiff presents evidence of the emergence of the corresponding right.
The procedure for recognizing the ownership of an ownerless immovable property by the body authorized to manage municipal property is defined in Chapter 33 of the Code of Civil Procedure of the Russian Federation.
Registration of an object of real estate as ownerless, as well as subsequent recognition by the body authorized to manage municipal property of ownership of ownerless real estate in court on the basis of Article 225 of the Civil Code of the Russian Federation is possible only in the absence of legal claims of other persons to such property. At the same time, the absence of state registration of the ownership rights of a company or another person to a disputed object cannot in itself be a basis for declaring it ownerless.
According to Part 7 of Article 12 of Law No. 5-FKZ, management of property located on the territory of the Donetsk People's Republic, which has signs of ownerlessness and is not classified as federal property, the property of a constituent entity of the Russian Federation or municipal property, is carried out by the authorities of the Donetsk People's Republic in the manner established by the Government of the Russian Federation, unless otherwise provided by this Federal Constitutional Law. The procedure for recognizing such property as ownerless, established by the regulatory legal acts of the Donetsk People's Republic, is applied until January 1, 2028, in agreement with the federal executive body exercising the functions of managing state property.
Decree of the Acting Head of the Donetsk People's Republic dated May 10, 2023 No. 140 (hereinafter Decree No. 140) established the specifics of acquiring state and municipal property rights to ownerless real estate on the territory of the Donetsk People's Republic.
According to paragraph 1.2 of Decree No. 140, the executive body of the Donetsk People's Republic authorized to manage state property has the right to apply to the court with a demand for recognition of the right of state ownership of the objects defined in subparagraph 1.1 of this paragraph (with the exception of residential premises, outbuildings intended to satisfy citizens' household and other needs), after three months from the date of their registration as ownerless real estate by the body, carrying out state registration of rights to real estate.
