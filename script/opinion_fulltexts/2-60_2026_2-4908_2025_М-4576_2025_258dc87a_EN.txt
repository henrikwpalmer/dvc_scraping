In support of the stated requirements, it is stated that paragraph 3 of Article 218 of the Civil Code of the Russian Federation (hereinafter referred to as the Civil Code of the Russian Federation) establishes that in cases and in the manner provided for by the Civil Code of the Russian Federation, a person can acquire ownership of property that does not have an owner, property whose owner is unknown, or property that the owner has refused or to which he has lost ownership on other grounds provided by law.
In accordance with Article 225 of the Civil Code of the Russian Federation, an ownerless thing is a thing that does not have an owner or the owner of which is unknown or, unless otherwise provided by law, the right of ownership to which the owner has refused.
Ownerless real estate is accepted for registration by the body that carries out state registration of rights to real estate, upon application from the local government body in whose territory they are located. After a year has passed from the date of registration of an ownerless immovable property, the body authorized to manage municipal property may apply to the court with a request to recognize the right of municipal ownership of this thing.

DD.MM.YYYY

“On approval of the Rules for the management of bodies of the Donetsk People’s Republic, Lugansk People’s Republic, Zaporozhye region and Kherson region with signs of being ownerless and not classified as federal property, property of a constituent entity of the Russian Federation or municipal property, located on the territories of these constituent entities of the Russian Federation”, Decree of the Acting Head of the Donetsk People’s Republic dated May 10, 2023 No. 140 “On the Features acquisition of state and municipal property rights to ownerless real estate on the territory of the Donetsk People's Republic”, this Procedure is based on the order of the head of the administration (clause 1.4 of Procedure No. 622).
Clauses 2.1 and 2.2 of Order No. 622 provide that in accordance with Article 225 of the Civil Code of the Russian Federation, the administration of the city of Donetsk (hereinafter referred to as the administration) independently carries out actions to identify, record and accept ownerless real estate located on the territory of the Donetsk urban district into the municipal property of the Donetsk urban district.
Information about immovable things that have signs of being ownerless may be received by the administration from the administrations of the districts of the city of Donetsk, legal entities and individuals who have become aware of property located on the territory of the urban district of Donetsk, which does not have an owner or the owner of which is unknown, or, unless otherwise provided by law, the ownership of which has been renounced by the owner.
According to the provisions of paragraphs 2.8 - 2.14 of Procedure No. 622, after receiving an application for the identification of an immovable property that has signs of being ownerless, the Department, no later than five working days, places on the official website of the administration on the Internet information and telecommunications network an announcement about the identification of an immovable property in the territory of the Donetsk urban district that has signs of being ownerless, and about the need for a person (persons) who considers themselves to be its owner. the owner (owners), or having (having) the right (rights) to it, contact the administration with documents excluding the possibility of considering the specified real estate object ownerless.
If applications are not received from the above persons, the immovable thing, at the request of the Office, on behalf of the administration, is subject to registration as ownerless in the territorial body of the federal executive body that carries out state cadastral registration, state registration of rights, and maintenance of the Unified State Register of Real Estate.
Within thirty calendar days from the date of publication of the announcement, the Department organizes a commission inspection of an item that has signs of being ownerless (with an on-site visit), with the involvement of representatives of the structural divisions of the administration (depending on the functional purpose of the object), administrations of the districts of the city of Donetsk (depending on the territorial location of the object) to draw up an inspection report of the ownerless real estate in the form in accordance with Appendix 1 to this Procedure.
The act indicates the location, condition and technical characteristics of the object, as well as other information. Photographs of the property may be attached to the property inspection report.
To conduct an inspection of ownerless immovable property, a commission for the inspection of ownerless immovable property (hereinafter referred to as the Commission) is created.
The composition of the Commission and the procedure for its work are approved by order of the head of the administration.
The commission, within ten working days after inspecting the ownerless immovable property, draws up an inspection report for the ownerless immovable property in the form in accordance with Appendix 1 to this Procedure.
After receiving an application for the identification of an ownerless immovable property, in order to obtain information about the identified ownerless immovable property, the Office, within thirty calendar days, sends requests: to the territorial body of the federal executive body carrying out state cadastral registration, state registration of rights, maintaining the Unified State Register of Real Estate, to provide information about such a thing contained in the Unified State Register of Real Estate, or available to such a body; to the territorial body of the federal executive body carrying out state registration of legal entities about the availability in the Unified State Register of Legal Entities of information about a legal entity in the manner established by the legislation on state registration of legal entities, as well as regarding information about payment for land at the established address, information about the availability of information about registered lease (sublease) agreements for real estate; to the territorial body of the federal executive body that carries out the functions of managing federal property, and to the executive body of the Donetsk People's Republic, which ensures accounting of state property of the Donetsk People's Republic, about the presence or absence of ownerless real estate in the registers of federal property (information about filing a declaration on an object of property subject to registration in the register of federal property or a proposal to classify the object as state property of the Donetsk People's Republic or municipal property, or on the transfer to state ownership of the Donetsk People's Republic or municipal property), property owned by the Donetsk People's Republic; to state and municipal enterprises that provide services to legal entities and individuals in the field of housing and communal services, on the fulfillment of the burden of maintaining ownerless real estate (the requested information is provided to the Administration within ten days).
After thirty calendar days from the date of publication of the announcement, in the event of the absence of the person (persons) who consider themselves the owner (owners) of the immovable thing, and after receiving from the authorities specified in paragraph 2.11 of this Procedure, answers indicating the absence of the owner of the immovable thing that has signs of being ownerless, the Commission draws up a report on the impossibility of identifying the owner of the immovable thing that has signs of being ownerless. form in accordance with Appendix 2 to this Procedure.
If the real estate does not have signs of ownerlessness, the Department sends a notification to the person who identified the object.
Based on the results of the responses received from the authorities specified in paragraph 2.11 of this Procedure, and confirming that the object has signs of ownerless property, the Department prepares a draft order of the head of the administration on filing an application with the authorized territorial body of the federal executive body that carries out state cadastral registration, state registration of rights, maintaining the Unified State Register of Real Estate for registering real estate as ownerless, and submits an application on behalf of the administration on registration of real estate as ownerless no later than ten days from the date of receipt of responses to requests.
By virtue of clause 2.15 of Procedure No. 622, an application for registration of an ownerless immovable property, the ownership of which the owner (owners) - an individual has refused (has refused), is submitted to the authorized territorial body of the federal executive body that carries out state cadastral registration, state registration of rights, maintenance of the Unified State Register of Real Estate no later than ten days from the date of receipt of the corresponding application, on the basis of an order from the head of the administration.
The provisions of paragraphs 2.17 – 2.19 of Procedure No. 622 provide that the Office, within ten working days from the date of registration of a real estate object as an ownerless immovable thing, sends a notification to the State Property Fund of the Donetsk People's Republic about the registration of the corresponding ownerless immovable thing.
