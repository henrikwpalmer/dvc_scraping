INSTALLED:
City administration Mariupol DPR applied to the court to recognize the right of municipal ownership of an ownerless immovable property. In support of the stated requirements, she indicated that as a result of the work carried out in accordance with the procedure established by law to identify ownerless property, an object of real estate was identified that had signs of ownerlessness. She indicated that the specified object was included in the appropriate register of ownerless real estate and it was registered accordingly as ownerless property. She indicated that the three-month period for the above-mentioned object to be registered as ownerless has now expired, and therefore there are grounds provided for by law for its conversion into the ownership of the municipality. Asks the court to recognize as ownerless an immovable property - an apartment located at the address:
<address>

Mariupol DPR did not appear, was duly notified of the day, time and place of its holding (including in accordance with the rules of Part 2.1 of Article 113 of the Code of Civil Procedure of the Russian Federation), submitted an application to consider the case in her absence. The representative of the interested party, the Rosreestr Office for the DPR, did not appear at the court hearing, was duly notified of the day, time and place of its holding, and submitted an application to consider the case in his absence.
Taking into account the fact that the parties were properly notified in the case and the lack of information about the valid reasons for their failure to appear, the court considers it possible to consider the case on the merits in the absence of the parties.

Currently, the city administration Mariupol DPR asks the court to recognize the right of municipal ownership of the above-mentioned ownerless real estate property and the court finds these claims valid on the following grounds:
According to Part 2 of Art. 290 of the Civil Procedure Code of the Russian Federation, an application for recognition of ownership of an ownerless immovable property is submitted to the court at its location by the body authorized to manage municipal property or property owned by the federal city of Moscow, St. Petersburg or Sevastopol.
According to Article 4 of the Federal Constitutional Law of the Russian Federation dated October 4, 2022 No. 5-FKZ “On the admission of the Donetsk People's Republic into the Russian Federation and the formation within the Russian Federation of a new subject - the Donetsk People's Republic” (hereinafter referred to as Law No. 5-FKZ), legislative and other regulatory legal acts of the Russian Federation are valid on the territory of the Donetsk People's Republic from the date of admission of the Donetsk People's Republic to the Russian Federation of the People's Republic and the formation of a new subject within the Russian Federation, unless otherwise provided by this Federal Constitutional Law. Regulatory legal acts of the Donetsk People's Republic are valid on the territory of the Donetsk People's Republic until the end of the transition period or until the adoption of the corresponding regulatory legal act of the Russian Federation and (or) the regulatory legal act of the Donetsk People's Republic. According to Part 7 of Article 12 of Law No. 5-FKZ, management of property located on the territory of the Donetsk People's Republic, which has signs of ownerlessness and is not classified as federal property, the property of a constituent entity of the Russian Federation or municipal property, is carried out by the authorities of the Donetsk People's Republic in the manner established by the Government of the Russian Federation, unless otherwise provided by this Federal Constitutional Law; the procedure for recognizing such property as ownerless, established by the regulatory legal acts of the Donetsk People's Republic, is applied until January 1, 2028, in agreement with the federal executive body exercising the functions of managing state property. Decree of the Acting Head of the Donetsk People's Republic dated 05/10/2023 No. 140 (hereinafter referred to as Decree No. 140 dated 05/10/2023) established the specifics of acquiring state and municipal property rights to ownerless real estate on the territory of the Donetsk People's Republic. According to clause 1.1 of Decree No. 140 dated 10.05.2023, the local government body of a municipal formation in the Donetsk People's Republic, authorized to manage municipal property, has the right to apply to the court with a demand for recognition of the right of municipal ownership of ownerless buildings, structures (except for linear objects), premises, objects of unfinished construction that do not have an owner or the owner of which is unknown, or the ownership of which the owner has refused, located on the territory of such a municipal entity, after three months from the date of their registration as ownerless real estate by the body carrying out state registration of rights to real estate. By Decree of the Acting Head of the Donetsk People's Republic dated 02.27.2023 No. 64 “On some issues related to the management of property that has signs of ownerlessness, located on the territory of the Donetsk People's Republic, and the recognition of such property as ownerless” (hereinafter referred to as Decree dated 02.27.2023 No. 64), the Government of the Donetsk People's Republic is vested with the authority to establish (adoption) of the procedure for recognizing as ownerless property that has signs of ownerlessness and is not classified as federal property, the property of a constituent entity of the Russian Federation or municipal property located on the territory of the Donetsk People's Republic, with the exception of real estate objects in the form of residential premises, residential buildings, and outbuildings.
