The Khartsyzsk Interdistrict Court received an application from the Administration of the Khartsyzsk Urban District of the Donetsk People's Republic, the interested party - the Department of the Federal Service for State Registration of Cadastre and Cartography for the Donetsk People's Republic, to recognize the right of municipal ownership of an ownerless real estate property.

from the applicant’s representative of the Administration of the Khartsyzsk urban district of the Donetsk People’s Republic –
Full name2
an application was received to refuse the application and terminate the proceedings.
The applicant asks the court to accept the refusal of the application and to terminate the proceedings.
According to Art. 39 of the Code of Civil Procedure of the Russian Federation, the applicant has the right to refuse the application.
The court has the right to accept the plaintiff’s refusal of the stated demands, if this does not contradict the law and does not violate the rights and legitimate interests of other persons. According to Art. 220 of the Code of Civil Procedure of the Russian Federation, the proceedings are terminated if the plaintiff renounces the claim and the refusal is accepted by the court.
Since the refusal of the application does not contradict the law and does not violate the rights and legitimate interests of other persons, the court considers it possible to accept the refusal of the application by the Administration of the Khartsyzsk Urban District of the Donetsk People's Republic.
Consequences of termination of proceedings under Art. 221 of the Code of Civil Procedure of the Russian Federation, explained and understandable to the applicant.
Based on the above, guided by Art. 220 Code of Civil Procedure of the Russian Federation, court -

A private complaint against the ruling is filed with the Supreme Court of the Donetsk People's Republic through the Khartsyzsk Interdistrict Court of the Donetsk People's Republic within fifteen days from the date of the ruling by the court of first instance.
