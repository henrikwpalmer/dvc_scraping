According to Art. 39 of the Code of Civil Procedure of the Russian Federation, the applicant has the right to refuse the application.
The court has the right to accept the applicant’s refusal of the stated requirements, if this does not contradict the law and does not violate the rights and legitimate interests of other persons. According to Art. 220 of the Code of Civil Procedure of the Russian Federation, the proceedings are terminated if the plaintiff renounces the claim and the refusal is accepted by the court.
Since the refusal of the application does not contradict the law and does not violate the rights and legitimate interests of other persons, the court considers it possible to accept the refusal of the application by the Administration of the Ilovaisk City District of the Donetsk People's Republic.
