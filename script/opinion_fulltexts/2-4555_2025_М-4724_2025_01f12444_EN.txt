In accordance with Art. 225 of the Civil Code of the Russian Federation, ownerless is a thing that does not have an owner or the owner of which is unknown or, unless otherwise provided by law, the owner of which has renounced the right of ownership.
If this is not excluded by the rules of this Code on the acquisition of ownership of things that the owner has abandoned (Article 226), on finds (Articles 227 and 228), on stray animals (Articles 230 and 231) and treasure (Article 233), the right of ownership of ownerless movable things can be acquired by virtue of acquisitive prescription.
Ownerless real estate is accepted for registration by the body that carries out state registration of rights to real estate, upon application from the local government body in whose territory they are located.
By virtue of the provisions of Art. 236 of the Civil Code of the Russian Federation, a citizen or legal entity may renounce the right of ownership of property belonging to him by declaring this or taking other actions that definitely indicate his removal from ownership, use and disposal of property without the intention of retaining any rights to this property.
From the content of these norms it follows that the current legislation provides for the possibility of terminating the right of ownership of a particular property by the owner performing actions indicating his renunciation of his property right.
At the same time, actions indicating the owner’s renunciation of the right of ownership may include, among other things, the removal of the owner from the possession and use of property belonging to him, and failure to take measures to maintain this property.
In accordance with Part 1 of Art. 4 of the Federal Constitutional Law of October 4, 2022 No. 5-FKZ “On the admission of the Donetsk People's Republic into the Russian Federation and the formation of a new subject within the Russian Federation - the Donetsk People's Republic” (hereinafter referred to as Law No. 5-FKZ) legislative and other regulatory legal acts of the Russian Federation are valid on the territory of the Donetsk People's Republic from the date of admission to the Russian Federation of the Donetsk People's Republic and the formation of of the Russian Federation as a new subject, unless otherwise provided by this Federal Constitutional Law.
According to Part 2 of Art. 4 of Law No. 5-FKZ, the regulatory legal acts of the Donetsk People's Republic are valid on the territory of the Donetsk People's Republic until the end of the transition period or until the adoption of the relevant regulatory legal act of the Russian Federation and (or) the regulatory legal act of the Donetsk People's Republic.
At the same time, Art. 21 of Law No. 5-FKZ establishes the specifics of regulating relations in the sphere of real estate turnover on the territory of the Donetsk People's Republic.
In accordance with clause 2, part 1, art. 21 of Law No. 5-FKZ, until January 01, 2028, on the territory of the Donetsk People's Republic, regulatory legal acts of the Donetsk People's Republic may establish features of the regulation of property relations, including the circulation of land, in agreement with the federal executive body performing the functions of managing state property.
By virtue of Part 7 of Art. 12 of Law No. 5-FKZ, management of property located on the territory of the Donetsk People's Republic, which has signs of ownerlessness and is not classified as federal property, the property of a constituent entity of the Russian Federation or municipal property, is carried out by the authorities of the Donetsk People's Republic in the manner established by the Government of the Russian Federation, unless otherwise provided by this Federal Constitutional Law. The procedure for recognizing such property as ownerless, established by the regulatory legal acts of the Donetsk People's Republic, is applied until January 1, 2028, in agreement with the federal executive body exercising the functions of managing state property.
In accordance with clause 1.1 of the Decree of the Head of the Donetsk People's Republic dated May 10, 2023 No. 140 “On the specifics of acquiring the right of state and municipal property to ownerless real estate on the territory of the Donetsk People’s Republic,” the body and officials of the Donetsk People’s Republic exercising the powers classified by the legislation of the Russian Federation as the powers of local self-government bodies, until the completion of the formation of local authorities self-government, a local government body of a municipal formation in the Donetsk People's Republic, authorized to manage municipal property, has the right to go to court with a demand for recognition of the right of municipal ownership of ownerless buildings, structures (except for linear objects), premises, objects of unfinished construction that do not have an owner or the owner of which is unknown or the owner of which has refused ownership, located on the territory of such a municipal formation, after three months from the date their registration as ownerless real estate by the body carrying out state registration of rights to real estate, unless otherwise provided by this Decree.
Clause 1.2. Decree No. 140 of May 10, 2023 provides that the executive body of the Donetsk People's Republic authorized to manage state property has the right to apply to the court with a demand for recognition of the right of state ownership of the objects defined in subparagraph 1.1 of this paragraph (with the exception of residential premises, outbuildings intended to satisfy citizens' household and other needs), after three months from the date of their registration as ownerless immovable things by the body carrying out state registration of rights to real estate.
According to the Decree of the Head of the Donetsk People's Republic of February 27, 2023 No. 64 “On some issues related to the management of property that has signs of ownerlessness, located on the territory of the Donetsk People's Republic, and the recognition of such property as ownerless” (hereinafter referred to as Decree No. 64), the Government of the Donetsk People's Republic is vested with the powers to:
1.1. Management of property that has signs of ownerlessness and is not classified as federal property, property of the Donetsk People's Republic or municipal property located on the territory of the Donetsk People's Republic, in accordance with the Rules for the management of bodies of the Donetsk People's Republic, Lugansk People's Republic, Zaporozhye region and Kherson region that has signs of ownerlessness and is not classified as federal property, property of a constituent entity of the Russian Federation or municipal property of property located on territories of the specified constituent entities of the Russian Federation, approved by Decree of the Government of the Russian Federation of December 28, 2022 No. 2474 (hereinafter referred to as the Rules), with the exception of real estate in the form of residential premises, garden houses, outbuildings intended to satisfy citizens' household and other needs.
1.2. Establishment (adoption) of a procedure for recognizing ownerless property specified in subclause 1.1 of this clause, with the period for application of such a procedure until January 1, 2028.
In accordance with the Decree of the Government of the Donetsk People's Republic of June 22, 2023 No. 45-5 "On the recognition of real estate located on the territory of the Donetsk People's Republic as ownerless, with the exception of residential buildings, residential premises, garden houses, outbuildings intended to satisfy citizens' household and other needs" (together with the "Procedure for recognition of real estate located on the territory of the Donetsk People's Republic ownerless real estate, with the exception of residential buildings, residential premises, garden houses and outbuildings intended to satisfy citizens' household and other needs", "The procedure for including ownerless linear objects (including their integral technological parts) intended for electricity supply, heat supply, gas supply, water supply, sewerage, located on the territory of the Donetsk People's Republic, in the Unified Register of Property of State Property Objects of the Donetsk People's Republic and objects in in respect of which a temporary administration (operational management) has been introduced, as well as objects of other forms of ownership under state control"), it is determined that: the executive body of the Donetsk People's Republic authorized to manage state real estate is the Ministry of Property and Land Relations of the Donetsk People's Republic, the procedure for recognizing real estate located on the territory of the Donetsk People's Republic as ownerless has been approved, with the exception of residential buildings, residential premises, garden houses and outbuildings intended for satisfaction of citizens' household and other needs (Appendix 1).
