The court considers it possible to consider the case in the absence of the parties who failed to appear.
Chapter 33 of the Code of Civil Procedure of the Russian Federation regulates the procedure for considering cases of recognizing a movable thing as ownerless or recognizing ownership of an ownerless immovable thing.
According to clause 6, part 1, art. 262, part 2 art. 290 of the Code of Civil Procedure of the Russian Federation, an application for recognition of ownership of an ownerless immovable thing is considered by the court in a special proceeding.
Article 292 of the Code of Civil Procedure of the Russian Federation stipulates that the judge, when preparing a case for trial, finds out which persons (owners, actual owners and others) can provide information about the ownership of the property, and also requests the relevant organizations about the information available about it. An application to recognize a thing as ownerless or to recognize the right of ownership to an ownerless immovable thing is considered by the court with the participation of interested persons.
Part 3 of Art. 263 of the Code of Civil Procedure of the Russian Federation establishes that if, when filing an application or considering a case in a special proceeding, the existence of a dispute about the right is established, the court issues a ruling to leave the application without consideration, in which it explains to the applicant and other interested parties their right to resolve the dispute in the manner of adjudication proceedings.

The above circumstances confirm that in relation to the specified object of real estate there is a dispute about the right and it is subject to consideration in the procedure of claim proceedings, and not through consideration of the application in the procedure of special proceedings.
If there is a dispute about the right, resolving the issue within the framework of the procedure established by law for recognizing a thing as ownerless is unacceptable.
Based on the provisions of the above circumstances and the rules of law, the requirements specified by the applicant for recognition of the right of municipal ownership of an ownerless real estate property are not indisputable, they are disputed by the interested party - Movchan A.B. and are subject to proof, that is, aimed at resolving a civil dispute that cannot be considered according to the rules of Chapter 33 of the Code of Civil Procedure of the Russian Federation.
The requirement to establish the presence or absence of grounds for the emergence of a right can be stated only by filing a claim and must be considered in the procedure of claim proceedings.
Under these circumstances, the court comes to the conclusion that the stated demands of the Administration of the Mangush Municipal District of the Donetsk People's Republic for recognition of the right of municipal ownership of an ownerless piece of real estate are not subject to consideration in a special manner, and therefore, the application of the Administration of the Mangush Municipal District of the Donetsk People's Republic must be left without consideration.
The demands of the Administration of the Mangush Municipal District of the Donetsk People's Republic for recognition of the right of municipal ownership of an ownerless real estate property must be resolved according to the general rules of claim proceedings.
