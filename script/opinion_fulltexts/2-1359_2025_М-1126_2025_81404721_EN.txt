SOLUTION
In the name of the Russian Federation
city of Mariupol July 14, 2025
Ilyichevsky District Court of Mariupol, Donetsk People's Republic
consisting of:
presiding judge Logvinova O.V.,
with the secretary of the court session Kalinichenko D.S.,
examined in open court a civil case at the request of the administration of the city district of Mariupol, with the participation of an interested party of the Rosreestr Office for the Donetsk People's Republic, on the recognition of the right of municipal ownership of ownerless property,
installed:
The administration of the city district of Mariupol filed a statement with the court, from which it appears that during the inventory of real estate located on the territory
<address>
Donetsk People's Republic, at the address: Russian Federation, Donetsk People's Republic,
<address>
, an object (real estate) was identified that has signs of an ownerless real estate.
According to paragraph 1 of Article 225 of the Civil Code of the Russian Federation, an ownerless thing is a thing that does not have an owner or the owner of which is unknown or, unless otherwise provided by law, the right of ownership to which the owner has refused.
According to paragraph 1 of Article 235 of the Civil Code of the Russian Federation, the right of ownership terminates when the owner alienates his property to other persons, the owner renounces the right of ownership, the destruction or destruction of property and when the right of ownership to property is lost in other cases provided for by law.
Part 7 of Article 12 of the Federal Constitutional Law of October 4, 2022 No. 5-FKZ “On the admission of the Donetsk People's Republic into the Russian Federation and the formation of a new subject within the Russian Federation - the Donetsk People's Republic” provides that the management of property located on the territory of the Donetsk People's Republic, which has signs of ownerlessness and is not classified as federal property, the property of a subject of the Russian Federation or municipal property, is carried out bodies of the Donetsk People's Republic in the manner established by the Government of the Russian Federation, unless otherwise provided by this Federal Constitutional Law. The procedure for recognizing such property as ownerless, established by the regulatory legal acts of the Donetsk People's Republic, is applied until January 1, 2028, in agreement with the federal executive body exercising the functions of managing state property.
In pursuance of the above norm, Decree of the Government of the Russian Federation dated December 28, 2022 No. 2474 “On approval of the rules for managing bodies of the Donetsk People's Republic, Lugansk People's Republic, Zaporozhye region and Kherson region having signs of ownerlessness was adopted. not classified as federal property, property of a constituent entity of the Russian Federation or municipal property, property located on the territory of these constituent entities of the Russian Federation", Law of the Donetsk People's Republic of March 21, 2024 No. 66-RZ "On the specifics of identifying, using and recognizing the right of municipal property of municipalities of the Donetsk People's Republic to residential premises with signs of an ownerless creature located on the territory of the Donetsk People's Republic" hereinafter referred to as Law No. 66-RZ), amendments were made to the Decree of the Head of the Donetsk People's Republic of April 28, 2021 No. 116 “On the identification, accounting and acceptance of municipal property of ownerless real estate escheatable property” (hereinafter referred to as Decree No. 116).
In order to accept, record and register ownerless immovable property of escheat, and increase the efficiency of its use, the administration of the city of Mariupol adopted Order No. 766 dated December 05, 2023 “On approval of the Procedure for identifying, recording and accepting into municipal ownership ownerless immovable property and escheat property of the administrative territory of the municipality of the city of Mariupol” (as amended) (hereinafter referred to as the Procedure).
In accordance with paragraph 2.12 of Decree No. 116, in order to register a movable thing as ownerless, the administration issues a corresponding order and applies to the territorial body of the Federal executive body that carries out state cadastral registration, state registration of rights, maintaining the Unified State Register of Real Estate, with an application to register the immovable thing as ownerless and other documents provided for by current legislation.
A similar norm is enshrined in paragraph 2 of part 1 of Article 7 of the Law of the Donetsk People’s Republic of March 21, 2024 No. 66-RZ “On the specifics of identifying, using and recognizing the right of municipal property of municipalities of the Donetsk People’s Republic to residential premises with signs of ownerless property located on the territory of the Donetsk People’s Republic.”
Guided by clause 2.9 of the Procedure, Article 5 of Law No. 66-RZ, clause 2.9.1 of Decree No. 116, on April 18, 2024, on the official website of the municipality of the city district of Mariupol of the Donetsk People's Republic, an announcement was posted on the Internet about the identification of the Donetsk People's Republic in the city of Mariupol at the address: Karpinsky Street, 37A/18A, square 7 of an immovable property that has signs of being ownerless, and the need for the person(s) who consider themselves to be its owner or have rights to it to appear and contact the administration with documents excluding the possibility of considering the specified real estate item ownerless.
According to clause 2.9.2. Decree No. 116 On August 19, 2024, a commission inspection was organized with an on-site visit to a real estate property located at the address: Russian Federation, Donetsk People's Republic, Mariupol urban district, Mariupol city, Karpinsky Street, building 37A/18A, apartment 7, and having signs of ownerlessness, based on the results of which an inspection report was drawn up for the property. As a result of the inspection of the real estate property, it was established that the apartment is closed, there is no free access to the residential premises, and the location of the keys is unknown. It is not possible to establish the existence of an owner based on the results of the commission survey.
The applicant asked to recognize the right of municipal ownership of the administration of the city district of Mariupol to an ownerless immovable property intended for residential premises with an area of 43.3 sq.m., located at the address: Russian Federation, Donetsk People's Republic, city district of Mariupol,
<address>
The representative of the Mariupol city district did not appear at the court hearing; they were duly notified of the time and place of the consideration of the civil case.
The representative of the interested party - the Office of the Federal Service for State Registration, Cadastre and Cartography for the Donetsk People's Republic did not appear at the court hearing; according to the petition, he asked to consider the case in his absence.
The case was considered in the absence of the parties in accordance with Art. 167 Code of Civil Procedure of the Russian Federation.
Having examined the case materials and the evidence provided in accordance with the requirements of Art. 67 of the Code of Civil Procedure of the Russian Federation, the court believes that the application of the administration of the city of Mariupol must be satisfied.
According to paragraph 1 of Article 225 of the Civil Code of the Russian Federation, an ownerless thing is a thing that does not have an owner or the owner of which is unknown or, unless otherwise provided by law, the right of ownership to which the owner has refused.
According to paragraph 1 of Article 235 of the Civil Code of the Russian Federation, the right of ownership terminates when the owner alienates his property to other persons, the owner renounces the right of ownership, the destruction or destruction of property and when the right of ownership to property is lost in other cases provided for by law.
According to Art. 56 of the Code of Civil Procedure of the Russian Federation, each party must prove the circumstances to which it refers as the basis for its claims and objections.
By virtue of Art. 67 of the Code of Civil Procedure of the Russian Federation, the court evaluates evidence according to its internal conviction, based on a comprehensive, complete, objective and direct examination of the evidence available in the case.
The court found that the commission of the administration of the city of Mariupol of the Donetsk People's Republic to conduct an inventory of ownerless, destroyed and abandoned property located on the territory of the municipal formation of the city of Mariupol carried out an inspection of the Property, based on the results of which a corresponding act was drawn up.
Based on the results of the inspection, an authorized employee of the administration of the Ilyichevsky district of the city of Mariupol drew up an act dated August 19, 2024 on the impossibility of identifying the owner (copyright holder) of a real estate property that had signs of ownerlessness or escheat on the administrative territory of the municipality of the city of Mariupol. To confirm information about the identified immovable property, which has signs of being ownerless, the administration of the city of Mariupol sent the following requests:
to the territorial department of the federal agency for state property management in the Donetsk People's Republic about the presence or absence of information about an immovable property that has signs of being ownerless in the registers of federal property, state property of the Donetsk People's Republic (information about filing a declaration of property subject to registration in the register of federal property or proposals to classify the object as state property of the Donetsk People's Republic or municipal property or to transfer such an object to state ownership Donetsk People's Republic or municipal property);
to the liquidation commission of the State Property Fund of the Donetsk People's Republic about the presence or absence of information about an immovable thing that has signs of being ownerless in the registers of federal property, state property of the Donetsk People's Republic (information about filing a declaration of property subject to registration in the register of federal property or a proposal to classify the object as state property of the Donetsk People's Republic or municipal property or about transferring such an object to the state property of the Donetsk People's Republic People's Republic or municipal property);
The administration of the city district of Mariupol of the Donetsk People's Republic adopted a resolution dated December 11, 2024 No. 772 “On registering real estate as ownerless.”
Thus, by registering an ownerless real estate property, the Federal Service for State Registration, Cadastre and Cartography of the Russian Federation confirms the fact that there is no state registration of ownership and derivative real rights to the specified real estate in the State Register of Rights.
According to the Extract from the Unified State Register of Real Estate on the main characteristics and registered rights to the property, it was registered as an ownerless property. The above extract confirms the fact that there is no information about the registered ownership of residential premises in the Unified State Register of Real Estate and the technical characteristics of the property that has signs of being ownerless, namely the area of ​​the property.
By the decree of the administration of the city district of Mariupol of the Donetsk People's Republic "On recognizing real estate objects as ownerless and including them in the register of ownerless real estate objects" the object was included in the Register of ownerless real estate objects (Appendix - Extract from the Register of ownerless real estate objects on the territory of the administration of the city district of Mariupol of the Donetsk People's Republic).
Based on Article 10 of the Law of the Donetsk People's Republic dated March 21, 2024 No. 66-RZ “On the specifics of identifying, using and recognizing the right of municipal property of municipalities of the Donetsk People's Republic to residential premises with signs of ownerless property located on the territory of the Donetsk People's Republic”, paragraph 3.1 of the Standard procedure for identifying, recording and accepting ownerless real estate and escheat into municipal ownership property approved by Decree of the Head of the Donetsk People's Republic No. 116
dated 04/28/2021 “On the identification, registration and acceptance into municipal ownership of ownerless real estate and escheat property”, clause 3.1 of the Procedure for identifying, recording and acceptance into municipal ownership of ownerless real estate and escheat property in the administrative territory of the municipality of the city of Mariupol, after three months from the date of registration of a residential premises as an ownerless territorial body of the federal the executive body carrying out state cadastral registration, state registration of rights, maintaining the Unified State Register of Real Estate, the authorized body applies to the court with an application for recognition of the right of municipal property of a municipal entity to this residential premises in the manner prescribed by the legislation of the Russian Federation.
The court comes to the conclusion that the administration of the city district of Mariupol of the Donetsk People's Republic complied with the procedure provided for by the current regulatory legal acts, namely: publication of an object that has signs of ownerless property. sending requests to confirm information about an identified residential premises that has signs of being ownerless, drawing up an inspection report on an ownerless property, drawing up a report on the impossibility of identifying the owner of a residential premises that has signs of being ownerless, registering a real estate property as ownerless.
Within three months from the moment the object was registered as ownerless, no one claimed their rights to this object.
There is no dispute about the right.
Since from the day this object was registered as ownerless, within the period established by law, no one declared their rights to it, in order to claim it for registration in the manner established by the current legislation, the Administration of the Mariupol City District of the Donetsk People's Republic had the right to apply to the court for recognition of the right of municipal ownership to it. In this connection, the requirements of the application are subject to satisfaction.
Guided by Art. Art. 194-199 Code of Civil Procedure of the Russian Federation, court
decided:
the application of the administration of the urban district of Mariupol of the Donetsk People's Republic, with the participation of the interested party of the Rosreestr Office for the Donetsk People's Republic, on the recognition of the right of municipal ownership of ownerless property - to be satisfied.
Recognize for the Administration of the Mariupol City District of the Donetsk People's Republic the right of municipal ownership of ownerless property - an apartment located at the address: Russian Federation, Donetsk People's Republic, Mariupol City District,
<address>
, with a total area of 43.3 sq.m.
The decision can be appealed to the Supreme Court of the Donetsk People's Republic within a month from the date the decision was made in final form.
The decision in final form was made on July 16, 2025
Judge O.V.Logvinov
