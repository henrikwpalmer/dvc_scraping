DECISION
In the name of the Russian Federation
May 20, 2025 Amvrosievka
Amvrosievsky District Court of the Donetsk People's Republic composed of:
presiding judge Lityuk V.V.,
when keeping minutes by the secretary of the court session Belitskaya I.S.,
having considered in open court on the premises of the Amvrosiyevsky District Court a civil case on the application of the administration of the Amvrosiyevsky Municipal District of the Donetsk People's Republic for recognition of the right of municipal ownership of ownerless real estate,
INSTALLED:
The administration of the Amvrosievsky municipal district of the Donetsk People's Republic applied to the court for recognition of the right of municipal ownership of ownerless property (the interested party is the Office of the Federal Service for State Registration, Cadastre and Cartography for the Donetsk People's Republic), in which it asks the court to recognize the right of municipal ownership of the municipal formation of the Amvrosievsky municipal district of the Donetsk People's Republic for ownerless real estate - a structure, a suspended bridge over the Krynka river, located at the address: Russian Federation, Donetsk People's Republic, Amvrosievsky municipal district, Karpovo-Nadezhdinka village, Lenin street, no cadastral number. In support of the stated requirements, the applicant indicated that on December 19, 2024, the specified real estate was accepted by the Rosreestr Office for the Donetsk People's Republic for registration as ownerless. Other owners of this real estate are unknown. Thus, by registering an object as ownerless, the territorial body of the federal executive body, which carries out cadastral registration, state registration of rights, maintaining the Unified State Register of Real Estate, confirms the fact that the state registration of the object as ownerless was carried out lawfully. Within 3 months and after 3 months from the moment the object was registered as ownerless, no one declared their rights to this object, that is, there is no dispute about the right.
At the court hearing, the representative of the administration of the Amvrosievsky municipal district, Podlesnaya O.I., having been duly notified, asked to consider the case in her absence, insisted on meeting the stated demands, asked to satisfy them, the case was considered in her absence in accordance with Article 167 of the Code of Civil Procedure of the Russian Federation.
The interested party, the Office of the Federal Service for State Registration, Cadastre and Cartography for the Donetsk People's Republic, having been duly notified, did not send a representative to the court hearing; in their application they asked to consider the case without the participation of their representative, the case was considered in their absence in accordance with Article 167 of the Code of Civil Procedure of the Russian Federation.
Having studied the case materials, the court comes to the following conclusions.
By virtue of Article 125 of the Civil Code of the Russian Federation, on behalf of municipalities, by their actions, local government bodies can acquire and exercise rights and obligations within the framework of their competence, established by acts defining the status of these bodies.
According to Part 3 of Article 218 of the Civil Code of the Russian Federation, in cases and in the manner provided for by the Civil Code of the Russian Federation, a person can acquire ownership of property that does not have an owner, property whose owner is unknown, or property that the owner has abandoned or to which he has lost ownership on other grounds provided by law.
In accordance with Part 1 of Article 225 of the Civil Code of the Russian Federation, an ownerless thing is one that does not have an owner or the owner of which is unknown or, unless otherwise provided by law, the owner of which has renounced the right of ownership.
According to Part 3 of Article 225 of the Civil Code of the Russian Federation, ownerless real estate is accepted for registration by the body that carries out state registration of rights to real estate, upon application from the local government body in whose territory they are located.
Ownerless real estate is accepted for registration by the body that carries out state registration of rights to real estate, upon application from the local government body in whose territory they are located. After a year has passed from the date of registration of an ownerless immovable property, the body authorized to manage municipal property may apply to the court with a request to recognize the right of municipal ownership of this thing.
An ownerless immovable property, not recognized by a court decision as having come into municipal ownership, may be again taken into possession, use and disposal by the owner who abandoned it, or acquired into ownership by virtue of acquisitive prescription.
Meanwhile, in accordance with Part 1 of Article 4 of the Federal Constitutional Law of October 4, 2022 No. 5-FKZ “On the admission of the Donetsk People’s Republic into the Russian Federation and the formation of a new subject within the Russian Federation - the Donetsk People’s Republic” (hereinafter referred to as Law No. 5-FKZ), legislative and other regulatory legal acts of the Russian Federation are valid on the territory of the Donetsk People’s Republic from the date of admission to the Russian Federation Donetsk People's Republic and the formation of a new entity within the Russian Federation, unless otherwise provided by Law No. 5-FKZ.
According to Part 2 of Article 4 of Law No. 5-FKZ, the regulatory legal acts of the Donetsk People's Republic are valid on the territory of the Donetsk People's Republic until the end of the transition period or until the adoption of the relevant regulatory legal act of the Russian Federation and (or) the regulatory legal act of the Donetsk People's Republic.
Article 21 of Law No. 5-FKZ establishes the specifics of regulating relations in the sphere of real estate turnover on the territory of the Donetsk People's Republic.
According to clause 2, part 1, article 21 of Law No. 5-FKZ, until 01.01.2028, on the territory of the Donetsk People's Republic, regulatory legal acts of the Donetsk People's Republic may establish features of regulation of property relations, including land turnover, in agreement with the federal executive body performing the functions of managing state property.
By virtue of Part 7 of Article 12 of Law No. 5-FKZ, the procedure for recognizing property that is not classified as federal property, the property of a constituent entity of the Russian Federation or municipal property as ownerless, established by the regulatory legal acts of the Donetsk People's Republic, applies until 01/01/2028 in agreement with the federal executive body performing the functions of managing state property.
In pursuance of the above federal constitutional law, on August 18, 2023, the Acting Head of the Donetsk People's Republic adopted Decree No. 295 “On amendments to the Decree of the Head of the Donetsk People's Republic dated April 28, 2021 No. 116 “On the identification, accounting and acceptance into municipal ownership of ownerless real estate and escheat property,” which approved the Standard Procedure, in accordance with clause 3.1 of which, after three months from the date of registration of an ownerless immovable property, the administration is obliged to apply to the court with a claim for recognition of the right of municipal ownership of this thing.
In accordance with Law No. 5-FKZ, the Resolution of the People's Council dated March 21, 2024, adopted the Law of the Donetsk People's Republic No. 66-RZ "On the specifics of identifying, using and recognizing the right of municipal property of municipalities of the Donetsk People's Republic to residential premises with signs of ownerless property located on the territory of the Donetsk People's Republic", according to Article 10 of which, after three months from the date of installation of the residential premises as ownerless by the territorial body of the federal executive body carrying out state cadastral registration, state registration of rights, maintaining the Unified State Register of Real Estate, the authorized body applies to the court with an application for recognition of the right of municipal ownership of the municipality to this residential premises in the manner prescribed by the legislation of the Russian Federation.
According to information certificate No. 7 dated November 18, 2024, the suspension bridge over the Krynka river, located at the address: Donetsk People's Republic, Amvrosievsky municipal district, Karpovo-Nadezhdinka village, Lenin street (45 sq.m), identified within the boundaries of the Amvrosievsky municipal district as ownerless, is not listed in the municipal property register.
According to the resolution of the Head of the Administration of the Amvrosiyevsky District of the Donetsk People's Republic No. 1513 dated December 10, 2024, it was decided to register real estate in the form of a structure (suspension bridge over the Krynka River), located at the address: Donetsk People's Republic, Amvrosiyevsky Municipal District, Karpovo-Nadezhdinka village, Lenin St. (45.0 sq.m.) immovable property as ownerless identified within the boundaries of the administrative-territorial unit of the Amvrosievsky district, with a view to subsequent acceptance into municipal ownership.
The official website of the Donetsk People's Republic on the Internet information and telecommunications network contains information about a real estate property located at the address: Donetsk People's Republic, Amvrosievsky municipal district, Karpovo-Nadezhdinka village, Lenin street, in order to identify and inform the owner of the property.
According to the notification of the Unified State Register of Real Estate on the main characteristics and registered rights to the property - a suspension bridge across the Krynka River, issued by the Department of State Registration, Cadastre and Cartography for the Donetsk People's Republic, from which it is clear that there is no information about the registration of rights to this object.
According to the application, the administration of the Amvrosievsky municipal district of the Donetsk People's Republic applied on December 17, 2024 to the Department of State Registration, Cadastre and Cartography Service for the Donetsk People's Republic to register ownerless real estate - a structure (suspension bridge over the Krynka River), located at the address: Donetsk People's Republic, Amvrosievsky municipal district, Karpovo-Nadezhdinka village, Lenin street.
According to information from the acting Deputy Chairman of the State Property Fund of the Donetsk People's Republic dated 10.23.2024, as of 10.23.2024 information about the real estate property located at the address: Amvrosievsky district, from the village of Karpovo-Nadezhdinka, Lenin St., in the Unified Register of State Property Objects, and objects in respect of which temporary administration (operational management) has been introduced, as well as objects of other forms There are no properties under management.
According to the act of the commission for conducting an inventory of ownerless real estate objects identified on the territory of the Amvrosievsky district dated December 06, 2024, as a result of a study of the structure (suspension bridge over the Krynka River), located at the address: Donetsk People's Republic, Amvrosievsky municipal district, Karpovo-Nadezhdinka village, Lenin street, it was established that, based on the collected documents, the owner of this structure could be identified and its location was not possible, the specified structure has signs of being ownerless, and the maintenance of this immovable object is not ensured.
According to the inspection report of ownerless real estate of the Amvrosievsky district administration commission No. 11/4 dated December 6, 2024, the structure (suspension bridge over the Krynka river), located at the address: Donetsk People's Republic, Amvrosievsky municipal district, Karpovo-Nadezhdinka village, Lenin street, the owner of the structure is absent, the location is unknown, the condition of the structure is unsatisfactory (the unsatisfactory condition of the pedestrian deck, the suspension cables have a large deflection and pose a risk of collapse, there is no bridge fencing, there is no information about the former owner, the property has been ownerless since 2000, it has signs of ownerless property.
According to the notice from the Unified State Register of Real Estate about the property dated 04/11/2025, the registered ownership of the property Donetsk People's Republic, Amvrosievsky municipal district, Karpovo-Nadezhdinka village, Lenin St., cadastral number is missing, information is missing.
Thus, the court found that the above-mentioned real estate property does not have an owner; more than three months have passed since it was registered as an ownerless real estate property (since December 19, 2024), while the object is located on the territory of the Amvrosievsky municipal formation of the Donetsk People's Republic.
In accordance with Part 2 of Article 293 of the Code of Civil Procedure of the Russian Federation, the court, having recognized that the immovable thing does not have an owner or the owner of the immovable thing is unknown and it has been registered in the prescribed manner, makes a decision to recognize the right of municipal ownership of this thing.
By virtue of Article 12 of the Civil Code of the Russian Federation, the protection of civil rights can be carried out through the recognition of rights.
Taking into account the circumstances established by the court, the position of the interested party, the absence of violations when registering the above-mentioned property as ownerless, the court comes to the conclusion that the stated requirements of the administration of the Amvrosievsky municipal formation of the Donetsk People's Republic have been satisfied in full.
Guided by Article 194-199, Article 293 of the Code of Civil Procedure of the Russian Federation, the court
DECISION:
the stated demands of the administration of the Amvrosievsky municipal district of the Donetsk People's Republic for recognition of the right of municipal ownership of ownerless real estate should be satisfied.
Recognize the right of municipal ownership of the municipal formation Amvrosiyevsky municipal district of the Donetsk People's Republic to ownerless real estate - a structure, a suspension bridge across the Krynka river, with an area of 45.0 sq.m, located at the address: Russian Federation, Donetsk People's Republic, Amvrosiyevsky municipal district, village of Karpovo-Nadezhdinka, Lenin street.
The decision can be appealed to the Supreme Court of the Donetsk People's Republic through the Amvrosievsky District Court of the Donetsk People's Republic within a month from the date the decision was made in final form.
Judge V.V. Lityuk
The final decision was made on May 27, 2025.
