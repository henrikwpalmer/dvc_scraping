By a court ruling dated December 5, 2024, Rusinko Pavel Gaborovich, Egorova Maria Yuryevna, Egorova Veronika Pavlovna, Egorov Nikita Pavlovich, Egorov Danil Pavlovich were brought to participate in the case as interested parties.
At the court hearing, interested parties asked the court to refuse to satisfy the stated demands of the Administration of the Telmanovsky Municipal District of the Donetsk People's Republic to recognize the right of municipal ownership of ownerless real estate - an apartment belonging to them; they indicated that they do not live in this apartment due to the unsuitability of this housing for living.
In accordance with Part 3 of Art. 263 of the Code of Civil Procedure of the Russian Federation, if, when filing an application or considering a case in a special proceeding, it is established that there is a dispute about the law, the court issues a ruling to leave the application without consideration.
Based on the above, the application of the Administration of the Telmanovsky Municipal District of the Donetsk People's Republic, interested parties: Office of the Federal Service for State Registration, Cadastre and Cartography for the Donetsk People's Republic, Rusinko P.G., Egorova M.Yu., Egorova V.P., Egorov N.P., Egorov D.P., on recognition of the right of municipal ownership of ownerless real estate, is subject to leaving without consideration, due to the existence of a dispute about law.
