The court, having examined the case materials and assessed the totality of the evidence presented, considers the stated demands to be justified and subject to satisfaction, for the following reasons.
According to Part 2 of Article 290 of the Civil Procedure Code of the Russian Federation, an application for recognition of ownership of an ownerless immovable property is submitted to the court at its location by the body authorized to manage municipal property or property owned by the federal city of Moscow, St. Petersburg or Sevastopol.
In accordance with Article 4 of the Federal Constitutional Law of October 4, 2022 No. 5-FKZ “On the admission of the Donetsk People's Republic to the Russian Federation and the formation of a new entity within the Russian Federation - the Donetsk People's Republic” (hereinafter referred to as Law No. 5-FKZ), legislative and other regulatory legal acts of the Russian Federation are valid on the territory of the Donetsk People's Republic from the date of admission to the Russian Federation Donetsk People's Republic and the formation of a new subject within the Russian Federation, unless otherwise provided by this Federal Constitutional Law.
Regulatory legal acts of the Donetsk People's Republic are valid on the territory of the Donetsk People's Republic until the end of the transition period or until the adoption of the relevant regulatory legal act of the Russian Federation and (or) the regulatory legal act of the Donetsk People's Republic.
According to Part 7 of Article 12 of Law No. 5-FKZ, management of property located on the territory of the Donetsk People's Republic, which has signs of ownerlessness and is not classified as federal property, the property of a constituent entity of the Russian Federation or municipal property, is carried out by the authorities of the Donetsk People's Republic in the manner established by the Government of the Russian Federation, unless otherwise provided by this Federal Constitutional Law. The procedure for recognizing such property as ownerless, established by the regulatory legal acts of the Donetsk People's Republic, is applied until January 1, 2028, in agreement with the federal executive body exercising the functions of managing state property.
In accordance with paragraph 1.2 of Decree of the Acting Head of the Donetsk People's Republic No. 140 dated May 10, 2023 (hereinafter referred to as Decree No. 140), the executive body of the Donetsk People's Republic authorized to manage state property has the right to apply to the court with a demand for recognition of state ownership of the objects defined in subparagraph 1.1 of this paragraph (with the exception of residential premises, outbuildings intended to satisfy citizens' household and other needs), after three months from the date of their registration as ownerless real estate by the body carrying out state registration of rights to real estate.
According to clause 1.2. Decree of the Acting Head of the Donetsk People's Republic of February 27, 2023 No. 64 “On some issues related to the management of property that has signs of ownerlessness, located on the territory of the Donetsk People’s Republic, and the recognition of such property as ownerless” The Government of the Donetsk People’s Republic has the authority to establish (adopt) the procedure for recognizing ownerless property specified in subclause 1.1 of this clause, with The period for application of this procedure is until January 1, 2028.
As part of the implementation of the above Decrees, Decree of the Government of the Donetsk People's Republic dated June 22, 2023 No. 45-5, the executive body of the Donetsk People's Republic authorized to manage the state property of the Donetsk People's Republic established the State Property Fund of the Donetsk People's Republic and approved the Procedure for recognizing real estate located on the territory of the Donetsk People's Republic as ownerless, with the exception of residential buildings, residential premises, gardens houses and outbuildings intended to satisfy citizens' household and other needs (hereinafter in the text of the decision - the Procedure), which regulates legal relations and establishes the procedure for recognizing property on the territory of the Donetsk People's Republic as ownerless, as well as recognizing the right of state ownership of the Donetsk People's Republic to an ownerless immovable property.
In addition, in pursuance of the above norm, Decree of the Government of the Russian Federation of December 28, 2022 No. 2474 was adopted “On approval of the Rules for the management of bodies of the Donetsk People's Republic, Lugansk People's Republic, Zaporozhye region and Kherson region that have signs of ownerlessness and are not classified as federal property, property of a constituent entity of the Russian Federation or municipal property located on the territory of these constituent entities of the Russian Federation.”
In accordance with paragraph 2.12 of the Decree of the Head of the Donetsk People's Republic of April 28, 2021 No. 116 “On the identification, registration and acceptance into municipal ownership of ownerless real estate and escheat property”, in order to register an immovable object as ownerless, the administration issues a corresponding order and applies to the territorial body of the federal executive body that carries out state cadastral registration, state registration of rights, maintenance of the Unified State Register of Real Estate, with an application for registration of an immovable property as ownerless and other documents provided for by current legislation.
According to paragraph 4 of the Procedure for registering ownerless real estate, established by the Order of the Federal Service for State Registration, Cadastre and Cartography dated
DD.MM.YYYY
