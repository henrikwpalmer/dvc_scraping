The applicant's representative did not appear at the court hearing and filed a motion to consider the civil case in his absence.

The court, having examined the case materials, as well as the application to waive the stated demands and terminate the proceedings in the case, finds the petition to terminate the proceedings in the case justified and subject to satisfaction on the following grounds.
According to Article 263 of the Civil Procedure Code of the Russian Federation, cases of special proceedings are considered and resolved by the court according to the general rules of claim proceedings with the features established by this chapter and Chapters 28 - 38 of this Code.
In accordance with Part 1 of Art. 39 of the Code of Civil Procedure of the Russian Federation, the plaintiff has the right to abandon the claim.
The grounds provided for in Part 2 of Art. 39 of the Code of Civil Procedure of the Russian Federation, there is no reason to reject the claim.
According to paragraph 3 of Art. 220 of the Code of Civil Procedure of the Russian Federation, the proceedings are terminated if the plaintiff abandons the claim and accepts it by the court.
Taking into account the above circumstances, since the refusal of the claim does not contradict the law and does not violate the rights and legitimate interests of other persons, the court considers it possible to grant the petition and accept the refusal of the claim and terminate the proceedings in this case.
In accordance with clause 22, part 2 of Art. 5 of the Law of the Donetsk People's Republic “On the Court Fee” No. 26-IHC dated April 14, 2015, state authorities and local government bodies, enterprises, institutions, organizations financed from the Republican and local budgets are exempt from paying the court fee, the court does not consider the procedure for returning or offsetting the state fee.
Based on the above and guided by Art. Art. 39, 173, 220, 224, 225 Code of Civil Procedure of the Russian Federation, court, -
