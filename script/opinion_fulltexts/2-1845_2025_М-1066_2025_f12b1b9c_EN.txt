Case No. 2-1845/2025
SOLUTION
In the name of the Russian Federation
Donetsk People's Republic
Mariupol April 10, 2025
Zhovtnevy District Court of Mariupol, Donetsk People's Republic, consisting of: presiding judge Leonov A.Yu., with the secretary of the court session Zhuravlev S.O.,
having considered in open court a civil case on the application of the Administration of the Mariupol Urban District for recognition of the right of municipal ownership of an ownerless immovable property, with the interested party, the department of the Federal Service for State Registration of Cadastre and Cartography in the Donetsk People's Republic
INSTALLED:
The administration of the city district of Mariupol appealed to the court with the above-mentioned statement, in which it indicated that during the inventory it was established that at the address:
<address>
, there is real estate that has signs of being ownerless.
Further, according to an extract from the Unified State Register of Real Estate dated November 14, 2024, the specified real estate was registered as an ownerless property; there is no information about registered rights. Indicate that the activities set out in paragraphs. 2.9.1, 2.9.2 and 2.9.3 of the standard procedure for identifying, recording and accepting into municipal ownership ownerless real estate and escheat property, approved by Decree of the Head of the DPR dated April 28, 2021 No. 116, were implemented by the administration.
Since the applicant complied with the procedure provided for by the current regulatory legal acts, namely: publication about the object, sending requests to confirm information about the identified premises that have signs of ownerless property, drawing up an inspection report of the ownerless property, drawing up an act on the impossibility of identifying the owner of the premises that has signs of ownerlessness, registering the real estate property as ownerless; and within three months from the date of registration of the object as ownerless, no one has declared their rights to this object, then they are asked to recognize for the Administration of the Mariupol Urban District the right of municipal ownership to ownerless property located at the address:
<address>
.
The applicant's representative did not appear at the court hearing; they were duly notified of the place and time of the consideration of the case; they submitted an application for consideration of the case in their absence, supporting their application.
The representative of the interested party - the department of the Federal Service for State Registration of Cadastre and Cartography for the Donetsk People's Republic did not appear, was duly notified, and no petitions to postpone the court hearing were submitted.
In accordance with the requirements of Art. 167 of the Code of Civil Procedure of the Russian Federation, the court considers it possible to consider the case in the absence of the applicant and the interested party.
Having examined the case materials, the court comes to the following conclusions.
According to Part 2 of Art. 290 of the Code of Civil Procedure of the Russian Federation, an application for recognition of ownership of an ownerless immovable thing is submitted to the court at its location by the body authorized to manage municipal property or property owned by the federal city of Moscow, St. Petersburg or Sevastopol.
In accordance with Art. 4 of the Federal Constitutional Law of 04.10.2022 N 5-FKZ “On the admission of the Donetsk People’s Republic to the Russian Federation and the formation of a new subject within the Russian Federation - the Donetsk People’s Republic” (hereinafter in the text of the decision - Law No. 5-FKZ) legislative and other regulatory legal acts of the Russian Federation are valid on the territory of the Donetsk People’s Republic from the date of admission of the Donetsk People’s Republic to the Russian Federation Republic and the formation of a new subject within the Russian Federation, unless otherwise provided by this Federal Constitutional Law.
Regulatory legal acts of the Donetsk People's Republic are valid on the territory of the Donetsk People's Republic until the end of the transition period or until the adoption of the relevant regulatory legal act of the Russian Federation and (or) the regulatory legal act of the Donetsk People's Republic.
According to Part 7 of Art. 12 of Law No. 5-FKZ, management of property located on the territory of the Donetsk People's Republic, which has signs of ownerlessness and is not classified as federal property, the property of a constituent entity of the Russian Federation or municipal property, is carried out by the authorities of the Donetsk People's Republic in the manner established by the Government of the Russian Federation, unless otherwise provided by this Federal Constitutional Law. The procedure for recognizing such property as ownerless, established by the regulatory legal acts of the Donetsk People's Republic, is applied until January 1, 2028, in agreement with the federal executive body exercising the functions of managing state property.
In accordance with clause 1.2 of Decree of the Acting Head of the Donetsk People's Republic No. 140 dated 05.10.2023 (hereinafter referred to as Decree No. 140), the executive body of the Donetsk People's Republic authorized to manage state property has the right to apply to the court with a demand for recognition of state ownership of the objects defined in subparagraph 1.1 of this paragraph (with the exception of residential premises, outbuildings, intended to satisfy citizens' household and other needs), after three months from the date of their registration as ownerless real estate by the body carrying out state registration of rights to real estate.
According to clause 1.2. Decree of the Acting Head of the Donetsk People's Republic dated 02/27/2023 No. 64 “On some issues related to the management of property that has signs of ownerlessness, located on the territory of the Donetsk People's Republic, and the recognition of such property as ownerless” The Government of the Donetsk People's Republic has the authority to establish (adopt) the procedure for recognizing ownerless property specified in subclause 1.1 of this clause, with the period for application of this procedure until January 1, 2028.
As part of the implementation of the above Decrees, by Decree of the Government of the Donetsk People's Republic dated June 22, 2023 No. 45-5, the executive body of the Donetsk People's Republic authorized to manage the state property of the Donetsk People's Republic established the State Property Fund of the Donetsk People's Republic and approved the Procedure for recognizing real estate located on the territory of the Donetsk People's Republic as ownerless, with the exception of residential buildings, residential premises, garden houses and outbuildings intended to satisfy citizens' household and other needs (hereinafter in the text of the decision - the Procedure), which regulates legal relations and establishes the procedure for recognizing property on the territory of the Donetsk People's Republic as ownerless, as well as recognizing the right of state ownership of the Donetsk People's Republic to ownerless real estate.
In addition, in pursuance of the above norm, Resolution of the Government of the Russian Federation dated December 28, 2022 No. 2474 was adopted “On approval of the Rules for the management of bodies of the Donetsk People's Republic, Lugansk People's Republic, Zaporozhye region and Kherson region with signs of ownerlessness and not classified as federal property, property of a constituent entity of the Russian Federation or municipal property located on the territory of these constituent entities of the Russian Federation.”
In accordance with paragraph 2.12 of the Decree of the Head of the Donetsk People's Republic of April 28, 2021 No. 116 “On the identification, registration and acceptance into municipal ownership of ownerless real estate and escheat property”, in order to register a real estate object as ownerless, the administration issues a corresponding order and applies to the territorial body of the federal executive body that carries out state cadastral registration, state registration of rights, maintenance of the Unified State Register of Real Estate, with an application for registration of an immovable property as ownerless and other documents provided for by current legislation.
According to paragraph 4 of the Procedure for registering ownerless real estate, established by Order No. P/0086 of the Federal Service for State Registration, Cadastre and Cartography dated March 15, 2023, registration of ownerless real estate is carried out in the following order:
acceptance of an application for registration of an ownerless real estate object, for taking the real estate object back into possession, use and disposal (hereinafter referred to as the application) and the documents provided for in paragraph 10 of the Procedure for registering ownerless real estate;
checking documents for compliance with the requirements of Federal Law N 218-FZ for documents received by the rights registration authority;
making entries in the Unified State Register of Real Estate on the registration of ownerless real estate.
Thus, by registering an ownerless real estate property, the Federal Service for State Registration, Cadastre and Cartography of the Russian Federation confirms the fact that there is no state registration of ownership and derivative real rights to the specified real estate in the State Register of Rights.
According to the Extract from the Unified State Register of Real Estate on the main characteristics and registered rights to the property dated November 14, 2024, the property located at:
<address>
, registered as an ownerless property.
The above Extract from the Unified State Register confirms the fact that there is no information about the registered ownership of residential premises in the Unified State Register of Real Estate and the technical characteristics of a property that has signs of being ownerless, namely the area of the property.
Measures in accordance with clauses 2.9.1, 2.9.2, 2.9.3 of the standard procedure for identifying, recording and accepting into municipal ownership ownerless real estate and escheat property, approved by Decree of the Head of the Donetsk People's Republic of April 28, 2021 No. 116 by the administration of the city of Mariupol, have been completed.
Clause 3.1 of the standard procedure for identifying, recording and accepting into municipal ownership ownerless real estate and escheat property, approved by Decree of the Head of the Donetsk People's Republic No. 116 dated April 28, 2021 “On identifying, recording and accepting into municipal ownership ownerless real estate and escheat property”, it is established that the identification, accounting and acceptance into municipal ownership of ownerless real estate and escheat property on the administrative territory of the municipality of the city of Mariupol, after three months from the date of registration of the premises as ownerless by the territorial body of the federal executive body carrying out state cadastral registration, state registration of rights, maintenance of the Unified State Register of Real Estate, the authorized body applies to the court with an application for recognition of the right of municipal ownership of the municipality to this residential premises in the manner prescribed by the legislation of the Russian Federation.
From the above it follows that the Administration of the Mariupol City District has followed the procedure prescribed by the current regulatory legal acts, namely: publication of an object that has signs of ownerless property, sending requests to confirm information about an identified premises that has signs of ownerless property, drawing up an inspection report of an ownerless property, drawing up an act on the impossibility of identifying the owner of a premises that has signs of ownerless property, registration of the property on the cadastral register real estate as ownerless.
Within three months from the moment the object was registered as ownerless, no one claimed their rights to this object.
Having assessed the evidence presented, the court recognizes that the immovable property located at:
<address>
is ownerless, and there is every reason for the Administration of the Mariupol Urban District to recognize the right of municipal ownership of the specified real estate.
Guided by Art. Art. 194-199 Code of Civil Procedure of the Russian Federation, court

the application of the Mariupol City District Administration is to be satisfied.
