Taking into account the above, guided by Article 167 of the Civil Procedure Code of the Russian Federation, due to the fact that the persons participating in the case were properly notified, the court, in accordance with the requirements of Art. 154 of the Civil Procedure Code of the Russian Federation considers it possible to consider a civil case in their absence.
Having studied the case materials, the court found the following.
In accordance with the provisions of Art. 4 of the Code of Civil Procedure of the Russian Federation, the court initiates a civil case upon the application of a person who has applied for protection of his rights, freedoms and legitimate interests.
At the same time, the right to judicial protection, as formulated in Art. 46 of the Constitution of the Russian Federation, does not indicate the possibility for a citizen to choose at his own discretion one or another method and procedure of judicial protection, the specifics of which in relation to certain categories of cases are determined by federal laws, which include the Civil Procedure Code of the Russian Federation.
According to Part 3 of Art. 263 of the Code of Civil Procedure of the Russian Federation, if, when filing an application or considering a case in a special proceeding, it is established that there is a dispute about the right within the jurisdiction of the court, the court issues a ruling to leave the application without consideration, in which it explains to the applicant and other interested parties their right to resolve the dispute in the manner of claim proceedings.
Within the meaning of these norms, a legal fact is established in special proceedings (Part 1 of Article 262 of the Code of Civil Procedure of the Russian Federation), when there is no need to resolve a dispute about the subjective right itself, the existence of which depends on the presence or absence of this fact.
