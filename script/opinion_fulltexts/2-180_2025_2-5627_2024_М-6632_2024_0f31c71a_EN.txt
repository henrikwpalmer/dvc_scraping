Case No. 2-180/2025
DEFINITION
January 30, 2025 Yenakievo
Yenakievo Interdistrict Court composed of: presiding judge Bug D.V., with the secretary of the court session Sysoeva E.E.,
having considered in an open preliminary court session in the courtroom a civil case at the request of the Administration of the Yenakievo Urban District of the Donetsk People's Republic, the interested party, the Office of the Federal Service for State Registration, Cadastre and Cartography of the Donetsk People's Republic, on recognition of the right of municipal ownership of an ownerless immovable thing,
INSTALLED:
On December 17, 2024, the Administration of the Yenakievo Urban District of the Donetsk People's Republic filed a petition with the court to recognize the right of municipal ownership of an ownerless real estate located at the address:
<address>
.
On January 24, 2025, the Yenakievo Interdistrict Court received a statement from the representative of the applicant of the Administration of the Yenakievo City District of the Donetsk People's Republic, A.V. Deryugina, in which the latter indicated that she was asking to terminate the civil proceedings on the application of the Administration of the Yenakievo City District of the Donetsk People's Republic to recognize the right of municipal ownership of an ownerless immovable property, since the owner of the immovable property had been identified, and therefore she refuses the stated demands. Consequences of refusal of a claim, provided for in Art. 221 of the Code of Civil Procedure of the Russian Federation is clear to her.
The applicant's representative did not appear at the court hearing; he provided a statement in which he indicated that he asked to consider the case without his participation.
At the court hearing, the representative of the interested party of the Office of the Federal Service for State Registration, Cadastre and Cartography of the Donetsk People's Republic did not appear, was duly notified of the date, time and place of the hearing of the case, and did not inform the court about the reasons for the failure to appear.
According to Part 3 of Art. 167 of the Code of Civil Procedure of the Russian Federation, the court has the right to consider the case in the absence of the interested person, a representative of the Office of the Federal Service for State Registration, Cadastre and Cartography of the Donetsk People's Republic, notified of the time and place of the court hearing, since the latter did not inform the court about valid reasons for failure to appear and did not ask to consider the case in his absence.
Having examined the case materials within the framework of the resolved issue of accepting the applicant’s refusal of the stated demands, the court came to the following conclusions.
According to Part 1 of Art. 263 of the Code of Civil Procedure of the Russian Federation, cases of special proceedings are considered and resolved by the court according to the general rules of claim proceedings with the features established by this chapter and Chapters 28 - 38 of this Code.
According to Part 4 of Art. 152 of the Code of Civil Procedure of the Russian Federation, in the presence of circumstances provided for in Articles 215, 216, 220, paragraphs two through six of Article 222 of this Code, the proceedings in the case at the preliminary court hearing may be suspended or terminated, the application left without consideration.
According to Art. 220 of the Code of Civil Procedure of the Russian Federation, the court terminates the proceedings if: the plaintiff abandoned the claim and the refusal was accepted by the court.
In accordance with the provisions of Part 3 of Article 173 of the Code of Civil Procedure of the Russian Federation, if the plaintiff refuses the claim and accepts it by the court or approves a settlement agreement between the parties, the court issues a ruling, which simultaneously terminates the proceedings.
In accordance with the provisions of Part 2 of Article 39 of the Code of Civil Procedure of the Russian Federation, the court does not accept the plaintiff’s refusal of the claim, the recognition of the claim by the defendant and does not approve a settlement agreement between the parties if this is contrary to the law or violates the rights and legitimate interests of other persons.
The court concludes that the applicant’s refusal of the stated demands does not contradict the law and does not violate the rights and legitimate interests of other persons. The consequences of refusal of the stated requirements are explained to the applicant.
Thus, the court considers it possible to accept the applicant’s refusal of the stated demands and terminate the proceedings.
Guided by Art. Art. 39, 152, 173, 220, 221, 224, 225, 332, 263 Code of Civil Procedure of the Russian Federation, court,
DEFINED:
Accept the refusal of the Administration of the Yenakievo Urban District of the Donetsk People's Republic from the stated requirements for recognition of the right of municipal ownership of an ownerless real estate.
Proceedings in a civil case at the request of the Administration of the Yenakievo Urban District of the Donetsk People's Republic, the interested party, the Office of the Federal Service for State Registration, Cadastre and Cartography of the Donetsk People's Republic, to recognize the right of municipal ownership of an ownerless immovable property shall be terminated.
Explain to the applicant’s representative of the Administration of the Yenakievo Urban District of the Donetsk People’s Republic that repeated appeals to the court between the same parties, about the same subject and on the same grounds are not allowed.
A private complaint against the ruling may be filed with the Supreme Court of the Donetsk People's Republic through the Yenakievo Interdistrict Court within fifteen days from the date of the ruling.
