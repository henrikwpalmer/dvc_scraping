INSTALLED:
City administration Mariupol DPR filed a petition with the court to the Office of Rosreestr for the DPR to recognize the right of municipal ownership of an ownerless real estate property. In support of the stated requirements, she indicated that as a result of the work carried out in accordance with the procedure established by law to identify ownerless property, an object of real estate was identified that had signs of ownerlessness. She indicated that the specified object was included in the corresponding register of ownerless real estate and it was registered in the cadastral register as ownerless property. She indicated that the three-month period for the above-mentioned object to be registered as ownerless has now expired, and therefore there are grounds provided for by law for its conversion into the ownership of the municipality. Asks the court to recognize the administration of the city. Mariupol DPR the right of municipal ownership of an ownerless real estate object - an apartment located at the address:
<address>

Taking into account the fact that the parties were properly notified in the case and the lack of information about the valid reasons for their failure to appear, the court considers it possible to consider the case on the merits in the absence of the parties.
