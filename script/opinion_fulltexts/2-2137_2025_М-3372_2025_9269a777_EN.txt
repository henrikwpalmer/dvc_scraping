The court, having listened to the explanations of the applicant's representative, examined all the evidence in the case and assessed it in its entirety, comes to the conclusion that it cannot be considered as a special proceeding, for the following reasons.
Special proceedings are a type of non-action civil proceedings in which civil cases are considered to confirm the presence or absence of legal facts that are important for protecting the rights and interests of a person or creating conditions for the exercise of personal non-property or property rights or confirming the presence or absence of indisputable rights.
The norms of Article 264 of the Code of Civil Procedure of the Russian Federation provide for the consideration of cases in a special proceeding to establish facts on which the emergence, change, termination of personal non-property or property rights of individuals and legal entities depends.
According to Part 3 of Article 263 of the Code of Civil Procedure of the Russian Federation, if, when filing an application or considering a case in a special proceeding, the existence of a dispute about the right is established, the court issues a ruling to leave the application without consideration, in which it explains to the applicant and other interested parties their right to resolve the dispute in the manner of litigation.
