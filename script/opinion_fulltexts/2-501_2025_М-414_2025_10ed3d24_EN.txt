Having studied the materials of the civil case, the court comes to the conclusion that the stated requirements have been satisfied in full, which is based on the following.
According to an extract from the Unified State Register of Real Estate about the property, provided at the request of the court by the Branch of PPK "Roskadastr" in the Donetsk People's Republic, this property was registered as an ownerless property.
Part 2 of Article 290 of the Code of Civil Procedure of the Russian Federation stipulates that an application for recognition of ownership of an ownerless immovable property is submitted to the court at its location by the body authorized to manage municipal property.
According to Article 4 of the Federal Constitutional Law of the Russian Federation dated

Regulatory legal acts of the Donetsk People's Republic are valid on the territory of the Donetsk People's Republic until the end of the transition period or until the adoption of the corresponding regulatory legal act of the Russian Federation and (or) the regulatory legal act of the Donetsk People's Republic.

According to Art. 2 of the Law of the DPR “On the specifics of identifying, using and recognizing the right of municipal property of municipal entities of the DPR to residential premises with signs of ownerless property located on the territory of the DPR” (hereinafter referred to as the Law), adopted by the Resolution of the People's Council
DD.MM.YYYY

According to Art. 3 of the Law, local government bodies of the Donetsk People's Republic, represented by the bodies authorized by them (hereinafter referred to as the authorized bodies), independently carry out actions to identify, record and accept residential premises that have signs of ownerless property, and the municipal property of the municipalities of the Donetsk People's Republic, on the territory of which they are located, on the grounds provided by law.
