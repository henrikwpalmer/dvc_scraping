The court, having examined the written evidence in the case, analyzing them both individually and in aggregate, evaluating the presented evidence according to its internal conviction, based on a comprehensive study of the evidence, comes to the following conclusions.
According to the first and third parts of Article 1 of the Federal Constitutional Law of the Russian Federation dated October 4, 2022 No. 5-FKZ “On the admission of the Donetsk People's Republic into the Russian Federation and the formation of a new subject within the Russian Federation - the Donetsk People's Republic (hereinafter referred to as the Federal Constitutional Law of October 4, 2022 No. 5-FKZ), it is determined that the Donetsk People's Republic is admitted to the Russian Federation in accordance with The Constitution of the Russian Federation and Article 4 of the Federal Constitutional Law of December 17, 2001 No. 6-FKZ “On the procedure for admission to the Russian Federation and the formation of a new subject of the Russian Federation within it.” The Donetsk People's Republic is considered accepted into the Russian Federation from the date of signing the Agreement between the Russian Federation and the Donetsk People's Republic on the admission of the Donetsk People's Republic to the Russian Federation and the formation of a new entity within the Russian Federation.
Article one of the Federal Law of the Russian Federation "On the ratification of the Treaty between the Russian Federation and the Donetsk People's Republic and the admission of the Donetsk People's Republic into the Russian Federation and the formation of a new subject within the Russian Federation" dated October 4, 2022 No. 372-FZ, provides that the Treaty between the Russian Federation and the Donetsk People's Republic on the admission of Donetsk to the Russian Federation has been ratified People's Republic and the formation of a new entity within the Russian Federation, signed on September 30, 2022, October 4, 2022. And article two of the named law shows that Federal Law No. 372-FZ of October 4, 2022 comes into force from the moment of official publication. Federal Law No. 372-FZ of October 4, 2022, published on October 5, 2022.
Part seven of Article 12 of the Federal Law No. 5-FKZ dated October 4, 2022, establishes that the management of property located on the territory of the Donetsk People's Republic, which has signs of ownerlessness and is not classified as federal property, the property of a constituent entity of the Russian Federation or municipal property, is carried out by the authorities of the Donetsk People's Republic in the manner established by the Government of the Russian Federation, unless otherwise provided by the Federal Constitutional Law of October 4 2022 No. 5-FKZ. The procedure for recognizing such property as ownerless, established by the regulatory legal acts of the Donetsk People's Republic, is applied until January 1, 2028, in agreement with the federal executive body exercising the functions of managing state property.
Paragraph one of 225 of the Civil Code of the Russian Federation defines that an ownerless thing is a thing that does not have an owner or the owner of which is unknown or, unless otherwise provided by law, the right of ownership to which the owner has refused.
Paragraph 3 of Article 218 of the Civil Code of the Russian Federation states that in cases and in the manner provided for by the Civil Code of the Russian Federation, a person can acquire ownership of property that does not have an owner, property whose owner is unknown, or property that the owner has refused or to which he has lost ownership on other grounds provided by law.
Part 2 of Article 290 of the Code of Civil Procedure of the Russian Federation states that an application for recognition of ownership of an ownerless immovable property is submitted to the court at its location by the body authorized to manage municipal property or property owned by the federal city of Moscow, St. Petersburg or Sevastopol, or by a person obliged in accordance with the law to operate linear facilities.
Part 2.1. Article 17 of the Federal Law of October 6, 2003 No. 131-FZ “On the General Principles of the Organization of Local Self-Government in the Russian Federation” stipulates that the powers of local self-government bodies in the areas of municipal property management are not allowed to be attributed to the powers of state authorities of a subject of the Russian Federation.
Clause 5 of Part 4 of Article 32 of the Law of the Donetsk People's Republic "On Local Self-Government in the Donetsk People's Republic" dated August 17, 2023 No. 468-IINS, states that the exclusive competence of the representative body of the municipality includes, in particular, determining the procedure for managing and disposing of property in municipal ownership.
In view of the fact that, in accordance with the requirements stated by the administration of the city district of Donetsk of the Donetsk People's Republic, this application requests recognition of the right of municipal ownership of the administration of the city district of Donetsk of the Donetsk People's Republic to an ownerless immovable property that is not residential real estate, in connection with which the court comes to the conclusion that this application for recognition of the right of ownership was submitted by a body authorized by current legislation.
Clause 1 of the Procedure for identifying, recording and accepting ownerless real estate into the ownership of the municipality of the Donetsk city district of the Donetsk People's Republic, approved by the decision of the Donetsk City Council of the Donetsk People's Republic dated July 4, 2024 No. 1/32-4 (hereinafter referred to as the Procedure), it is determined that the Procedure determines the procedure for identifying, registering, storing and accepting ownerless property as municipal property real estate identified on the territory of the municipality of the Donetsk urban district of the Donetsk People's Republic.
Thus, paragraph 7 of the Procedure determines that the administration of the Donetsk urban district of the Donetsk People's Republic independently carries out actions to identify, record and accept ownerless real estate located on the territory of the Donetsk urban district, represented by the Department of Municipal Property of the Donetsk urban district administration, into the municipal property of the Donetsk urban district.
Information about real estate that has signs of being ownerless may be received by the Department of Municipal Property of the Donetsk City District Administration from legal entities and individuals, including public authorities, who have become aware of such property (clause 8 of the Procedure).
And paragraph 9 of the Procedure states that applicants send to the Department of Municipal Property of the Donetsk City District Administration applications in paper or electronic form about identified real estate that has signs of ownerlessness.
Paragraph 11 of the Procedure states that the information on identified real estate that has signs of being ownerless shall indicate all the data known to the applicant: the address of the object (if absent, its location), its name, other data characterizing the object and its condition, including the number of storeys of the object, area or natural measurements. Photographs, inspection reports and other documents confirming the circumstances stated in the application or appeal may be attached to the application or appeal.
In accordance with paragraph 14 of the Procedure, it is determined that the Department of Municipal Property of the Donetsk City District Administration, in connection with the information received in accordance with paragraph 12 of the Procedure: 1) sends requests to confirm information about identified real estate that has signs of ownerlessness and is subject to the Procedure: to the territorial body of the federal executive body that carries out state cadastral registration, state registration of rights, maintaining a Unified the state register of real estate, on the existence of registered rights to a real estate object that has signs of being ownerless, and their restrictions (encumbrances), as well as on the existence of registered rights to a land plot, if the real estate object is integrally connected with the land plot; to the territorial body of the federal executive body carrying out state registration of legal entities, about the availability of information about the legal entity in the Unified State Register of Legal Entities; to the relevant bodies for accounting of state and municipal property that maintain registers of federal property, state property of the Donetsk People's Republic and municipal property; to state and municipal enterprises that provide services to legal entities and individuals in the field of housing and communal services, to fulfill the burden of maintaining ownerless real estate; 2) within 10 calendar days from the date of discovery of a real estate object that has signs of ownerlessness, publishes it on the official website of the municipality I city district of Donetsk of the Donetsk People's Republic on the Internet, as well as in accessible places at the entrance to non-residential premises, an announcement about the identification of non-residential premises that have signs of ownerlessness, and about the need for a personal appearance at the Department of Municipal Property of the Administration of the Donetsk City District of the person (persons) who considers themselves to be its owner or has (have) rights to it, within 30 calendar days from the date of placement of such an announcement; 3) after 30 calendar days from the date of placement of the announcement, in the event of the absence of a person (persons) who considers themselves the owner of real estate, and after receiving responses from the authorities specified in subparagraph 1 of paragraph 14 of the Procedure, indicating that the real estate object, which has signs of being ownerless, does not have an owner: within 5 working days, a report is drawn up on the impossibility of identifying the owner of the real estate object, which has signs of ownerlessness;
