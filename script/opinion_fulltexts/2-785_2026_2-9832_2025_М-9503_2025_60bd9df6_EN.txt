Having studied the case materials, the court comes to the following conclusion.
In accordance with paragraph 1 of Art. 225 of the Civil Code of the Russian Federation, ownerless is a thing that does not have an owner or the owner of which is unknown or, unless otherwise provided by law, the owner of which has renounced the right of ownership.
According to paragraph 1, clause 3, art. 225 of the Civil Code of the Russian Federation, ownerless real estate is accepted for registration by the body that carries out state registration of rights to real estate, upon application from the local government body in whose territory they are located.
Paragraph 2, paragraph 3, art. 225 of the Civil Code of the Russian Federation provides that after a year from the date of registration of an ownerless immovable property, and in the case of registration of a linear object, after three months from the date of registration, the body authorized to manage municipal property may apply to the court with a demand for recognition of the right of municipal ownership of this thing.
In accordance with paragraph 5 of part 2 of article 14 of the Federal Law of
DD.MM.YYYY
N 218-FZ “On State Registration of Real Estate” (hereinafter referred to as the Law on Registration), the grounds for state registration of rights are, among other things, judicial acts that have entered into legal force. Rights to real estate established by a court decision are subject to state registration in accordance with this Federal Law, that is, when the applicant applies to the rights registration authority, with an application for state registration of the right and the submission of other documents necessary for its implementation (Article 18 of the Registration Law).
In accordance with part 2 of article 4, part 7 of article 12 of the Federal Constitutional Law of

Management of property located on the territory of the Donetsk People's Republic, which has signs of ownerlessness and is not classified as federal property, the property of a constituent entity of the Russian Federation or municipal property, is carried out by the authorities of the Donetsk People's Republic in the manner established by the Government of the Russian Federation, unless otherwise provided by 5-FKZ.
